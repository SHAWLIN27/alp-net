# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Acceleration is a fundamental concept in kinematics that describes how quickly an object's velocity changes over time. Unlike speed, acceleration is a vector quantity, meaning it has both magnitude and direction. This sub-topic explores the definition, calculation, and physical interpretation of acceleration, including uniform (constant) acceleration and non-uniform acceleration. Understanding acceleration is essential for analyzing motion in [[Displacement, Velocity and Acceleration]], and it forms the foundation for [[Equations of Motion (SUVAT)]] and [[Motion Graphs]]. Acceleration is closely related to [[Speed and Velocity]] and [[Terminal Velocity]], and it relies on a solid grasp of [[Scalars and Vectors]].

**中文:**
加速度是运动学中的一个基本概念，描述物体速度随时间变化的快慢。与速率不同，加速度是一个矢量量，既有大小也有方向。本子知识点探讨加速度的定义、计算和物理解释，包括匀加速度（恒定加速度）和非匀加速度。理解加速度对于分析[[Displacement, Velocity and Acceleration]]中的运动至关重要，并且是[[Equations of Motion (SUVAT)]]和[[Motion Graphs]]的基础。加速度与[[Speed and Velocity]]和[[Terminal Velocity]]密切相关，并依赖于对[[Scalars and Vectors]]的扎实掌握。

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) |

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
Acceleration is defined as the rate of change of velocity. Since velocity is a vector (see [[Scalars and Vectors]]), acceleration is also a vector. This means that acceleration can occur in three ways:
1. **Change in speed** (magnitude of velocity) — e.g., a car speeding up or slowing down.
2. **Change in direction** — e.g., a car moving at constant speed around a circular track (this is covered in [[Circular Motion]]).
3. **Both speed and direction change** — e.g., a projectile in flight.

**Step-by-step reasoning:**
- To calculate average acceleration: $a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{v - u}{t}$, where $u$ is initial velocity, $v$ is final velocity, and $t$ is time.
- The SI unit of acceleration is $\text{m s}^{-2}$ (metres per second squared).
- A positive acceleration means velocity is increasing in the positive direction; a negative acceleration (deceleration) means velocity is decreasing or increasing in the negative direction.

**Physical interpretation:**
- If an object has zero acceleration, its velocity is constant (both speed and direction).
- If acceleration is constant (uniform), the velocity changes linearly with time.
- In free fall near Earth's surface, all objects experience the same acceleration $g \approx 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$ downward, regardless of mass (ignoring air resistance). This leads to [[Terminal Velocity]] when air resistance balances weight.

**Common pitfalls:**
- **Confusing acceleration with velocity:** A high-speed object can have zero acceleration (e.g., a car cruising at constant speed).
- **Thinking deceleration is always negative:** Deceleration is acceleration opposite to the direction of motion; it can be positive if the velocity is negative.
- **Forgetting direction:** Acceleration is a vector; always consider the sign convention.
- **Assuming constant acceleration:** Many real-world motions involve non-uniform acceleration (e.g., a car starting from rest).

**中文:**
加速度定义为速度的变化率。由于速度是矢量（参见[[Scalars and Vectors]]），加速度也是矢量。这意味着加速度可以通过三种方式发生：
1. **速率变化**（速度的大小）——例如，汽车加速或减速。
2. **方向变化**——例如，汽车以恒定速率绕圆形轨道行驶（这在[[Circular Motion]]中讨论）。
3. **速率和方向都变化**——例如，飞行中的抛射体。

**逐步推理：**
- 计算平均加速度：$a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{v - u}{t}$，其中 $u$ 是初速度，$v$ 是末速度，$t$ 是时间。
- 加速度的SI单位是 $\text{m s}^{-2}$（米每二次方秒）。
- 正加速度表示速度在正方向上增加；负加速度（减速）表示速度减小或在负方向上增加。

**物理解释：**
- 如果物体的加速度为零，则其速度恒定（速率和方向都不变）。
- 如果加速度恒定（匀加速度），速度随时间线性变化。
- 在地球表面附近的自由落体中，所有物体都经历相同的向下加速度 $g \approx 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$，与质量无关（忽略空气阻力）。这导致了当空气阻力与重力平衡时的[[Terminal Velocity]]。

**常见陷阱：**
- **混淆加速度和速度：** 高速物体可以具有零加速度（例如，以恒定速度行驶的汽车）。
- **认为减速总是负的：** 减速是与运动方向相反的加速度；如果速度为负，减速可以是正的。
- **忘记方向：** 加速度是矢量；始终考虑符号约定。
- **假设恒定加速度：** 许多现实世界的运动涉及非匀加速度（例如，从静止开始的汽车）。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

The key formula for average acceleration:

$$ a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{v - u}{t} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $a$ | Acceleration | 加速度 | $\text{m s}^{-2}$ |
| $\Delta v$ | Change in velocity | 速度变化量 | $\text{m s}^{-1}$ |
| $\Delta t$ | Time interval | 时间间隔 | $\text{s}$ |
| $v$ | Final velocity | 末速度 | $\text{m s}^{-1}$ |
| $u$ | Initial velocity | 初速度 | $\text{m s}^{-1}$ |
| $t$ | Time taken | 所用时间 | $\text{s}$ |

**Derivation / 推导:**
Acceleration is defined as the rate of change of velocity. For uniform acceleration:
$$ a = \frac{\text{change in velocity}}{\text{time taken}} = \frac{v - u}{t} $$
This can be rearranged to give the first SUVAT equation: $v = u + at$ (see [[Equations of Motion (SUVAT)]]).

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- This formula gives **average acceleration** over the time interval $\Delta t$.
- For **instantaneous acceleration**, we take the limit as $\Delta t \to 0$: $a = \frac{dv}{dt}$.
- The formula assumes acceleration is constant (uniform) when used in SUVAT equations.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Example 1: Calculating Acceleration from Velocity Change

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A car accelerates uniformly from rest to a velocity of $25 \text{ m s}^{-1}$ in $10 \text{ s}$. Calculate:
(a) The acceleration of the car.
(b) The distance travelled during this time (using $s = \frac{1}{2}(u+v)t$).

**中文:**
一辆汽车从静止匀加速到 $25 \text{ m s}^{-1}$，用时 $10 \text{ s}$。计算：
(a) 汽车的加速度。
(b) 这段时间内行驶的距离（使用 $s = \frac{1}{2}(u+v)t$）。

### Solution / 解答

**Part (a):**
Given: $u = 0 \text{ m s}^{-1}$, $v = 25 \text{ m s}^{-1}$, $t = 10 \text{ s}$.

Using $a = \frac{v - u}{t}$:
$$ a = \frac{25 - 0}{10} = 2.5 \text{ m s}^{-2} $$

**Part (b):**
Using $s = \frac{1}{2}(u+v)t$:
$$ s = \frac{1}{2}(0 + 25) \times 10 = \frac{1}{2} \times 25 \times 10 = 125 \text{ m} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) $2.5 \text{ m s}^{-2}$ (b) $125 \text{ m}$
**答案:** (a) $2.5 \text{ m s}^{-2}$ (b) $125 \text{ m}$

### Quick Tip / 提示
Always check units: acceleration in $\text{m s}^{-2}$, velocity in $\text{m s}^{-1}$, time in $\text{s}$. For uniform acceleration, the average velocity is $\frac{u+v}{2}$.

### Example 2: Deceleration (Negative Acceleration)

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A cyclist travelling at $8 \text{ m s}^{-1}$ applies brakes and comes to a stop in $4 \text{ s}$. Calculate the deceleration of the cyclist.

**中文:**
一名骑自行车的人以 $8 \text{ m s}^{-1}$ 的速度行驶，刹车后在 $4 \text{ s}$ 内停下。计算骑自行车的人的减速度。

### Solution / 解答

Given: $u = 8 \text{ m s}^{-1}$, $v = 0 \text{ m s}^{-1}$, $t = 4 \text{ s}$.

Using $a = \frac{v - u}{t}$:
$$ a = \frac{0 - 8}{4} = -2 \text{ m s}^{-2} $$

The negative sign indicates deceleration (acceleration opposite to the direction of motion).

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $-2 \text{ m s}^{-2}$ (deceleration of $2 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)
**答案:** $-2 \text{ m s}^{-2}$（减速度为 $2 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Quick Tip / 提示
Deceleration is often reported as a positive value (e.g., "a deceleration of $2 \text{ m s}^{-2}$"), but in calculations, use the negative sign to maintain vector consistency.
