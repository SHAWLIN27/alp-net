# Velocity-Time Graphs / 速度-时间图

---

# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Velocity-time graphs are a fundamental tool in kinematics, providing a visual representation of how an object's velocity changes over time. This sub-topic focuses on interpreting the shape, gradient, and area of a velocity-time graph to extract information about an object's motion. The gradient of the graph represents the object's acceleration, while the area under the graph represents the displacement. Understanding these relationships is crucial for solving problems involving motion, linking directly to the [[Equations of Motion (SUVAT)]] and building upon the concepts of [[Displacement, Velocity and Acceleration]]. This sub-topic is a core component of the [[Motion Graphs]] hub, alongside [[Displacement-Time Graphs]] and [[Acceleration-Time Graphs]].

**中文:**
速度-时间图是运动学中的基本工具，它直观地展示了物体速度随时间的变化情况。本子知识点侧重于解读速度-时间图的形状、斜率和面积，以提取物体的运动信息。图的斜率代表物体的加速度，而图下的面积代表位移。理解这些关系对于解决涉及运动的问题至关重要，它直接联系到[[运动学方程 (SUVAT)]]，并建立在[[位移、速度和加速度]]的概念之上。本子知识点是[[运动学图像]]知识图谱的核心组成部分，与[[位移-时间图]]和[[加速度-时间图]]并列。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Gradient Represents Acceleration / 斜率代表加速度

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** The gradient (or slope) of a velocity-time graph at any point is equal to the instantaneous acceleration of the object. For a straight line, the gradient is constant, indicating [[constant acceleration]]. The gradient is calculated as $a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{v_f - v_i}{t_f - t_i}$. A positive gradient means the velocity is increasing (acceleration), a negative gradient means the velocity is decreasing (deceleration or acceleration in the opposite direction), and a zero gradient means the velocity is constant (zero acceleration).

**中文:** 速度-时间图上任意一点的斜率等于物体的瞬时加速度。对于直线，斜率是恒定的，表示[[匀加速度]]。斜率的计算公式为 $a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{v_f - v_i}{t_f - t_i}$。正斜率表示速度增加（加速），负斜率表示速度减小（减速或反向加速），零斜率表示速度恒定（加速度为零）。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The gradient tells you how quickly the object's velocity is changing. A steep gradient means a large acceleration (rapid change in velocity), while a shallow gradient means a small acceleration (slow change in velocity).

**中文:** 斜率告诉你物体速度变化的快慢。陡峭的斜率意味着大的加速度（速度变化快），而平缓的斜率意味着小的加速度（速度变化慢）。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** A negative gradient always means the object is slowing down. (Correction: If the velocity is also negative, a negative gradient means the object is speeding up in the negative direction.)
- **中文:** 负斜率总是意味着物体在减速。（纠正：如果速度也是负的，负斜率意味着物体在负方向上加速。）
- **English:** A curved line means the object is not accelerating. (Correction: A curved line means the acceleration is changing, i.e., non-uniform acceleration.)
- **中文:** 曲线意味着物体没有加速度。（纠正：曲线意味着加速度在变化，即非匀变速运动。）

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always draw a large triangle on the graph to calculate the gradient. Use points on the line, not data points, for a line of best fit.
- **中文:** 始终在图上画一个大的三角形来计算斜率。对于拟合线，使用线上的点，而不是数据点。

## 4.2 Area Under Graph Represents Displacement / 图下面积代表位移

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** The area enclosed between the velocity-time graph line and the time axis represents the displacement of the object. For motion with constant acceleration, the graph is a straight line, and the area can be calculated by splitting it into simple geometric shapes like rectangles and triangles. The total displacement is the sum of these areas, taking into account the sign: area above the time axis is positive displacement, and area below the time axis is negative displacement.

**中文:** 速度-时间图线与时间轴之间所围成的面积代表物体的位移。对于匀加速运动，图线是直线，可以通过将其分割成矩形和三角形等简单几何形状来计算面积。总位移是这些面积的总和，需要考虑正负号：时间轴上方的面积为正位移，时间轴下方的面积为负位移。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The area under the graph is a measure of how far the object has traveled from its starting point, considering direction. If the object returns to its starting point, the net area (and thus net displacement) is zero.

**中文:** 图下的面积是衡量物体从其起点出发走了多远（考虑方向）的量度。如果物体返回起点，则净面积（以及净位移）为零。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** The area under the graph represents distance. (Correction: It represents displacement. To find total distance, you must add the absolute values of the areas.)
- **中文:** 图下的面积代表路程。（纠正：它代表位移。要找到总路程，必须将面积的绝对值相加。）
- **English:** You can only find the area if the graph is a straight line. (Correction: For curved lines, you can estimate the area by counting squares or using integration.)
- **中文:** 只有当图是直线时才能求面积。（纠正：对于曲线，可以通过数方格或使用积分来估算面积。）

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always show your working by drawing lines to split the area into shapes. Label each shape and calculate its area separately.
- **中文:** 始终通过画线将面积分割成不同形状来展示你的计算过程。标记每个形状并分别计算其面积。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## Equation 1: Acceleration from Gradient / 从斜率求加速度

$$ a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{v_f - v_i}{t_f - t_i} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $a$ | Acceleration | 加速度 | m s⁻² |
| $\Delta v$ | Change in velocity | 速度变化量 | m s⁻¹ |
| $\Delta t$ | Change in time | 时间变化量 | s |
| $v_f$ | Final velocity | 末速度 | m s⁻¹ |
| $v_i$ | Initial velocity | 初速度 | m s⁻¹ |

**Derivation / 推导:** This is the definition of acceleration.
**Conditions / 适用条件:** This equation gives the average acceleration over the time interval. For instantaneous acceleration, the time interval must approach zero.
**Limitations / 局限性:** It assumes a constant acceleration over the time interval if used with a straight line.

## Equation 2: Displacement from Area / 从面积求位移

$$ s = \text{Area under v-t graph} $$

For a trapezoid (constant acceleration from $u$ to $v$ over time $t$):

$$ s = \frac{1}{2}(u + v)t $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $s$ | Displacement | 位移 | m |
| $u$ | Initial velocity | 初速度 | m s⁻¹ |
| $v$ | Final velocity | 末速度 | m s⁻¹ |
| $t$ | Time | 时间 | s |

**Derivation / 推导:** The area of a trapezoid is average of parallel sides times height: $\frac{1}{2}(u+v) \times t$.
**Conditions / 适用条件:** This specific formula only applies for constant acceleration (straight line on v-t graph).
**Limitations / 局限性:** For non-constant acceleration, you must use integration or counting squares.

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 Constant Positive Acceleration / 匀正加速度

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Time / 时间 (t / s)
- **Y-axis:** Velocity / 速度 (v / m s⁻¹)

### Shape / 形状
A straight line with a positive gradient, starting from a positive initial velocity.

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
The gradient is constant and positive, representing a constant positive acceleration.

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
The area under the line (a trapezoid or triangle) represents the displacement.

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **English:** The object is moving in the positive direction and speeding up at a constant rate.
- **中文:** 物体沿正方向运动，并以恒定速率加速。

## 6.2 Constant Negative Acceleration (Deceleration) / 匀负加速度（减速）

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Time / 时间 (t / s)
- **Y-axis:** Velocity / 速度 (v / m s⁻¹)

### Shape / 形状
A straight line with a negative gradient, starting from a positive initial velocity and crossing the time axis.

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
The gradient is constant and negative, representing a constant deceleration (or acceleration in the negative direction).

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
The area above the axis (positive) minus the area below the axis (negative) gives the net displacement. The total distance is the sum of the absolute areas.

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **English:** The object is moving in the positive direction, slowing down, comes to rest, and then moves in the negative direction, speeding up.
- **中文:** 物体沿正方向运动，减速，停止，然后沿负方向运动并加速。

## 6.3 Non-Uniform Acceleration / 非匀变速运动

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Time / 时间 (t / s)
- **Y-axis:** Velocity / 速度 (v / m s⁻¹)

### Shape / 形状
A curved line.

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
The gradient changes along the curve. The instantaneous acceleration at a point is found by drawing a tangent to the curve at that point and calculating its gradient.

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
The area under the curve still represents displacement. It can be estimated by counting squares or using integration.

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **English:** The object's acceleration is changing over time. A steeper tangent means a larger acceleration.
- **中文:** 物体的加速度随时间变化。切线越陡，加速度越大。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating Acceleration and Displacement / 计算加速度和位移

### Question / 题目
**English:** A car accelerates uniformly from rest to a velocity of 20 m s⁻¹ in 10 seconds. It then travels at a constant velocity for 20 seconds before braking uniformly to a stop in 5 seconds.
(a) Sketch a velocity-time graph for the motion.
(b) Calculate the acceleration during the first 10 seconds.
(c) Calculate the total displacement of the car.

**中文:** 一辆汽车从静止开始匀加速，在10秒内达到20 m s⁻¹的速度。然后以恒定速度行驶20秒，最后在5秒内匀减速至停止。
(a) 画出该运动的速度-时间图草图。
(b) 计算前10秒内的加速度。
(c) 计算汽车的总位移。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Graph / 图:**
The graph has three sections:
1. A straight line from (0,0) to (10, 20) — acceleration.
2. A horizontal line from (10, 20) to (30, 20) — constant velocity.
3. A straight line from (30, 20) to (35, 0) — deceleration.

**(b) Acceleration / 加速度:**
$$ a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{20 - 0}{10 - 0} = \frac{20}{10} = 2 \text{ m s}^{-2} $$

**(c) Total Displacement / 总位移:**
The total displacement is the total area under the graph.

Area 1 (Triangle, acceleration phase):
$$ A_1 = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height} = \frac{1}{2} \times 10 \times 20 = 100 \text{ m} $$

Area 2 (Rectangle, constant velocity phase):
$$ A_2 = \text{length} \times \text{width} = 20 \times 20 = 400 \text{ m} $$

Area 3 (Triangle, deceleration phase):
$$ A_3 = \frac{1}{2} \times 5 \times 20 = 50 \text{ m} $$

Total Displacement:
$$ s = A_1 + A_2 + A_3 = 100 + 400 + 50 = 550 \text{ m} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) See sketch. (b) 2 m s⁻². (c) 550 m.
**答案：** (a) 见草图。 (b) 2 m s⁻²。 (c) 550 m。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always split the area into simple shapes (triangles, rectangles) to avoid errors.
- **中文:** 始终将面积分割成简单的形状（三角形、矩形）以避免错误。

## Example 2: Interpreting a Non-Linear Graph / 解读非线性图

### Question / 题目
**English:** The velocity-time graph of a cyclist is a curve. At time t = 5 s, the velocity is 8 m s⁻¹. A tangent drawn to the curve at t = 5 s passes through the points (3 s, 4 m s⁻¹) and (7 s, 12 m s⁻¹). Estimate the acceleration of the cyclist at t = 5 s.

**中文:** 一名骑自行车的人的速度-时间图是一条曲线。在时间 t = 5 s 时，速度为 8 m s⁻¹。在 t = 5 s 处画的切线经过点 (3 s, 4 m s⁻¹) 和 (7 s, 12 m s⁻¹)。估算骑自行车的人在 t = 5 s 时的加速度。

### Solution / 解答

The acceleration at t = 5 s is the gradient of the tangent at that point.

$$ a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t} = \frac{12 - 4}{7 - 3} = \frac{8}{4} = 2 \text{ m s}^{-2} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** 2 m s⁻².
**答案：** 2 m s⁻²。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** When drawing a tangent, make sure it only touches the curve at the point of interest. Use two points on the tangent that are far apart for a more accurate gradient calculation.
- **中文:** 画切线时，确保它只在感兴趣的点接触曲线。使用切线上相距较远的两个点来计算斜率，结果会更准确。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
Velocity-time graphs are often generated from experimental data, such as from a ticker-tape timer or a motion sensor connected to a data logger. Key practical skills include:
- **Data Collection:** Measuring displacement and time intervals accurately.
- **Graph Plotting:** Plotting velocity on the y-axis and time on the x-axis, with appropriate scales and units.
- **Drawing Tangents:** For non-linear graphs, accurately drawing a tangent to find the instantaneous acceleration.
- **Calculating Area:** Estimating the area under a curve by counting squares or using the trapezium rule.
- **Uncertainty Analysis:** The gradient and area calculations will have uncertainties based on the precision of the data and the graph plotting. The steeper the gradient, the larger the potential uncertainty in the acceleration value.

**中文:**
速度-时间图通常由实验数据生成，例如来自打点计时器或连接到数据记录器的运动传感器。关键的实验技能包括：
- **数据收集：** 准确测量位移和时间间隔。
- **绘图：** 以适当比例和单位，将速度标在纵轴上，时间标在横轴上。
- **画切线：** 对于非线性图，准确画切线以找到瞬时加速度。
- **计算面积：** 通过数方格或使用梯形法则估算曲线下的面积。
- **不确定度分析：** 斜率和面积的计算将根据数据和绘图的精度产生不确定度。斜率越陡，加速度值的潜在不确定度越大。
