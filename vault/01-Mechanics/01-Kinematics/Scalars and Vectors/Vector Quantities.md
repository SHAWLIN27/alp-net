# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Vector quantities are fundamental to physics because they describe physical phenomena that have both magnitude and direction. Unlike [[Scalar Quantities]], which are fully described by a single number and unit, vectors require additional directional information. This sub-topic introduces the concept of vectors, their notation, and how to represent them graphically. Understanding vectors is essential for mastering [[Vector Addition and Subtraction]], [[Resolution of Vectors]], and more advanced topics like [[Displacement, Velocity and Acceleration]] and [[Projectile Motion]].

In A-Level Physics, you will encounter vectors in mechanics (forces, velocity, acceleration), electricity (electric fields), and even quantum physics. The ability to distinguish between scalar and vector quantities is a core skill tested in both CAIE 9702 and Edexcel IAL examinations.

**中文:**
矢量（向量）量是物理学的基础，因为它们描述具有大小和方向的物理现象。与仅用单一数字和单位即可完整描述的[[标量]]不同，矢量需要额外的方向信息。本子知识点介绍矢量的概念、表示方法以及如何用图形表示矢量。理解矢量对于掌握[[矢量加减法]]、[[矢量分解]]以及更高级的主题如[[位移、速度和加速度]]和[[抛体运动]]至关重要。

在A-Level物理中，你会在力学（力、速度、加速度）、电学（电场）甚至量子物理中遇到矢量。区分标量和矢量量的能力是CAIE 9702和Edexcel IAL考试中测试的核心技能。

# 2. Core Definition / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) |
| ------------ | --------------- | --------------- |
| **Vector Quantity** / 矢量量 | A physical quantity that has both magnitude (size) and direction. | 既有大小又有方向的物理量。 |
| **Magnitude** / 大小 | The size or numerical value of a vector quantity, always positive. | 矢量量的大小或数值，始终为正。 |
| **Direction** / 方向 | The orientation of a vector in space, often measured relative to a reference direction (e.g., north, horizontal). | 矢量在空间中的指向，通常相对于参考方向（如北、水平方向）测量。 |
| **Resultant Vector** / 合矢量 | The single vector that represents the combined effect of two or more vectors. | 代表两个或多个矢量综合效果的单一矢量。 |
| **Free Vector** / 自由矢量 | A vector that can be moved parallel to itself without changing its effect. | 可以平行移动而不改变其效果的矢量。 |
| **Null Vector** / 零矢量 | A vector with zero magnitude and no specific direction. | 大小为零且无特定方向的矢量。 |

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
A vector quantity is defined by two essential components: **magnitude** and **direction**. The magnitude is always a positive number (or zero), while the direction is typically given as an angle relative to a reference line (e.g., 30° above the horizontal, or 45° east of north).

**Representation of Vectors:**
Vectors are represented graphically as **arrows**:
- The **length** of the arrow represents the magnitude (drawn to scale).
- The **arrowhead** indicates the direction.
- The **tail** is the starting point.

In written form, vectors are denoted in several ways:
- Boldface: **F** (common in textbooks)
- Arrow notation: $\vec{F}$
- Underline: $\underline{F}$
- In A-Level exams, you will often see $\vec{F}$ or simply $F$ with context indicating it's a vector.

**Examples of Vector Quantities in A-Level Physics:**
- [[Displacement, Velocity and Acceleration]]: displacement $\vec{s}$, velocity $\vec{v}$, acceleration $\vec{a}$
- [[Types of Force]]: force $\vec{F}$, weight $\vec{W}$, tension $\vec{T}$, friction $\vec{F}_f$
- Momentum $\vec{p}$
- Electric field strength $\vec{E}$
- Magnetic flux density $\vec{B}$

**Common Pitfall:**
Students often confuse **distance** (scalar) with **displacement** (vector). Distance is the total path length traveled, while displacement is the straight-line distance from start to finish with direction. For example, walking 3 m east then 4 m north gives a distance of 7 m but a displacement of 5 m at 53° north of east.

**中文:**
矢量量由两个基本要素定义：**大小**和**方向**。大小始终为正数（或零），而方向通常表示为相对于参考线的角度（例如，水平上方30°，或北偏东45°）。

**矢量的表示：**
矢量用**箭头**图形表示：
- 箭头的**长度**代表大小（按比例绘制）。
- **箭头**指示方向。
- **箭尾**是起点。

在书面形式中，矢量有多种表示方式：
- 粗体：**F**（常见于教科书）
- 箭头符号：$\vec{F}$
- 下划线：$\underline{F}$
- 在A-Level考试中，你常会看到$\vec{F}$或根据上下文表明是矢量的$F$。

**A-Level物理中的矢量量示例：**
- [[位移、速度和加速度]]：位移$\vec{s}$、速度$\vec{v}$、加速度$\vec{a}$
- [[力的类型]]：力$\vec{F}$、重量$\vec{W}$、张力$\vec{T}$、摩擦力$\vec{F}_f$
- 动量$\vec{p}$
- 电场强度$\vec{E}$
- 磁通密度$\vec{B}$

**常见错误：**
学生常混淆**距离**（标量）和**位移**（矢量）。距离是行进的总路径长度，而位移是从起点到终点的直线距离及方向。例如，向东走3米然后向北走4米，距离为7米，但位移为5米，方向为北偏东53°。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

There is no single formula for "vector quantities" as a concept, but the **magnitude** of a vector can be calculated using the Pythagorean theorem when components are known.

For a vector $\vec{v}$ with components $v_x$ and $v_y$:

$$ |\vec{v}| = \sqrt{v_x^2 + v_y^2} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $|\vec{v}|$ | Magnitude of vector $\vec{v}$ | 矢量$\vec{v}$的大小 | depends on quantity (m, m/s, N, etc.) |
| $v_x$ | Horizontal (x) component | 水平（x）分量 | same as vector |
| $v_y$ | Vertical (y) component | 垂直（y）分量 | same as vector |

**Direction / 方向:**
The direction angle $\theta$ (measured from the positive x-axis) is given by:

$$ \theta = \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{v_y}{v_x}\right) $$

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- This formula applies when the vector is resolved into perpendicular components.
- The components must be at right angles (90°) to each other.
- The angle $\theta$ must be interpreted carefully based on the quadrant of the components.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Question / 题目

**English:**
A student walks 3.0 m east, then 4.0 m north.
(a) Calculate the total distance traveled.
(b) Calculate the magnitude of the displacement.
(c) Determine the direction of the displacement relative to east.

**中文:**
一名学生向东走3.0米，然后向北走4.0米。
(a) 计算行走的总距离。
(b) 计算位移的大小。
(c) 确定位移相对于东的方向。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Distance / 距离:**
Distance is a scalar — just add the magnitudes:
$$ \text{Distance} = 3.0 + 4.0 = 7.0 \text{ m} $$

**(b) Displacement magnitude / 位移大小:**
Displacement is a vector. The path forms a right-angled triangle. Use Pythagoras:
$$ |\vec{s}| = \sqrt{(3.0)^2 + (4.0)^2} = \sqrt{9.0 + 16.0} = \sqrt{25.0} = 5.0 \text{ m} $$

**(c) Direction / 方向:**
Use trigonometry:
$$ \theta = \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}}\right) = \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{4.0}{3.0}\right) = \tan^{-1}(1.333) = 53.1^\circ $$
The direction is 53.1° north of east (or 53.1° above the eastward direction).

### Final Answer / 最终答案

**Answer:**
(a) 7.0 m
(b) 5.0 m
(c) 53.1° north of east

**答案:**
(a) 7.0米
(b) 5.0米
(c) 东偏北53.1°

### Quick Tip / 提示

**English:**
Always draw a vector diagram first! A simple sketch helps you visualize the problem and avoid sign errors. Remember: distance ≠ displacement — distance is the total path length (scalar), displacement is the straight-line distance with direction (vector).

**中文:**
始终先画矢量图！简单的草图有助于可视化问题并避免符号错误。记住：距离≠位移——距离是总路径长度（标量），位移是带方向的直线距离（矢量）。
