# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores the application of the [[Conservation of Momentum]] principle to **explosions** and **recoil** events. Unlike collisions where objects come together, in an explosion, a single stationary or moving object breaks apart into multiple fragments. The classic example is a gun firing a bullet (recoil) or a firework shell bursting in mid-air. The key insight is that the total momentum *before* the explosion is zero (or a known value), and the vector sum of the momenta of all fragments *after* the explosion must equal that same value. This is a direct consequence of [[Newton's Laws of Motion]], specifically Newton's Third Law (action-reaction pairs). Mastering this concept is crucial for understanding rocket propulsion, nuclear fission, and even the motion of stars in binary systems.

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨了**动量守恒定律**在**爆炸**和**反冲**事件中的应用。与碰撞不同（物体相互靠近），在爆炸中，一个原本静止或运动的物体分裂成多个碎片。典型的例子是枪发射子弹（反冲）或烟花弹在半空中爆炸。关键在于：爆炸*前*的总动量为零（或一个已知值），而爆炸*后*所有碎片动量的矢量和必须等于该值。这是[[牛顿运动定律]]的直接结果，特别是牛顿第三定律（作用力与反作用力对）。掌握这一概念对于理解火箭推进、核裂变，甚至双星系统的运动至关重要。

# 2. Core Definition / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) |
| ------------ | --------------- | --------------- |
| **Explosion** / 爆炸 | An event where a single object separates into two or more fragments due to internal forces. | 一个物体由于内部力作用而分裂成两个或多个碎片的事件。 |
| **Recoil** / 反冲 | The backward momentum of a gun or launcher when it fires a projectile forward. It is a specific type of explosion. | 枪械或发射器向前发射抛射物时产生的向后动量。它是一种特殊的爆炸类型。 |
| **Internal Forces** / 内力 | Forces that the particles of a system exert on each other. These forces cannot change the total momentum of the system. | 系统内粒子之间相互施加的力。这些力不能改变系统的总动量。 |
| **Isolated System** / 孤立系统 | A system on which no external resultant force acts. Total momentum is conserved within an isolated system. | 没有合外力作用的系统。在孤立系统内，总动量守恒。 |

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
The core principle is that **total momentum is conserved** in an explosion because the forces causing the separation are **internal forces**. There is no external net force acting on the system (assuming we ignore air resistance and friction for a brief moment).

1.  **The "Before" State:** Often, the object is initially at rest. Therefore, the total initial momentum is zero ($p_{initial} = 0$).
2.  **The "After" State:** The fragments fly apart. The vector sum of their individual momenta must equal the initial momentum.
3.  **Recoil as a Special Case:** When a gun fires a bullet, the gun (the "recoiling" object) moves backward. The bullet moves forward. The momentum of the bullet ($m_b v_b$) is equal in magnitude and opposite in direction to the momentum of the gun ($m_g v_g$). This gives us the classic recoil equation: $m_b v_b = - m_g v_g$.
4.  **Energy is NOT Conserved:** A common pitfall is assuming kinetic energy is conserved. In an explosion, chemical or elastic potential energy is converted into kinetic energy. The total kinetic energy *after* the explosion is always **greater** than the kinetic energy *before*. This is the opposite of an [[Inelastic Collisions|inelastic collision]].

**中文:**
核心原理是，在爆炸中**总动量守恒**，因为导致分离的力是**内力**。系统没有受到净外力（假设我们暂时忽略空气阻力和摩擦力）。

1.  **“之前”状态：** 物体通常最初是静止的。因此，总初始动量为零 ($p_{初始} = 0$)。
2.  **“之后”状态：** 碎片向各个方向飞散。它们各自动量的矢量和必须等于初始动量。
3.  **反冲作为一种特殊情况：** 当枪发射子弹时，枪（“反冲”的物体）向后运动。子弹向前运动。子弹的动量 ($m_b v_b$) 与枪的动量 ($m_g v_g$) 大小相等，方向相反。这给出了经典的反冲方程：$m_b v_b = - m_g v_g$。
4.  **能量不守恒：** 一个常见的误区是假设动能守恒。在爆炸中，化学能或弹性势能转化为动能。爆炸*后*的总动能总是**大于**爆炸*前*的动能。这与[[非弹性碰撞]]相反。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

The fundamental formula for an explosion from rest:

$$ 0 = m_1 v_1 + m_2 v_2 $$

For a recoil event (gun firing bullet):

$$ 0 = m_{gun} v_{gun} + m_{bullet} v_{bullet} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $m_1, m_2$ | Masses of fragments 1 and 2 | 碎片1和2的质量 | kg |
| $v_1, v_2$ | Velocities of fragments 1 and 2 | 碎片1和2的速度 | m/s |
| $m_{gun}$ | Mass of the gun | 枪的质量 | kg |
| $v_{gun}$ | Recoil velocity of the gun | 枪的反冲速度 | m/s |
| $m_{bullet}$ | Mass of the bullet | 子弹的质量 | kg |
| $v_{bullet}$ | Muzzle velocity of the bullet | 子弹的枪口速度 | m/s |

**Derivation / 推导:**
From the principle of conservation of momentum:
$$ p_{before} = p_{after} $$
If the system is initially at rest:
$$ 0 = p_{after} $$
$$ 0 = m_1 v_1 + m_2 v_2 $$
Therefore:
$$ m_1 v_1 = - m_2 v_2 $$

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- The system must be **isolated** (no external resultant force).
- The explosion must be instantaneous (forces are internal and act over a very short time).
- Velocities must be treated as **vectors** (direction matters).

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A stationary uranium-238 nucleus ($m = 3.95 \times 10^{-25} \text{ kg}$) undergoes radioactive decay, emitting an alpha particle ($m = 6.64 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg}$) at a speed of $1.5 \times 10^7 \text{ m/s}$. Calculate the recoil speed of the remaining daughter nucleus.

**中文:**
一个静止的铀-238原子核（$m = 3.95 \times 10^{-25} \text{ kg}$）发生放射性衰变，发射出一个α粒子（$m = 6.64 \times 10^{-27} \text{ kg}$），速度为 $1.5 \times 10^7 \text{ m/s}$。计算剩余子核的反冲速度。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1: Define the system and principle.**
The system is isolated. Total momentum is conserved. Initial momentum = 0.
**步骤1：定义系统和原理。** 系统是孤立的。总动量守恒。初始动量 = 0。

**Step 2: Write the conservation equation.**
$$ 0 = m_{\alpha} v_{\alpha} + m_{daughter} v_{daughter} $$
**步骤2：写出守恒方程。**

**Step 3: Find the mass of the daughter nucleus.**
$$ m_{daughter} = m_{Uranium} - m_{\alpha} = (3.95 \times 10^{-25}) - (6.64 \times 10^{-27}) = 3.88 \times 10^{-25} \text{ kg} $$
**步骤3：求子核的质量。**

**Step 4: Substitute and solve for $v_{daughter}$.**
$$ 0 = (6.64 \times 10^{-27})(1.5 \times 10^7) + (3.88 \times 10^{-25}) v_{daughter} $$
$$ (3.88 \times 10^{-25}) v_{daughter} = - (6.64 \times 10^{-27})(1.5 \times 10^7) $$
$$ v_{daughter} = - \frac{(6.64 \times 10^{-27})(1.5 \times 10^7)}{3.88 \times 10^{-25}} $$
$$ v_{daughter} = - \frac{9.96 \times 10^{-20}}{3.88 \times 10^{-25}} $$
$$ v_{daughter} = -2.57 \times 10^5 \text{ m/s} $$
**步骤4：代入并求解 $v_{daughter}$。**

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** The recoil speed of the daughter nucleus is $2.57 \times 10^5 \text{ m/s}$.
**答案:** 子核的反冲速度为 $2.57 \times 10^5 \text{ m/s}$。

### Quick Tip / 提示
**EN:** The negative sign in the answer indicates direction. The question asks for "speed", so you only give the magnitude. Always check if the question asks for velocity (vector) or speed (scalar).
**CN:** 答案中的负号表示方向。问题问的是“速度大小”，所以只给出数值。务必检查问题是要求速度（矢量）还是速率（标量）。

# 7. Flashcards / 闪卡

**Q (EN):** What is the key difference between an explosion and a collision regarding kinetic energy?
**Q (CN):** 在动能方面，爆炸和碰撞的关键区别是什么？
**A (EN):** In an explosion, kinetic energy increases (chemical/elastic PE → KE). In a collision, kinetic energy is either conserved (elastic) or decreases (inelastic).
**A (CN):** 在爆炸中，动能增加（化学能/弹性势能 → 动能）。在碰撞中，动能要么守恒（弹性碰撞），要么减少（非弹性碰撞）。

**Q (EN):** A stationary bomb explodes into two fragments. Fragment A has mass 2 kg and velocity 3 m/s East. What is the momentum of Fragment B?
**Q (CN):** 一个静止的炸弹爆炸成两个碎片。碎片A质量为2千克，速度为3米/秒向东。碎片B的动量是多少？
**A (EN):** 6 kg m/s West.
**A (CN):** 6 千克·米/秒 向西。

**Q (EN):** State the principle of conservation of momentum.
**Q (CN):** 陈述动量守恒定律。
**A (EN):** The total momentum of an isolated system remains constant.
**A (CN):** 孤立系统的总动量保持不变。

**Q (EN):** A gun of mass 5 kg fires a 0.01 kg bullet at 400 m/s. What is the recoil velocity of the gun?
**Q (CN):** 一支质量为5千克的枪发射一颗0.01千克的子弹，速度为400米/秒。枪的反冲速度是多少？
**A (EN):** -0.8 m/s (or 0.8 m/s in the opposite direction).
**A (CN):** -0.8 米/秒（或相反方向0.8米/秒）。

**Q (EN):** Why is momentum conserved in an explosion but kinetic energy is not?
**Q (CN):** 为什么在爆炸中动量守恒而动能不守恒？
**A (EN):** Momentum is conserved because the forces are internal (Newton's 3rd Law). Kinetic energy is not conserved because internal chemical/elastic potential energy is converted into kinetic energy.
**A (CN):** 动量守恒是因为力是内力（牛顿第三定律）。动能不守恒是因为内部的化学能/弹性势能转化为动能。
