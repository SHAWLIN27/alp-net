# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Newton’s Third Law is often the most misunderstood of the three laws. It states that forces always come in pairs: if object A exerts a force on object B, then object B exerts an equal and opposite force on object A. This sub-topic focuses on identifying **action-reaction pairs** correctly, distinguishing them from balanced forces, and applying the law to real-world scenarios like walking, rocket propulsion, and collisions. It is a cornerstone of [[Newton's Laws of Motion]] and directly connects to [[Linear Momentum and Impulse]] and [[Conservation of Momentum]].

**中文:**
牛顿第三定律通常是三大定律中最容易被误解的一个。它指出力总是成对出现的：如果物体 A 对物体 B 施加了一个力，那么物体 B 也会对物体 A 施加一个大小相等、方向相反的力。本子知识点侧重于正确识别 **作用力与反作用力对**，将其与平衡力区分开来，并将其应用于行走、火箭推进和碰撞等现实场景。它是 [[牛顿运动定律]] 的基石，并直接与 [[动量和冲量]] 及 [[动量守恒]] 相关联。

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
The most critical skill for this sub-topic is **identifying action-reaction pairs**. A common mistake is to think that the reaction to a weight force ($mg$) is the normal contact force ($N$). This is **wrong**.

*   **The correct pair for weight:** The Earth pulls the object down (weight). The reaction is the object pulling the Earth *up* with an equal force. Because the Earth’s mass is enormous, this force causes negligible acceleration of the Earth.
*   **The correct pair for normal force:** The table pushes the book *up* (normal force). The reaction is the book pushing the table *down*.

**Key difference from balanced forces:**
*   **Balanced forces** act on the *same* object and cancel out, resulting in zero net force (equilibrium).
*   **Action-reaction pairs** act on *different* objects and therefore do *not* cancel out. They cause acceleration in both objects.

This concept is essential for drawing accurate [[Free-body Diagrams]] and for understanding [[Applications of Newton's Laws]].

**中文:**
本子知识点最关键的是 **识别作用力与反作用力对**。一个常见的错误是认为重力的反作用力是法向接触力。这是 **错误** 的。

*   **重力的正确力对：** 地球向下拉物体（重力）。反作用力是物体以相等的力 *向上* 拉地球。由于地球质量巨大，这个力对地球产生的加速度可以忽略不计。
*   **法向力的正确力对：** 桌子 *向上* 推书（法向力）。反作用力是书 *向下* 推桌子。

**与平衡力的关键区别：**
*   **平衡力** 作用在 *同一个* 物体上，相互抵消，导致合力为零（平衡状态）。
*   **作用力与反作用力对** 作用在 *不同* 物体上，因此 *不会* 相互抵消。它们会导致两个物体都产生加速度。

这个概念对于绘制准确的 [[受力分析图]] 和理解 [[牛顿定律的应用]] 至关重要。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

The core formula is a statement of the law itself:

$$ \vec{F}_{AB} = -\vec{F}_{BA} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $\vec{F}_{AB}$ | Force exerted by object A on object B | 物体 A 对物体 B 施加的力 | N (Newtons) |
| $\vec{F}_{BA}$ | Force exerted by object B on object A | 物体 B 对物体 A 施加的力 | N (Newtons) |
| $-$ | Indicates opposite direction | 表示方向相反 | - |

**Derivation / 推导:**
This is a fundamental postulate of classical mechanics, not derived from other equations. It is based on experimental observation.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
*   The forces must be of the **same type** (e.g., both gravitational, both electromagnetic).
*   The forces act **simultaneously**.
*   The forces act on **different bodies**.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A 5.0 kg book is at rest on a table. Identify and describe the two action-reaction pairs acting on the book. Explain why the book does not move despite these forces.

**中文:**
一本 5.0 千克的书静止在桌子上。识别并描述作用在书上的两对作用力与反作用力。解释为什么尽管有这些力，书却不动。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1: Identify the first pair (Gravitational).**
The Earth exerts a gravitational force (weight) on the book, pulling it downwards.
$$ W = mg = 5.0 \times 9.81 = 49.05 \text{ N (downwards)} $$
The reaction to this is the book exerting an equal gravitational force on the Earth, pulling the Earth upwards.

**Step 2: Identify the second pair (Contact).**
The table exerts a normal contact force on the book, pushing it upwards. Since the book is at rest, this force must balance the weight.
$$ N = 49.05 \text{ N (upwards)} $$
The reaction to this is the book exerting an equal contact force on the table, pushing the table downwards.

**Step 3: Explain why the book doesn't move.**
The book does not move because the **two forces acting on the book** (Weight downwards and Normal force upwards) are **balanced forces** (equal and opposite, acting on the same object). The net force on the book is zero. The action-reaction pairs act on *different* objects and do not affect the motion of the book itself.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** The two pairs are (1) Earth-book gravitational interaction and (2) table-book contact interaction. The book is stationary because the forces *on it* (weight and normal) are balanced.
**答案:** 两对力是 (1) 地球-书本的引力相互作用和 (2) 桌子-书本的接触相互作用。书静止是因为作用在 *它上面* 的力（重力和法向力）是平衡的。

### Quick Tip / 提示
To check if you have an action-reaction pair, swap the two objects in the description. "Earth pulls book" becomes "Book pulls Earth". If the swapped description makes physical sense, it's a valid pair.
