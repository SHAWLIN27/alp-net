# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
The Normal Contact Force (often called the "normal reaction" or "normal force") is the perpendicular force exerted by a surface on an object in contact with it. This force is fundamental to understanding how objects interact with surfaces — it prevents objects from falling through tables, supports objects on slopes, and is essential for analyzing [[Free-body Diagrams]]. The term "normal" means perpendicular to the surface, not "ordinary" or "usual". This sub-topic is a foundational concept within [[Types of Force]] and is critical for solving problems involving equilibrium, motion on inclined planes, and circular motion.

**中文:**
法向接触力（常称为"法向反作用力"或"法向力"）是表面对其接触物体施加的垂直力。该力是理解物体如何与表面相互作用的基础——它防止物体穿过桌面、支撑斜面上的物体，并且对于分析[[Free-body Diagrams]]至关重要。"法向"一词意为垂直于表面，而非"通常的"或"平常的"。本子知识点是[[Types of Force]]中的基础概念，对于解决涉及平衡、斜面运动和圆周运动的问题至关重要。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The Nature of Normal Contact Force / 法向接触力的性质

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The normal contact force ($N$ or $R$) arises from the electromagnetic repulsion between atoms in the object and atoms in the surface. When an object sits on a surface, the atoms in both materials are compressed slightly, creating a repulsive force that pushes them apart. This force is always perpendicular to the surface at the point of contact. It is important to note that the normal force is NOT always equal to the weight of the object — it adjusts based on other forces acting on the object.

**中文:**
法向接触力（$N$ 或 $R$）源于物体原子与表面原子之间的电磁排斥。当物体放置在表面上时，两种材料中的原子都会轻微压缩，产生将它们推开的排斥力。该力始终垂直于接触点的表面。需要注意的是，法向力并不总是等于物体的重量——它会根据作用在物体上的其他力进行调整。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The normal force represents the surface's resistance to being penetrated. If you push harder on a surface, the atoms compress more, and the normal force increases. This is why the normal force can vary — it is a "self-adjusting" force that takes whatever value is needed to prevent the object from passing through the surface (up to the surface's breaking point).

**中文:**
法向力代表表面对被穿透的抵抗。如果你更用力地推表面，原子压缩更多，法向力就会增加。这就是为什么法向力可以变化——它是一种"自我调节"的力，会取任何必要的值来阻止物体穿过表面（直到表面的断裂点）。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- ❌ **"Normal force always equals weight"** — Only true for objects on a horizontal surface with no other vertical forces
- ❌ **"Normal force is a reaction to weight"** — It's a reaction to contact, not specifically to weight
- ❌ **"Normal force acts vertically upward"** — It acts perpendicular to the surface, which may not be vertical
- ❌ **"Normal force is constant"** — It changes with applied forces and acceleration

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ Always draw the normal force perpendicular to the surface in [[Free-body Diagrams]]
- ✅ Label it as $N$ or $R$ (both are accepted)
- ✅ Remember: $N = mg$ only for horizontal surfaces with no other vertical forces
- ✅ For inclined planes, resolve weight into components parallel and perpendicular to the plane

## 4.2 Normal Force on Horizontal Surfaces / 水平面上的法向力

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
For an object resting on a horizontal surface with no other vertical forces, the normal force equals the weight: $N = mg$. However, if additional vertical forces act (e.g., pushing down or lifting up), the normal force adjusts accordingly. For example, if you push down on a book with force $F$, the normal force becomes $N = mg + F$. If you lift up with force $F$, the normal force becomes $N = mg - F$ (until the object lifts off, at which point $N = 0$).

**中文:**
对于放置在水平表面上且没有其他垂直力的物体，法向力等于重量：$N = mg$。然而，如果有额外的垂直力作用（例如向下推或向上提），法向力会相应调整。例如，如果你用$F$的力向下推一本书，法向力变为$N = mg + F$。如果你用$F$的力向上提，法向力变为$N = mg - F$（直到物体离开表面，此时$N = 0$）。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The normal force is the surface's response to the total perpendicular force pressing the object against it. More compression → more normal force. When the upward force equals the weight, the object is just about to lift off, and $N = 0$.

**中文:**
法向力是表面对将物体压向它的总垂直力的响应。压缩越大→法向力越大。当向上的力等于重量时，物体即将离开表面，此时$N = 0$。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- ❌ Thinking $N = mg$ always applies
- ❌ Forgetting that pushing down increases $N$, lifting up decreases $N$

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ Apply Newton's Second Law vertically: $\sum F_y = ma_y$
- ✅ For equilibrium: $\sum F_y = 0$, so $N + F_{up} - mg - F_{down} = 0$
- ✅ When $N = 0$, the object loses contact with the surface

## 4.3 Normal Force on Inclined Planes / 斜面上的法向力

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
On an inclined plane at angle $\theta$ to the horizontal, the normal force is perpendicular to the plane's surface. The weight $mg$ must be resolved into two components:
- Perpendicular to plane: $mg\cos\theta$ (balanced by $N$)
- Parallel to plane: $mg\sin\theta$ (causing acceleration down the plane)

Since there is no acceleration perpendicular to the plane (the object stays on the surface), $N = mg\cos\theta$. Note that $N < mg$ on an inclined plane.

**中文:**
在与水平面成$\theta$角的斜面上，法向力垂直于斜面表面。重量$mg$必须分解为两个分量：
- 垂直于斜面：$mg\cos\theta$（与$N$平衡）
- 平行于斜面：$mg\sin\theta$（导致沿斜面加速）

由于垂直于斜面方向没有加速度（物体保持在表面上），$N = mg\cos\theta$。注意在斜面上$N < mg$。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The steeper the incline (larger $\theta$), the smaller the normal force. At $\theta = 90^\circ$ (vertical), $N = 0$ — the object falls freely. At $\theta = 0^\circ$ (horizontal), $N = mg$.

**中文:**
斜面越陡（$\theta$越大），法向力越小。当$\theta = 90^\circ$（垂直）时，$N = 0$——物体自由下落。当$\theta = 0^\circ$（水平）时，$N = mg$。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- ❌ Using $mg$ instead of $mg\cos\theta$ for normal force on an incline
- ❌ Drawing normal force vertically upward instead of perpendicular to the plane
- ❌ Confusing which component is $mg\cos\theta$ and which is $mg\sin\theta$

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ Always draw the normal force at 90° to the inclined surface
- ✅ Resolve weight, not the normal force
- ✅ Remember: $N = mg\cos\theta$ (not $mg\sin\theta$)
- ✅ The component $mg\sin\theta$ causes acceleration down the plane

## 4.4 Normal Force and Newton's Third Law / 法向力与牛顿第三定律

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The normal force is part of a Newton's Third Law pair, but careful: the pair is NOT "normal force and weight". The normal force on the object (from the surface) has its Newton's Third Law partner as the force the object exerts on the surface (equal in magnitude, opposite in direction). Weight is the gravitational force from the Earth on the object — its partner is the gravitational force the object exerts on the Earth.

**中文:**
法向力是牛顿第三定律作用力与反作用力对的一部分，但要注意：该对力不是"法向力和重量"。物体上的法向力（来自表面）的牛顿第三定律伙伴是物体对表面施加的力（大小相等，方向相反）。重量是地球对物体的引力——其伙伴是物体对地球施加的引力。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The book pushes down on the table (action), the table pushes up on the book (reaction). These two forces are equal in magnitude and opposite in direction, but they act on different objects. This is why they don't cancel out — the normal force acts on the book, while the book's force acts on the table.

**中文:**
书向下推桌子（作用力），桌子向上推书（反作用力）。这两个力大小相等、方向相反，但作用在不同物体上。这就是为什么它们不会抵消——法向力作用在书上，而书的力作用在桌子上。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- ❌ Thinking normal force and weight are a Newton's Third Law pair
- ❌ Confusing "reaction force" (normal force) with Newton's Third Law "reaction"

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ Normal force and weight act on the SAME object (the book)
- ✅ Newton's Third Law pairs act on DIFFERENT objects
- ✅ The pair for normal force is the force the object exerts on the surface

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## Equation 1: Normal Force on Horizontal Surface (No Other Vertical Forces)

$$ N = mg $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $N$ | Normal contact force | 法向接触力 | N (Newtons) |
| $m$ | Mass of object | 物体质量 | kg |
| $g$ | Acceleration due to gravity | 重力加速度 | m s⁻² |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** Object on horizontal surface, no other vertical forces, no vertical acceleration.
**Limitations / 局限性:** Does not apply if additional vertical forces act or if the surface is inclined.

---

## Equation 2: Normal Force on Horizontal Surface (With Additional Vertical Forces)

$$ N = mg + F_{down} - F_{up} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $F_{down}$ | Additional downward force | 额外向下的力 | N |
| $F_{up}$ | Additional upward force | 额外向上的力 | N |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** Object on horizontal surface, vertical forces only, no vertical acceleration.
**Limitations / 局限性:** $N \geq 0$; if $N < 0$, the object has lost contact.

---

## Equation 3: Normal Force on Inclined Plane

$$ N = mg\cos\theta $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $\theta$ | Angle of incline from horizontal | 斜面与水平面的夹角 | degrees or radians |

**Derivation / 推导:** Resolve weight $mg$ into components: perpendicular component = $mg\cos\theta$. Since no acceleration perpendicular to plane, $N = mg\cos\theta$.
**Conditions / 适用条件:** Object on frictionless inclined plane, no other forces perpendicular to plane.
**Limitations / 局限性:** Only for objects on a plane; does not include friction effects on normal force.

---

## Equation 4: General Normal Force from Newton's Second Law

$$ N = m(g\cos\theta \pm a_\perp) $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $a_\perp$ | Acceleration perpendicular to surface | 垂直于表面的加速度 | m s⁻² |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** General case with acceleration perpendicular to surface.
**Limitations / 局限性:** Sign depends on direction of acceleration relative to surface.

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 Normal Force vs. Angle of Incline / 法向力与斜面角度的关系

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Angle of incline $\theta$ (degrees) / 斜面角度 $\theta$（度）
- **Y-axis:** Normal force $N$ (N) / 法向力 $N$（牛）

### Shape / 形状
Cosine curve: $N = mg\cos\theta$, starting at $N = mg$ when $\theta = 0^\circ$, decreasing to $N = 0$ when $\theta = 90^\circ$.

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
The gradient $\frac{dN}{d\theta} = -mg\sin\theta$ represents the rate of change of normal force with angle. Steepest at $\theta = 0^\circ$ (gradient = 0), maximum negative gradient at $\theta = 90^\circ$.

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
Area under the curve has no direct physical meaning in this context.

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- At $\theta = 0^\circ$: $N = mg$ (horizontal surface)
- At $\theta = 30^\circ$: $N = mg\cos30^\circ = 0.866mg$
- At $\theta = 45^\circ$: $N = mg\cos45^\circ = 0.707mg$
- At $\theta = 60^\circ$: $N = mg\cos60^\circ = 0.5mg$
- At $\theta = 90^\circ$: $N = 0$ (vertical surface, free fall)

## 6.2 Normal Force vs. Applied Downward Force / 法向力与向下施加力的关系

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Applied downward force $F$ (N) / 向下施加的力 $F$（牛）
- **Y-axis:** Normal force $N$ (N) / 法向力 $N$（牛）

### Shape / 形状
Linear: $N = mg + F$, a straight line with gradient = 1, y-intercept = $mg$.

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
Gradient = 1: every 1 N of additional downward force increases normal force by 1 N.

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
No direct physical meaning.

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- When $F = 0$: $N = mg$
- When $F$ increases, $N$ increases proportionally
- The line continues indefinitely (until surface breaks)

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Normal Force on Horizontal Surface with Applied Force / 水平面上有施加力的法向力

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A box of mass 5.0 kg rests on a horizontal floor. A person pushes down on the box with a force of 20 N. Calculate the normal contact force exerted by the floor on the box. (Take $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
一个质量为5.0 kg的箱子放在水平地板上。一个人用20 N的力向下推箱子。计算地板对箱子的法向接触力。（取$g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify forces / 步骤1：识别力**
- Weight: $W = mg = 5.0 \times 9.81 = 49.05 \text{ N}$ (downward)
- Applied force: $F = 20 \text{ N}$ (downward)
- Normal force: $N$ (upward, unknown)

**Step 2: Apply Newton's Second Law vertically / 步骤2：竖直方向应用牛顿第二定律**
Since the box is not accelerating vertically: $\sum F_y = 0$

$$N - mg - F = 0$$

**Step 3: Solve for N / 步骤3：求解N**

$$N = mg + F = 49.05 + 20 = 69.05 \text{ N}$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $N = 69.1 \text{ N}$ (3 significant figures) | **答案：** $N = 69.1 \text{ N}$（3位有效数字）

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Pushing down increases the normal force. If the person were lifting up instead, the normal force would decrease: $N = mg - F$.

**中文:** 向下推会增加法向力。如果改为向上提，法向力会减小：$N = mg - F$。

---

## Example 2: Normal Force on an Inclined Plane / 斜面上的法向力

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A block of mass 2.0 kg slides down a frictionless inclined plane at an angle of 30° to the horizontal. Calculate the normal contact force acting on the block. (Take $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
一个质量为2.0 kg的块体沿与水平面成30°角的光滑斜面滑下。计算作用在块体上的法向接触力。（取$g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify forces / 步骤1：识别力**
- Weight: $W = mg = 2.0 \times 9.81 = 19.62 \text{ N}$ (vertical downward)
- Normal force: $N$ (perpendicular to plane, unknown)

**Step 2: Resolve weight into components / 步骤2：分解重量**
- Perpendicular to plane: $mg\cos\theta = 19.62 \times \cos30^\circ$
- Parallel to plane: $mg\sin\theta = 19.62 \times \sin30^\circ$

**Step 3: Apply Newton's Second Law perpendicular to plane / 步骤3：垂直于斜面方向应用牛顿第二定律**
No acceleration perpendicular to plane: $\sum F_\perp = 0$

$$N - mg\cos\theta = 0$$

**Step 4: Solve for N / 步骤4：求解N**

$$N = mg\cos\theta = 19.62 \times \cos30^\circ = 19.62 \times 0.8660 = 16.99 \text{ N}$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $N = 17.0 \text{ N}$ (3 significant figures) | **答案：** $N = 17.0 \text{ N}$（3位有效数字）

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** On an inclined plane, the normal force is always less than the weight. For $\theta = 30^\circ$, $N = mg\cos30^\circ \approx 0.866mg$.

**中文:** 在斜面上，法向力总是小于重量。对于$\theta = 30^\circ$，$N = mg\cos30^\circ \approx 0.866mg$。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
The normal contact force is central to several practical experiments in A-Level Physics:

1. **Measuring Coefficient of Friction:** In experiments where a block is pulled across a surface, the normal force is needed to calculate the coefficient of friction ($\mu = F_f / N$). Students must measure the weight of the block and ensure the surface is horizontal so $N = mg$.

2. **Investigating Motion on an Inclined Plane:** When studying acceleration down an incline, the normal force affects the frictional force (if present). Students must resolve forces correctly to predict acceleration.

3. **Spring Balance Experiments:** When using a spring balance to measure forces, the normal force from the support affects the reading.

4. **Uncertainty Considerations:** When calculating normal force, uncertainties in mass measurement ($\Delta m$) and angle measurement ($\Delta \theta$) propagate. For $N = mg\cos\theta$, the percentage uncertainty is:
   $$\frac{\Delta N}{N} = \frac{\Delta m}{m} + \frac{\Delta g}{g} + \tan\theta \cdot \Delta\theta$$

5. **Graph Plotting:** Students may plot normal force vs. angle of incline to verify the cosine relationship.

**中文:**
法向接触力是A-Level物理中几个实验的核心：

1. **测量摩擦系数：** 在将块体拉过表面的实验中，需要法向力来计算摩擦系数（$\mu = F_f / N$）。学生必须测量块体的重量，并确保表面水平，使$N = mg$。

2. **研究斜面运动：** 研究沿斜面加速时，法向力影响摩擦力（如果存在）。学生必须正确分解力以预测加速度。

3. **弹簧秤实验：** 使用弹簧秤测量力时，支撑物的法向力会影响读数。

4. **不确定度考虑：** 计算法向力时，质量测量（$\Delta m$）和角度测量（$\Delta \theta$）的不确定度会传播。对于$N = mg\cos\theta$，百分比不确定度为：
   $$\frac{\Delta N}{N} = \frac{\Delta m}{m} + \frac{\Delta g}{g} + \tan\theta \cdot \Delta\theta$$

5. **图表绘制：** 学生可以绘制法向力与斜面角度的关系图，以验证余弦关系。
