# Tension / 张力

---

# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Tension is the pulling force transmitted through a string, rope, cable, or chain when it is stretched by forces acting at both ends. It is a contact force that acts along the length of the connector, always pulling away from the object it is attached to. Tension is fundamental to understanding systems involving pulleys, hanging masses, and connected objects in [[Types of Force]]. Unlike [[Normal Contact Force]] or [[Friction]], tension can only pull — it cannot push.

In A-Level Physics, tension is often analyzed using [[Free-body Diagrams]] and resolved into components using [[Scalars and Vectors]]. It appears in problems involving equilibrium (stationary systems) and dynamics (accelerating systems), such as Atwood machines or objects suspended from ceilings.

**中文:**
张力是当绳子、绳索、缆绳或链条两端受到拉力作用时，沿其长度方向传递的拉力。它是一种接触力，沿连接物的长度方向作用，总是从它所连接的物体向外拉。张力是理解涉及滑轮、悬挂物体和连接物体系统的基础。与[[法向接触力]]或[[摩擦力]]不同，张力只能拉，不能推。

在A-Level物理中，通常使用[[受力分析图]]分析张力，并使用[[标量和矢量]]将其分解为分量。张力出现在涉及平衡（静止系统）和动力学（加速系统）的问题中，例如阿特伍德机或悬挂在天花板上的物体。

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**

### Nature of Tension
Tension is a **contact force** that arises from the electromagnetic interactions between atoms in the stretched material. In A-Level problems, we typically assume an **ideal string** — massless and inextensible — which means:
- Tension is **uniform** throughout the string (same magnitude at every point).
- The string does not stretch, so the acceleration of objects at both ends is the same magnitude.

### Direction of Tension
- Tension **always pulls** away from the object it is attached to.
- In a [[Free-body Diagrams|free-body diagram]], draw tension arrows **away from the object** along the string.
- For a hanging mass, tension pulls **upward** on the mass (opposing weight).

### Tension in Different Configurations

1. **Single hanging mass (stationary):**
   - Tension $T$ equals weight $mg$ (if no acceleration).
   - $T = mg$

2. **Two masses connected by a string over a pulley (Atwood machine):**
   - If masses are different, the system accelerates.
   - Tension is **not** equal to either weight — it lies between $m_1g$ and $m_2g$.
   - Use Newton's Second Law for each mass separately.

3. **String at an angle:**
   - Resolve tension into horizontal and vertical components using [[Scalars and Vectors]].
   - $T_x = T \cos \theta$, $T_y = T \sin \theta$

### Common Pitfalls
- ❌ **Assuming tension equals weight** in accelerating systems — only true in equilibrium.
- ❌ **Drawing tension pushing** — tension can only pull.
- ❌ **Forgetting that tension is the same** on both sides of an ideal pulley (frictionless, massless pulley).

**中文：**

### 张力的性质
张力是一种**接触力**，源于拉伸材料中原子的电磁相互作用。在A-Level问题中，我们通常假设**理想绳子**——无质量且不可伸长，这意味着：
- 张力在整根绳子上**均匀分布**（每一点大小相同）。
- 绳子不会伸长，因此两端物体的加速度大小相同。

### 张力的方向
- 张力**总是向外拉**，远离它所连接的物体。
- 在[[受力分析图]]中，沿绳子方向画出**远离物体**的张力箭头。
- 对于悬挂的质量，张力向上拉质量（与重力相反）。

### 不同配置中的张力

1. **单个悬挂质量（静止）：**
   - 张力 $T$ 等于重力 $mg$（如果没有加速度）。
   - $T = mg$

2. **通过滑轮连接的两个质量（阿特伍德机）：**
   - 如果质量不同，系统会加速。
   - 张力**不等于**任何一个重力——它介于 $m_1g$ 和 $m_2g$ 之间。
   - 分别对每个质量使用牛顿第二定律。

3. **成角度的绳子：**
   - 使用[[标量和矢量]]将张力分解为水平和垂直分量。
   - $T_x = T \cos \theta$，$T_y = T \sin \theta$

### 常见错误
- ❌ **在加速系统中假设张力等于重力**——仅在平衡时成立。
- ❌ **画出张力在推**——张力只能拉。
- ❌ **忘记理想滑轮两侧张力相同**（无摩擦、无质量滑轮）。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

## Primary Formula: Tension in a Stationary Hanging Mass

$$ T = mg $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $T$ | Tension in the string | 绳子中的张力 | N (newtons) |
| $m$ | Mass of the hanging object | 悬挂物体的质量 | kg |
| $g$ | Acceleration due to gravity ($9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$) | 重力加速度 | m s$^{-2}$ |

## General Formula: Newton's Second Law for Tension

For an object of mass $m$ connected by a string:

$$ \sum F = ma \quad \Rightarrow \quad T - mg = ma $$

Where $a$ is upward acceleration (positive upward).

## Tension in an Atwood Machine (Two Masses)

For masses $m_1$ and $m_2$ ($m_1 > m_2$) connected over a pulley:

$$ a = \frac{(m_1 - m_2)g}{m_1 + m_2} $$

$$ T = \frac{2m_1m_2g}{m_1 + m_2} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $a$ | Acceleration of the system | 系统的加速度 | m s$^{-2}$ |
| $T$ | Tension in the string | 绳子中的张力 | N |

**Derivation / 推导:**

For mass $m_1$ (heavier, moving down):
$$ m_1g - T = m_1a $$

For mass $m_2$ (lighter, moving up):
$$ T - m_2g = m_2a $$

Add the two equations:
$$ m_1g - m_2g = m_1a + m_2a $$
$$ (m_1 - m_2)g = (m_1 + m_2)a $$
$$ a = \frac{(m_1 - m_2)g}{m_1 + m_2} $$

Substitute $a$ back into either equation to find $T$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- String is massless and inextensible (ideal).
- Pulley is frictionless and massless.
- No air resistance.
- String remains taut throughout motion.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

## Example 1: Stationary Hanging Mass

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A 5.0 kg mass hangs stationary from a ceiling by a single vertical string. Calculate the tension in the string. (Take $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
一个5.0 kg的质量通过一根垂直绳子静止悬挂在天花板上。计算绳子中的张力。（取 $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1:** Identify forces acting on the mass.
- Weight $W = mg$ downward
- Tension $T$ upward

**Step 2:** Since the mass is stationary, it is in equilibrium. Net force = 0.

$$ \sum F = T - mg = 0 $$

**Step 3:** Solve for $T$.

$$ T = mg = (5.0)(9.81) = 49.05 \text{ N} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $T = 49 \text{ N}$ (to 2 significant figures)
**答案:** $T = 49 \text{ N}$（保留两位有效数字）

### Quick Tip / 提示
In equilibrium problems, always set the sum of forces to zero. For a stationary hanging mass, tension equals weight — but only if there is no acceleration.

在平衡问题中，始终将合力设为零。对于静止悬挂的质量，张力等于重力——但仅在没有加速度时成立。

## Example 2: Atwood Machine

### Question / 题目
**English:**
Two masses, $m_1 = 3.0 \text{ kg}$ and $m_2 = 1.0 \text{ kg}$, are connected by a light inextensible string over a frictionless pulley. Calculate:
(a) The acceleration of the system.
(b) The tension in the string.
(Take $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
两个质量 $m_1 = 3.0 \text{ kg}$ 和 $m_2 = 1.0 \text{ kg}$ 通过一根轻质不可伸长的绳子连接，绳子绕过无摩擦滑轮。计算：
(a) 系统的加速度。
(b) 绳子中的张力。
（取 $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1:** Write Newton's Second Law for each mass.

For $m_1$ (heavier, moving down):
$$ m_1g - T = m_1a $$

For $m_2$ (lighter, moving up):
$$ T - m_2g = m_2a $$

**Step 2:** Solve for acceleration $a$.

Add the two equations:
$$ m_1g - m_2g = m_1a + m_2a $$
$$ (m_1 - m_2)g = (m_1 + m_2)a $$
$$ a = \frac{(m_1 - m_2)g}{m_1 + m_2} = \frac{(3.0 - 1.0)(9.81)}{3.0 + 1.0} = \frac{2.0 \times 9.81}{4.0} = 4.905 \text{ m s}^{-2} $$

**Step 3:** Solve for tension $T$.

Substitute $a$ into the equation for $m_2$:
$$ T - m_2g = m_2a $$
$$ T = m_2(g + a) = 1.0(9.81 + 4.905) = 14.715 \text{ N} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:**
(a) $a = 4.9 \text{ m s}^{-2}$ (to 2 s.f.)
(b) $T = 15 \text{ N}$ (to 2 s.f.)

**答案:**
(a) $a = 4.9 \text{ m s}^{-2}$（保留两位有效数字）
(b) $T = 15 \text{ N}$（保留两位有效数字）

### Quick Tip / 提示
In Atwood machine problems, tension is **not** equal to either weight. Always solve the system of two equations simultaneously. Remember that the acceleration has the same magnitude for both masses but opposite directions.

在阿特伍德机问题中，张力**不等于**任何一个重力。始终同时解两个方程。记住两个质量的加速度大小相同但方向相反。
