# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores what happens to energy when it is "lost" from a mechanical system. In reality, no system is perfectly efficient. When work is done against forces like [[Friction]] or [[Air Resistance]], mechanical energy is not destroyed but is **dissipated** — it is transferred into non-useful forms, primarily thermal energy (heat) and sound. This concept is the bridge between the idealized [[Principle of Conservation of Energy]] and real-world [[Energy Transfers in Mechanical Systems]]. Understanding dissipative forces is crucial for calculating efficiency and explaining why perpetual motion machines are impossible.

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨当能量从机械系统中“损失”时会发生什么。在现实中，没有系统是完美高效的。当克服[[摩擦力]]或[[空气阻力]]等力做功时，机械能并没有被摧毁，而是被**耗散**了——它被转化为非有用的形式，主要是热能（热量）和声音。这个概念是理想化的[[能量守恒定律]]与现实世界中的[[机械系统中的能量转移]]之间的桥梁。理解耗散力对于计算效率和解释为什么永动机不可能存在至关重要。

# 2. Core Definition / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) |
| ------------ | --------------- | --------------- |
| **Dissipative Force** / 耗散力 | A force that causes mechanical energy (the sum of kinetic and potential energy) to be converted into other forms of energy (usually thermal or sound), which is not easily recovered. | 导致机械能（动能和势能之和）转化为其他形式能量（通常是热能或声能）且不易回收的力。 |
| **Energy Dissipation** / 能量耗散 | The process by which organized mechanical energy is transformed into disorganized thermal energy (internal energy) in the surroundings. | 有组织的机械能转化为周围环境中无组织的热能（内能）的过程。 |
| **Wasted Energy** / 无用能量 | The energy that is transferred to a non-useful store (e.g., thermal energy in the surroundings) as a result of dissipative forces. | 由于耗散力而转移到非有用储存库（例如，周围环境中的热能）的能量。 |
| **Useful Energy** / 有用能量 | The energy that is transferred to the intended, desired store or form (e.g., kinetic energy of a car). | 转移到预期、期望的储存库或形式（例如，汽车的动能）的能量。 |
| **Efficiency** / 效率 | The ratio of useful energy output to total energy input, often expressed as a percentage. | 有用能量输出与总能量输入之比，通常以百分比表示。 |

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
The core idea is that while total energy is always conserved ([[Principle of Conservation of Energy]]), the *quality* of energy degrades. A dissipative force, like friction between a box and a rough floor, does negative work on the system. This work removes mechanical energy from the system's "useful" store (its [[Kinetic Energy and Potential Energy]]) and transfers it to the internal (thermal) energy of the box and the floor.

**Step-by-step reasoning:**
1.  A moving object has kinetic energy.
2.  A dissipative force (e.g., friction) acts opposite to the direction of motion.
3.  Work is done by the dissipative force: $W = F \times d$.
4.  This work removes an equal amount of mechanical energy from the object.
5.  This energy is not destroyed; it is transferred to the thermal energy store of the surfaces in contact (they get warmer) and the surroundings.
6.  This thermal energy is highly disordered and very difficult to completely convert back into mechanical work.

**Common Pitfall:**
Students often think energy is "lost." It is not lost; it is **dissipated** or **wasted**. The total energy of the universe remains constant. The "loss" refers to the loss of *useful* mechanical energy available to do work.

**中文:**
核心思想是，虽然总能量总是守恒的（[[能量守恒定律]]），但能量的*品质*会下降。耗散力，例如盒子与粗糙地板之间的摩擦力，对系统做负功。这个功将机械能从系统的“有用”储存库（其[[动能和势能]]）中移除，并将其转移到盒子和地板的内能（热能）中。

**逐步推理：**
1.  一个运动的物体具有动能。
2.  一个耗散力（例如，摩擦力）作用在与运动方向相反的方向上。
3.  耗散力做功：$W = F \times d$。
4.  这个功从物体中移除了等量的机械能。
5.  这个能量并没有被摧毁；它被转移到接触表面的热能储存库（它们变热）和周围环境中。
6.  这种热能是高度无序的，并且很难完全转化回机械功。

**常见误区：**
学生经常认为能量“损失”了。它并没有损失；它是被**耗散**或**浪费**了。宇宙的总能量保持不变。“损失”指的是可用于做功的*有用*机械能的损失。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

The key formula for this sub-topic is the **Work Done by a Dissipative Force**, which quantifies the energy loss.

$$ W = F \times d $$

Where $F$ is the magnitude of the constant dissipative force and $d$ is the distance over which it acts.

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $W$ | Work done by the dissipative force (energy dissipated) | 耗散力所做的功（耗散的能量） | Joules (J) / 焦耳 (J) |
| $F$ | Magnitude of the dissipative force (e.g., friction) | 耗散力的大小（例如，摩擦力） | Newtons (N) / 牛顿 (N) |
| $d$ | Distance over which the force acts | 力作用的距离 | Metres (m) / 米 (m) |

**Derivation / 推导:**
This is derived directly from the definition of work: work is the product of force and displacement in the direction of the force. Since the dissipative force is constant and opposite to motion, the work done is simply $F \times d$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- The dissipative force must be **constant**.
- The distance $d$ is the total path length over which the force acts, not the net displacement.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A box of mass 5 kg is pushed with a constant horizontal force of 40 N across a rough floor. The box moves 12 m at a constant speed. Calculate:
(a) The work done by the pushing force.
(b) The work done against friction.
(c) The energy dissipated.

**中文:**
一个质量为 5 kg 的箱子被一个 40 N 的恒定水平力推过粗糙的地面。箱子以恒定速度移动了 12 m。计算：
(a) 推力所做的功。
(b) 克服摩擦力所做的功。
(c) 耗散的能量。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Work done by pushing force / 推力所做的功**
Since the force is constant and in the direction of motion:
$$ W_{push} = F \times d = 40 \text{ N} \times 12 \text{ m} = 480 \text{ J} $$

**(b) Work done against friction / 克服摩擦力所做的功**
The box moves at a constant speed, so the net force is zero. Therefore, the pushing force equals the frictional force in magnitude but opposite in direction.
$$ F_{friction} = 40 \text{ N} $$
The work done against friction is the work done by the frictional force:
$$ W_{friction} = F_{friction} \times d = 40 \text{ N} \times 12 \text{ m} = 480 \text{ J} $$

**(c) Energy dissipated / 耗散的能量**
The work done against the dissipative force (friction) is exactly the energy dissipated.
$$ \text{Energy Dissipated} = W_{friction} = 480 \text{ J} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) 480 J (b) 480 J (c) 480 J
**答案:** (a) 480 焦耳 (b) 480 焦耳 (c) 480 焦耳

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** If an object moves at a constant speed, the net work done on it is zero. The work input by the applied force is entirely dissipated by the frictional force.
**中文:** 如果物体以恒定速度运动，则对其所做的净功为零。施加力所做的输入功完全被摩擦力耗散。

# 7. Flashcards / 闪卡

**Card 1**
Q (EN): What is a dissipative force?
Q (CN): 什么是耗散力？
A (EN): A force that causes mechanical energy to be converted into non-useful forms like thermal energy (heat) and sound.
A (CN): 一种导致机械能转化为非有用形式（如热能（热量）和声音）的力。

**Card 2**
Q (EN): Is energy "lost" when a dissipative force acts?
Q (CN): 当耗散力作用时，能量会“损失”吗？
A (EN): No. Energy is conserved. It is transferred (dissipated) from a useful mechanical store to a less useful thermal store in the surroundings.
A (CN): 不会。能量是守恒的。它从有用的机械储存库转移到周围环境中不太有用的热能储存库（被耗散）。

**Card 3**
Q (EN): What is the formula for the work done by a constant dissipative force?
Q (CN): 恒定耗散力做功的公式是什么？
A (EN): $W = F \times d$, where F is the force and d is the distance over which it acts.
A (CN): $W = F \times d$，其中 F 是力，d 是力作用的距离。

**Card 4**
Q (EN): If a car travels at a constant speed on a flat road, what happens to the chemical energy from the fuel?
Q (CN): 如果一辆汽车在平坦的道路上以恒定速度行驶，燃料中的化学能会发生什么变化？
A (EN): It is entirely dissipated as thermal energy and sound due to friction and air resistance. No energy is converted to kinetic energy (since speed is constant).
A (CN): 由于摩擦力和空气阻力，它完全以热能和声能的形式被耗散。没有能量转化为动能（因为速度恒定）。

**Card 5**
Q (EN): What is the relationship between work done against friction and energy dissipated?
Q (CN): 克服摩擦力所做的功与耗散的能量之间有什么关系？
A (EN): They are equal. The work done against a dissipative force is the amount of mechanical energy that is dissipated.
A (CN): 它们是相等的。克服耗散力所做的功就是被耗散的机械能的量。
