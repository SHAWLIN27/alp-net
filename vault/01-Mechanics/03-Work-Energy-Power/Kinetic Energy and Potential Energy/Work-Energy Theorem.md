# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
The Work-Energy Theorem is one of the most powerful and elegant principles in physics, directly linking the concept of [[Work Done by a Force]] to changes in [[Kinetic Energy (KE)]]. This theorem states that the net work done on an object equals its change in kinetic energy. It provides a direct bridge between forces (causes of motion) and energy (a measure of motion), allowing us to solve problems without needing to analyze forces and accelerations in detail. This sub-topic is fundamental to understanding how energy transfers occur in mechanical systems and forms the foundation for the [[Conservation of Energy]] principle. For A-Level students, mastering this theorem is essential for solving dynamics problems efficiently, especially those involving variable forces or curved paths.

**中文:**
功-能定理是物理学中最强大、最优雅的原理之一，它直接将[[Work Done by a Force|力做功]]的概念与[[Kinetic Energy (KE)|动能]]的变化联系起来。该定理指出，作用在物体上的净功等于其动能的变化。它在力（运动的原因）和能量（运动的量度）之间架起了一座直接的桥梁，使我们无需详细分析力和加速度就能解决问题。这个子知识点对于理解机械系统中能量如何转移至关重要，并构成了[[Conservation of Energy|能量守恒]]原理的基础。对于A-Level学生来说，掌握这个定理对于高效解决动力学问题至关重要，特别是那些涉及变力或曲线路径的问题。

# 2. Core Definition / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) |
| ------------ | --------------- | --------------- |
| **Work-Energy Theorem** / 功-能定理 | The net work done on an object equals the change in its kinetic energy. | 作用在物体上的净功等于其动能的变化。 |
| **Net Work** / 净功 | The total work done by all forces acting on an object, equal to the sum of work done by each force. | 所有作用在物体上的力所做的总功，等于每个力所做功的总和。 |
| **Change in Kinetic Energy** / 动能变化 | The difference between final and initial kinetic energy: $\Delta KE = KE_f - KE_i$ | 末动能与初动能之差：$\Delta KE = KE_f - KE_i$ |
| **Positive Work** / 正功 | Work done by a force that increases the object's kinetic energy (force and displacement in same direction). | 增加物体动能的力所做的功（力与位移方向相同）。 |
| **Negative Work** / 负功 | Work done by a force that decreases the object's kinetic energy (force and displacement in opposite direction). | 减少物体动能的力所做的功（力与位移方向相反）。 |

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
The Work-Energy Theorem is expressed mathematically as:

$$W_{net} = \Delta KE = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

Where $W_{net}$ is the net work done on the object.

**Step-by-step reasoning:**
1. **Identify all forces** acting on the object (gravity, friction, tension, normal reaction, applied forces, etc.)
2. **Calculate work done by each force** using $W = F \cdot s \cdot \cos\theta$ (see [[Work Done by a Force]])
3. **Sum all work** to find $W_{net}$ (positive work adds energy, negative work removes energy)
4. **Equate to change in kinetic energy**: $W_{net} = \Delta KE$

**Physical interpretation:**
- **Positive net work** → object speeds up (KE increases)
- **Negative net work** → object slows down (KE decreases)
- **Zero net work** → constant speed (KE unchanged)

**Key insight:** The theorem works for any path, including curved paths, because work is a scalar quantity. This makes it far more powerful than Newton's Second Law for problems involving variable forces or complex trajectories.

**Common pitfalls:**
- Forgetting to include ALL forces (especially weight and friction)
- Confusing net work with work done by a single force
- Using the theorem when non-mechanical energy transfers occur (e.g., heat from friction — but friction work is still accounted for)
- Not recognizing that the theorem applies to translational kinetic energy only (not rotational)

**Connection to [[Gravitational Potential Energy (GPE)]] and [[Elastic Potential Energy]]:**
When conservative forces (gravity, springs) do work, the Work-Energy Theorem can be extended to include potential energy changes, leading to the full [[Conservation of Energy]] principle.

**中文:**
功-能定理的数学表达式为：

$$W_{net} = \Delta KE = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

其中 $W_{net}$ 是作用在物体上的净功。

**逐步推理：**
1. **识别所有力** 作用在物体上的力（重力、摩擦力、张力、法向反作用力、施加的力等）
2. **计算每个力所做的功** 使用 $W = F \cdot s \cdot \cos\theta$（参见[[Work Done by a Force|力做功]]）
3. **求和所有功** 得到 $W_{net}$（正功增加能量，负功减少能量）
4. **等于动能的变化**：$W_{net} = \Delta KE$

**物理意义：**
- **正净功** → 物体加速（动能增加）
- **负净功** → 物体减速（动能减少）
- **零净功** → 匀速（动能不变）

**关键洞察：** 该定理适用于任何路径，包括曲线路径，因为功是标量。这使得它在处理涉及变力或复杂轨迹的问题时比牛顿第二定律强大得多。

**常见陷阱：**
- 忘记包括所有力（特别是重力和摩擦力）
- 将净功与单个力所做的功混淆
- 在发生非机械能转移时使用该定理（例如，摩擦产生的热量——但摩擦功仍然被计入）
- 没有认识到该定理仅适用于平动动能（不适用于转动）

**与[[Gravitational Potential Energy (GPE)|重力势能]]和[[Elastic Potential Energy|弹性势能]]的联系：**
当保守力（重力、弹簧）做功时，功-能定理可以扩展到包括势能变化，从而引出完整的[[Conservation of Energy|能量守恒]]原理。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

## Primary Formula / 主要公式

$$W_{net} = \Delta KE = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $W_{net}$ | Net work done on the object | 作用在物体上的净功 | J (joules) |
| $\Delta KE$ | Change in kinetic energy | 动能的变化 | J (joules) |
| $m$ | Mass of the object | 物体的质量 | kg |
| $v_f$ | Final velocity | 末速度 | m/s |
| $v_i$ | Initial velocity | 初速度 | m/s |

## Expanded Form / 展开形式

$$W_{net} = \sum (F \cdot s \cdot \cos\theta) = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

## Alternative Form (for constant net force) / 替代形式（适用于恒定净力）

$$F_{net} \cdot s \cdot \cos\theta = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

Where $F_{net}$ is the magnitude of the net force, $s$ is the displacement, and $\theta$ is the angle between the net force and displacement.

**Derivation / 推导:**
Starting from Newton's Second Law: $F_{net} = ma$
Using $v_f^2 = v_i^2 + 2as$ (constant acceleration), we get $a = \frac{v_f^2 - v_i^2}{2s}$
Substituting: $F_{net} = m \cdot \frac{v_f^2 - v_i^2}{2s}$
Therefore: $F_{net} \cdot s = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$
For the general case with angle: $F_{net} \cdot s \cdot \cos\theta = \Delta KE$

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- The theorem applies to all situations where work is defined
- For variable forces, use $W = \int F \cdot dx$ (beyond A-Level scope but conceptually important)
- The theorem applies to translational kinetic energy only
- All work must be calculated in the same inertial reference frame

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

## Example 1: Horizontal Motion with Friction

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A block of mass 5.0 kg is pulled along a rough horizontal surface by a constant horizontal force of 40 N. The frictional force opposing motion is 12 N. The block starts from rest and moves a distance of 8.0 m. Use the Work-Energy Theorem to find the final speed of the block.

**中文:**
一个质量为5.0 kg的方块在粗糙水平面上被一个大小为40 N的恒定水平力拉动。阻碍运动的摩擦力为12 N。方块从静止开始运动了8.0 m的距离。使用功-能定理求方块的末速度。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify forces and calculate work done by each force**

Applied force: $W_F = F \cdot s \cdot \cos 0° = 40 \times 8.0 \times 1 = 320 \text{ J}$

Friction: $W_f = f \cdot s \cdot \cos 180° = 12 \times 8.0 \times (-1) = -96 \text{ J}$

Weight and normal reaction do no work (perpendicular to displacement): $W_{mg} = W_N = 0 \text{ J}$

**Step 2: Calculate net work**

$$W_{net} = 320 + (-96) + 0 + 0 = 224 \text{ J}$$

**Step 3: Apply Work-Energy Theorem**

$$W_{net} = \Delta KE = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

Since the block starts from rest, $v_i = 0$:

$$224 = \frac{1}{2} \times 5.0 \times v_f^2 - 0$$

$$224 = 2.5 \times v_f^2$$

$$v_f^2 = \frac{224}{2.5} = 89.6$$

$$v_f = \sqrt{89.6} = 9.47 \text{ m/s}$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $v_f = 9.5 \text{ m/s}$ (to 2 significant figures)
**答案:** $v_f = 9.5 \text{ m/s}$（保留两位有效数字）

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Always include the work done by ALL forces, even if some do zero work. The Work-Energy Theorem is often faster than using $F=ma$ and kinematic equations, especially when the net force is not constant or when multiple forces are involved.

**中文:** 始终包括所有力所做的功，即使有些力做功为零。功-能定理通常比使用 $F=ma$ 和运动学方程更快，特别是当净力不恒定或涉及多个力时。

## Example 2: Motion on an Inclined Plane

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A block of mass 2.0 kg slides down a rough inclined plane of length 5.0 m at an angle of 30° to the horizontal. The coefficient of kinetic friction between the block and the plane is 0.20. The block starts from rest at the top. Use the Work-Energy Theorem to find the speed of the block at the bottom of the plane.

**中文:**
一个质量为2.0 kg的方块沿粗糙斜面下滑，斜面长度为5.0 m，与水平面夹角为30°。方块与斜面之间的动摩擦系数为0.20。方块从顶端静止开始下滑。使用功-能定理求方块到达斜面底端时的速度。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify forces and calculate work**

Weight component parallel to slope: $mg\sin\theta = 2.0 \times 9.81 \times \sin 30° = 2.0 \times 9.81 \times 0.5 = 9.81 \text{ N}$

Work done by gravity (parallel component): $W_g = mg\sin\theta \cdot s = 9.81 \times 5.0 = 49.05 \text{ J}$

Normal reaction: $N = mg\cos\theta = 2.0 \times 9.81 \times \cos 30° = 2.0 \times 9.81 \times 0.866 = 16.99 \text{ N}$

Friction force: $f = \mu N = 0.20 \times 16.99 = 3.40 \text{ N}$

Work done by friction: $W_f = -f \cdot s = -3.40 \times 5.0 = -17.0 \text{ J}$

**Step 2: Calculate net work**

$$W_{net} = 49.05 + (-17.0) = 32.05 \text{ J}$$

**Step 3: Apply Work-Energy Theorem**

$$W_{net} = \frac{1}{2}mv_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}mv_i^2$$

Since $v_i = 0$:

$$32.05 = \frac{1}{2} \times 2.0 \times v_f^2$$

$$32.05 = v_f^2$$

$$v_f = \sqrt{32.05} = 5.66 \text{ m/s}$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $v_f = 5.7 \text{ m/s}$ (to 2 significant figures)
**答案:** $v_f = 5.7 \text{ m/s}$（保留两位有效数字）

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Notice that we didn't need to calculate acceleration or use kinematic equations. The Work-Energy Theorem directly relates the net work (from all forces) to the change in speed. This approach is particularly powerful when the forces are not constant or when the path is curved.

**中文:** 注意我们不需要计算加速度或使用运动学方程。功-能定理直接将净功（来自所有力）与速度变化联系起来。当力不恒定或路径为曲线时，这种方法特别强大。
