# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Efficiency is a measure of how well a device or system converts input energy (or power) into useful output energy (or power). In an ideal world, all energy transfers would be 100% efficient, but in reality, energy is always dissipated to the surroundings, often as heat and sound. This sub-topic explores the quantitative definition of efficiency, how to calculate it for both energy and power transfers, and why no real device can ever be perfectly efficient. Understanding efficiency is crucial for evaluating the performance of machines, engines, and electrical appliances, and it links directly to the [[Conservation of Energy]] principle and the concept of [[Work Done by a Force]].

**中文:**
效率是衡量一个设备或系统将输入能量（或功率）转换为有用输出能量（或功率）好坏程度的指标。在理想情况下，所有能量转换都应该是100%高效的，但实际上，能量总是会耗散到周围环境中，通常以热和声音的形式。本子知识点探讨了效率的定量定义、如何计算能量和功率转换的效率，以及为什么没有实际设备能够完美高效。理解效率对于评估机器、发动机和电器的性能至关重要，并且它直接联系到[[能量守恒定律]]和[[力做的功]]的概念。

# 2. Core Definition / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) |
| ------------ | --------------- | --------------- |
| **Efficiency** / 效率 | The ratio of useful output energy (or power) to total input energy (or power), often expressed as a percentage. | 有用输出能量（或功率）与总输入能量（或功率）的比值，通常以百分比表示。 |
| **Useful Output Energy** / 有用输出能量 | The energy that is transferred to the intended, desired form by a device. | 设备转换到预期、所需形式的能量。 |
| **Wasted Energy** / 浪费的能量 | The energy that is dissipated to the surroundings in a non-useful form (e.g., heat, sound, light). | 以非有用形式（如热、声音、光）耗散到周围环境中的能量。 |
| **Dissipation** / 耗散 | The process by which useful energy is transformed into a less useful form, often thermal energy, and spreads to the surroundings. | 有用能量转化为不太有用的形式（通常是热能）并扩散到周围环境的过程。 |
| **Input Power** / 输入功率 | The total power supplied to a device or system. | 提供给设备或系统的总功率。 |
| **Output Power** / 输出功率 | The useful power delivered by a device or system. | 设备或系统输出的有用功率。 |

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
Efficiency is fundamentally a consequence of the [[Conservation of Energy]]. Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transferred from one store to another. In any real process, some of the input energy is inevitably transferred to non-useful stores (e.g., thermal energy in the surroundings). This "wasted" energy is not lost; it is simply not in the form we wanted.

The efficiency of a device can be calculated in two equivalent ways:
1.  **Using Energy:** $Efficiency = \frac{Useful\ Output\ Energy}{Total\ Input\ Energy}$
2.  **Using Power:** $Efficiency = \frac{Useful\ Output\ Power}{Total\ Input\ Power}$

Since power is the rate of energy transfer ($P = \frac{E}{t}$), these two formulas are mathematically identical, provided the time interval is the same for both input and output.

**Common Pitfalls:**
*   **Confusing "wasted" with "lost":** Wasted energy is not lost; it is still present in the surroundings as thermal energy.
*   **Forgetting the percentage:** Efficiency is often required as a percentage, so remember to multiply the decimal ratio by 100%.
*   **Using the wrong values:** Always ensure you are using the *useful* output, not the total output. For example, a light bulb's useful output is light, not the heat it also produces.

**中文:**
效率本质上是[[能量守恒定律]]的一个结果。能量既不能被创造也不能被消灭，只能从一种储存形式转移到另一种。在任何实际过程中，一部分输入能量不可避免地会转移到非有用的储存形式（例如，周围环境中的热能）。这种“浪费的”能量并没有消失；它只是没有以我们想要的形式存在。

设备的效率可以通过两种等效方式计算：
1.  **使用能量：** $效率 = \frac{有用输出能量}{总输入能量}$
2.  **使用功率：** $效率 = \frac{有用输出功率}{总输入功率}$

由于功率是能量转换的速率（$P = \frac{E}{t}$），只要输入和输出的时间间隔相同，这两个公式在数学上是完全相同的。

**常见误区：**
*   **混淆“浪费”与“丢失”：** 浪费的能量并没有丢失；它仍然以热能的形式存在于周围环境中。
*   **忘记百分比：** 效率通常需要以百分比表示，所以记得将小数比值乘以100%。
*   **使用错误的值：** 始终确保你使用的是*有用的*输出，而不是总输出。例如，灯泡的有用输出是光，而不是它同时产生的热。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

The core formula for efficiency:

$$ \eta = \frac{E_{useful}}{E_{input}} \times 100\% $$

Or, using power:

$$ \eta = \frac{P_{useful}}{P_{input}} \times 100\% $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $\eta$ | Efficiency (eta) | 效率 | % (percentage) |
| $E_{useful}$ | Useful output energy | 有用输出能量 | J (Joules) |
| $E_{input}$ | Total input energy | 总输入能量 | J (Joules) |
| $P_{useful}$ | Useful output power | 有用输出功率 | W (Watts) |
| $P_{input}$ | Total input power | 总输入功率 | W (Watts) |

**Derivation / 推导:**
The formula is a direct definition. It is derived from the concept of a ratio. If a device receives 100 J of energy and converts 25 J into useful work, then for every 1 J of input, 0.25 J is useful. The ratio is 25/100 = 0.25, or 25%.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
*   The formula applies to any energy transfer process.
*   The efficiency can never be greater than 1 (or 100%) for a real process, as this would violate the [[Conservation of Energy]].
*   When using the power formula, ensure that the input and output powers are measured over the same time interval.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A motor is used to lift a mass of 20 kg through a vertical height of 5.0 m. The motor is supplied with 1500 J of electrical energy. Calculate the efficiency of the motor. (Take $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
一个电动机用于将20 kg的质量提升5.0米的垂直高度。电动机被提供了1500 J的电能。计算该电动机的效率。（取 $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Calculate the useful output energy.**
The useful output is the gain in gravitational potential energy (GPE) of the mass.
$$E_{useful} = \Delta GPE = mgh$$
$$E_{useful} = (20 \text{ kg}) \times (9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}) \times (5.0 \text{ m})$$
$$E_{useful} = 981 \text{ J}$$

**Step 2: Apply the efficiency formula.**
$$\eta = \frac{E_{useful}}{E_{input}} \times 100\%$$
$$\eta = \frac{981 \text{ J}}{1500 \text{ J}} \times 100\%$$
$$\eta = 0.654 \times 100\%$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** 65.4% **答案:** 65.4%

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Always identify the *useful* output energy first. In lifting problems, the useful output is almost always the gain in gravitational potential energy ($mgh$). The wasted energy is the difference between input and useful output ($1500 \text{ J} - 981 \text{ J} = 519 \text{ J}$).

**中文:** 始终首先确定*有用的*输出能量。在提升问题中，有用输出几乎总是重力势能的增加（$mgh$）。浪费的能量是输入与有用输出之间的差值（$1500 \text{ J} - 981 \text{ J} = 519 \text{ J}$）。
