# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic extends the basic definition of [[Definition of Work]] to situations where the force is not applied parallel to the direction of motion. In real-world physics, forces are rarely perfectly aligned with displacement. For example, when you pull a suitcase on wheels, you apply the force at an angle to the horizontal. This sub-topic introduces the crucial concept of the **component of force** in the direction of motion. Understanding this is essential for correctly calculating energy transfer and links directly to [[Kinetic Energy and Potential Energy]] via the Work-Energy Principle. It also relies heavily on your understanding of [[Scalars and Vectors]], specifically vector resolution.

**中文:**
本子知识点将[[Definition of Work]]的基本定义扩展到力不平行于运动方向的情况。在现实物理中，力很少与位移完全对齐。例如，当你拉一个带轮子的行李箱时，你是以一个角度施加水平方向的力。本子知识点引入了**力的分量**在运动方向上的关键概念。理解这一点对于正确计算能量转移至关重要，并通过功能原理直接联系到[[Kinetic Energy and Potential Energy]]。它也严重依赖于你对[[Scalars and Vectors]]的理解，特别是矢量分解。

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
The core idea is that only the part of the force that acts **parallel** to the displacement does work. The perpendicular component does no work because there is no displacement in that direction.

1.  **Vector Resolution:** To find the component of the force $F$ in the direction of displacement $s$, we use trigonometry. If the force is applied at an angle $\theta$ to the displacement, the component parallel to the displacement is $F \cos \theta$.
2.  **The Formula:** The general equation for work done is therefore $W = F s \cos \theta$.
    *   If $\theta = 0^\circ$ (force parallel to displacement), $\cos 0^\circ = 1$, so $W = Fs$. This is the simple case from [[Definition of Work]].
    *   If $\theta = 90^\circ$ (force perpendicular to displacement), $\cos 90^\circ = 0$, so $W = 0$. This is why the normal reaction force from a surface does no work on an object sliding across it.
    *   If $\theta = 180^\circ$ (force opposite to displacement), $\cos 180^\circ = -1$, so $W = -Fs$. This is negative work, meaning the force is removing energy from the object (e.g., friction).
3.  **Common Pitfall:** Students often forget to check the angle. The angle $\theta$ is always the angle **between** the force vector and the displacement vector. It is not the angle of the slope or any other arbitrary angle.

**中文:**
核心思想是，只有与位移**平行**的那部分力才做功。垂直分量不做功，因为在该方向上没有位移。

1.  **矢量分解：** 为了找到力 $F$ 在位移 $s$ 方向上的分量，我们使用三角函数。如果力与位移之间的夹角为 $\theta$，则平行于位移的分量为 $F \cos \theta$。
2.  **公式：** 因此，做功的通用方程为 $W = F s \cos \theta$。
    *   如果 $\theta = 0^\circ$（力平行于位移），$\cos 0^\circ = 1$，所以 $W = Fs$。这是[[Definition of Work]]中的简单情况。
    *   如果 $\theta = 90^\circ$（力垂直于位移），$\cos 90^\circ = 0$，所以 $W = 0$。这就是为什么来自表面的法向反作用力对在其上滑动的物体不做功。
    *   如果 $\theta = 180^\circ$（力与位移相反），$\cos 180^\circ = -1$，所以 $W = -Fs$。这是负功，意味着该力正在从物体中移除能量（例如，摩擦力）。
3.  **常见陷阱：** 学生经常忘记检查角度。角度 $\theta$ 始终是**力矢量**和**位移矢量**之间的夹角。它不是斜坡的角度或任何其他任意角度。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

The key formula for this sub-topic is:

$$ W = F s \cos \theta $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $W$ | Work Done | 做功 | J (Joules) |
| $F$ | Magnitude of the applied force | 施加力的大小 | N (Newtons) |
| $s$ | Magnitude of the displacement | 位移的大小 | m (metres) |
| $\theta$ | Angle between the force vector and the displacement vector | 力矢量与位移矢量之间的夹角 | degrees or radians |

**Derivation / 推导:**
1.  Resolve the force $F$ into components parallel ($F_{\parallel}$) and perpendicular ($F_{\perp}$) to the displacement $s$.
2.  $F_{\parallel} = F \cos \theta$
3.  $F_{\perp} = F \sin \theta$
4.  Work is only done by the parallel component: $W = F_{\parallel} \times s = (F \cos \theta) \times s = F s \cos \theta$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
*   The force $F$ must be constant.
*   The displacement $s$ must be in a straight line.
*   The angle $\theta$ is measured between the force and displacement vectors.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A child pulls a toy wagon with a force of 50 N at an angle of 60° to the horizontal. The wagon is displaced 20 m along a straight, horizontal path. Calculate the work done by the child on the wagon.

**中文:**
一个小孩用50 N的力以与水平方向成60°角拉动一辆玩具车。玩具车沿直线水平路径被移动了20米。计算小孩对玩具车所做的功。

### Solution / 解答

1.  **Identify knowns:**
    *   Force, $F = 50 \text{ N}$
    *   Displacement, $s = 20 \text{ m}$
    *   Angle between force and displacement, $\theta = 60^\circ$

2.  **Choose the correct formula:**
    $$ W = F s \cos \theta $$

3.  **Substitute values:**
    $$ W = (50 \text{ N}) \times (20 \text{ m}) \times \cos(60^\circ) $$

4.  **Calculate:**
    $$ \cos(60^\circ) = 0.5 $$
    $$ W = 50 \times 20 \times 0.5 $$
    $$ W = 1000 \times 0.5 $$
    $$ W = 500 \text{ J} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** 500 J **答案:** 500 焦耳

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Always draw a quick sketch of the force and displacement vectors. Mark the angle between them. This will prevent you from using the wrong angle (e.g., using 30° instead of 60°).

**中文:** 总是快速画出力和位移矢量的草图。标记它们之间的夹角。这可以防止你使用错误的角度（例如，使用30°而不是60°）。
