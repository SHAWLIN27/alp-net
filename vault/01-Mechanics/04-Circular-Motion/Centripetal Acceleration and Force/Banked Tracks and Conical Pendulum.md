# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic extends the basic concept of [[Centripetal Force]] to two classic real-world scenarios where the centripetal force is provided by a combination of forces rather than a single tension or friction. For a **banked track**, the horizontal component of the normal reaction from the road (or track) provides the centripetal force, allowing vehicles to turn safely without relying on friction. For a **conical pendulum**, the horizontal component of the string tension provides the centripetal force, causing the bob to move in a horizontal circle while the string sweeps out a cone.

Understanding these systems is crucial for A-Level exams because they require resolving forces into horizontal (centripetal) and vertical (equilibrium) components. They are a direct application of [[Newton's Laws of Motion]] and [[Angular Measures]]. This sub-topic bridges the gap between simple circular motion and more complex dynamics seen in [[Circular Orbits]].

**中文:**
本子知识点将[[向心力]]的基本概念扩展到两个经典的实际场景：**倾斜轨道**和**圆锥摆**。在倾斜轨道上，来自路面（或轨道）的法向反作用力的水平分量提供向心力，使车辆无需依赖摩擦力即可安全转弯。对于圆锥摆，绳子张力的水平分量提供向心力，使摆锤在水平面上做圆周运动，同时绳子扫出一个圆锥体。

理解这些系统对A-Level考试至关重要，因为它们要求将力分解为水平（向心）和垂直（平衡）分量。它们是[[牛顿运动定律]]和[[角度测量]]的直接应用。本子知识点连接了简单的圆周运动与[[圆形轨道]]中更复杂的动力学。

# 3. Key Concepts / 关键概念

**English:**
The core skill for this sub-topic is **resolving forces** in a non-inertial (but effectively static) frame.

**1. Banked Tracks (No Friction):**
- The vehicle is moving in a horizontal circle of radius $r$.
- The forces acting are: weight ($mg$) downwards and normal reaction ($N$) perpendicular to the track surface.
- **Vertical Equilibrium:** $N \cos \theta = mg$ (No vertical acceleration).
- **Horizontal Centripetal Force:** $N \sin \theta = \frac{mv^2}{r}$.
- By dividing the two equations, we get the **design speed** for a banked track: $\tan \theta = \frac{v^2}{rg}$.
- **Common Pitfall:** Students often forget that the normal reaction is *not* equal to $mg$ here. It is larger than $mg$ because it must provide both the vertical support and the horizontal centripetal force.

**2. Conical Pendulum:**
- The bob moves in a horizontal circle of radius $r$.
- The string of length $L$ makes an angle $\theta$ with the vertical.
- The forces are: weight ($mg$) downwards and tension ($T$) along the string.
- **Vertical Equilibrium:** $T \cos \theta = mg$.
- **Horizontal Centripetal Force:** $T \sin \theta = m \omega^2 r$, where $\omega$ is the angular speed.
- **Key Result:** Combining these gives $\tan \theta = \frac{\omega^2 r}{g}$. Since $r = L \sin \theta$, we can derive $\cos \theta = \frac{g}{\omega^2 L}$.
- **Physical Interpretation:** The angle $\theta$ depends only on $\omega$ and $L$, not on the mass of the bob. A faster rotation means a larger angle (the string becomes more horizontal).

**中文:**
本子知识点的核心技能是在非惯性（但实际上是静态）参考系中**分解力**。

**1. 倾斜轨道（无摩擦）：**
- 车辆在半径为 $r$ 的水平圆上运动。
- 作用力：向下的重力 ($mg$) 和垂直于轨道表面的法向反作用力 ($N$)。
- **垂直平衡：** $N \cos \theta = mg$（无垂直加速度）。
- **水平向心力：** $N \sin \theta = \frac{mv^2}{r}$。
- 通过两式相除，得到倾斜轨道的**设计速度**：$\tan \theta = \frac{v^2}{rg}$。
- **常见错误：** 学生经常忘记这里的法向反作用力 *不等于* $mg$。它大于 $mg$，因为它必须同时提供垂直支撑和水平向心力。

**2. 圆锥摆：**
- 摆锤在半径为 $r$ 的水平圆上运动。
- 长度为 $L$ 的绳子与垂直方向成 $\theta$ 角。
- 作用力：向下的重力 ($mg$) 和沿绳子方向的张力 ($T$)。
- **垂直平衡：** $T \cos \theta = mg$。
- **水平向心力：** $T \sin \theta = m \omega^2 r$，其中 $\omega$ 是角速度。
- **关键结果：** 结合这两个方程得到 $\tan \theta = \frac{\omega^2 r}{g}$。由于 $r = L \sin \theta$，我们可以推导出 $\cos \theta = \frac{g}{\omega^2 L}$。
- **物理解释：** 角度 $\theta$ 仅取决于 $\omega$ 和 $L$，与摆锤的质量无关。旋转越快，角度越大（绳子变得更水平）。

# 4. Formulas / 公式

**Formula 1: Banked Track (Ideal Speed)**
$$ \tan \theta = \frac{v^2}{rg} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $\theta$ | Angle of banking | 倾斜角 | rad or ° |
| $v$ | Speed of vehicle | 车辆速度 | m s⁻¹ |
| $r$ | Radius of circular path | 圆轨道半径 | m |
| $g$ | Acceleration due to gravity | 重力加速度 | m s⁻² |

**Derivation / 推导:**
1. Resolve $N$ vertically: $N \cos \theta = mg$.
2. Resolve $N$ horizontally: $N \sin \theta = \frac{mv^2}{r}$.
3. Divide (2) by (1): $\frac{N \sin \theta}{N \cos \theta} = \frac{mv^2 / r}{mg} \implies \tan \theta = \frac{v^2}{rg}$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- No friction is required for the turn.
- The vehicle is moving at the **design speed** $v$.
- The track is banked at angle $\theta$.

---

**Formula 2: Conical Pendulum (Angle vs Angular Speed)**
$$ \cos \theta = \frac{g}{\omega^2 L} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
| ------------- | ------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| $\theta$ | Angle string makes with vertical | 绳子与垂直方向的夹角 | rad or ° |
| $\omega$ | Angular speed of bob | 摆锤的角速度 | rad s⁻¹ |
| $L$ | Length of string | 绳子长度 | m |
| $g$ | Acceleration due to gravity | 重力加速度 | m s⁻² |

**Derivation / 推导:**
1. Vertical: $T \cos \theta = mg$.
2. Horizontal: $T \sin \theta = m \omega^2 r$.
3. Divide (2) by (1): $\tan \theta = \frac{\omega^2 r}{g}$.
4. Geometry: $r = L \sin \theta$.
5. Substitute: $\tan \theta = \frac{\omega^2 (L \sin \theta)}{g} \implies \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta} = \frac{\omega^2 L \sin \theta}{g}$.
6. Cancel $\sin \theta$ (assuming $\theta \neq 0$): $\frac{1}{\cos \theta} = \frac{\omega^2 L}{g} \implies \cos \theta = \frac{g}{\omega^2 L}$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- The bob moves in a perfect horizontal circle.
- The string is light and inextensible.
- Air resistance is negligible.

# 6. Worked Example / 典型例题

### Example 1: Banked Track

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A racing track has a curve of radius 50 m. The track is banked at an angle of 15° to the horizontal. Calculate the speed at which a car can take the curve without relying on friction. (Take $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
一条赛车道有一个半径为 50 m 的弯道。赛道相对于水平面倾斜了 15° 角。计算汽车在不依赖摩擦力的情况下通过该弯道的速度。（取 $g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答
1. **Identify the formula:** $\tan \theta = \frac{v^2}{rg}$.
2. **Rearrange for $v$:** $v = \sqrt{rg \tan \theta}$.
3. **Substitute values:** $v = \sqrt{50 \times 9.81 \times \tan 15^\circ}$.
4. **Calculate:** $\tan 15^\circ \approx 0.268$, so $v = \sqrt{50 \times 9.81 \times 0.268} = \sqrt{131.45} \approx 11.5 \text{ m s}^{-1}$.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $v \approx 11.5 \text{ m s}^{-1}$ **答案:** $v \approx 11.5 \text{ m s}^{-1}$

### Quick Tip / 提示
Always check if the question asks for the *design speed* (no friction) or if friction is involved. For design speed, use $\tan \theta = v^2/rg$ directly.

### Example 2: Conical Pendulum

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A conical pendulum has a string of length 0.80 m. The bob rotates in a horizontal circle at an angular speed of 4.0 rad s⁻¹. Calculate the angle the string makes with the vertical. ($g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$)

**中文:**
一个圆锥摆的绳子长度为 0.80 m。摆锤以 4.0 rad s⁻¹ 的角速度在水平面上做圆周运动。计算绳子与垂直方向的夹角。（$g = 9.81 \text{ m s}^{-2}$）

### Solution / 解答
1. **Identify the formula:** $\cos \theta = \frac{g}{\omega^2 L}$.
2. **Substitute values:** $\cos \theta = \frac{9.81}{(4.0)^2 \times 0.80} = \frac{9.81}{16 \times 0.80} = \frac{9.81}{12.8}$.
3. **Calculate:** $\cos \theta \approx 0.766$.
4. **Find angle:** $\theta = \cos^{-1}(0.766) \approx 40^\circ$.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $\theta \approx 40^\circ$ **答案:** $\theta \approx 40^\circ$

### Quick Tip / 提示
If the question gives the *linear speed* $v$ instead of $\omega$, use $\omega = v/r$ and $r = L \sin \theta$ to derive a formula in terms of $v$: $\tan \theta = \frac{v^2}{gL \sin \theta}$, which simplifies to $\frac{v^2}{gL} = \frac{\sin^2 \theta}{\cos \theta}$.
