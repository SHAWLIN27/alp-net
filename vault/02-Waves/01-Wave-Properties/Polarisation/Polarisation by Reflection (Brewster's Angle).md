# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores how unpolarised light becomes partially or fully polarised when reflected off a non-metallic surface. The key concept is **Brewster's Angle** — the specific angle of incidence at which the reflected light is completely polarised perpendicular to the plane of incidence. This phenomenon is a direct consequence of the transverse wave nature of light and provides a practical method for producing polarised light without using polarising filters. Understanding Brewster's angle is essential for applications such as polarising sunglasses, photography filters, and analysing surface properties. This sub-topic builds on [[Progressive Waves]] and connects to [[What is Polarisation]] and [[Polarisation by Filters (Malus's Law)]].

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨非偏振光在非金属表面反射时如何变成部分或完全偏振光。核心概念是**布儒斯特角**——入射光以特定角度入射时，反射光完全垂直于入射面偏振。这一现象是光具有横波特性的直接结果，提供了一种无需偏振片即可产生偏振光的实用方法。理解布儒斯特角对于偏振太阳镜、摄影滤镜和表面性质分析等应用至关重要。本子知识点建立在[[Progressive Waves]]基础上，并与[[What is Polarisation]]和[[Polarisation by Filters (Malus's Law)]]相关联。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Brewster's Angle / 布儒斯特角

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
When unpolarised light strikes a non-metallic surface (e.g., glass, water), the reflected light is partially polarised. At a specific angle of incidence called **Brewster's angle** ($\theta_B$), the reflected light becomes **completely polarised** with its electric field vector oscillating **perpendicular to the plane of incidence** (i.e., parallel to the reflecting surface). The transmitted (refracted) light is partially polarised parallel to the plane of incidence.

The condition for Brewster's angle is that the reflected and refracted rays are **perpendicular** to each other. This leads to Brewster's law: $\tan \theta_B = n$, where $n$ is the refractive index of the reflecting medium (assuming the incident medium is air with $n=1$).

**中文:**
当非偏振光照射到非金属表面（如玻璃、水）时，反射光部分偏振。在特定入射角——**布儒斯特角** ($\theta_B$) 处，反射光**完全偏振**，其电场矢量在**垂直于入射面**的方向振动（即平行于反射面）。透射（折射）光则部分平行于入射面偏振。

布儒斯特角的条件是反射光线和折射光线**相互垂直**。由此得出布儒斯特定律：$\tan \theta_B = n$，其中 $n$ 是反射介质的折射率（假设入射介质为空气，$n=1$）。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
At Brewster's angle, the electric field component of the incident light that is parallel to the plane of incidence cannot be reflected because it would require oscillations parallel to the direction of propagation in the reflected ray — which is impossible for a transverse wave. Only the perpendicular component is reflected, resulting in complete polarisation.

**中文:**
在布儒斯特角处，入射光中平行于入射面的电场分量无法被反射，因为这将要求反射光线中的振动方向平行于传播方向——这对于横波是不可能的。只有垂直分量被反射，从而产生完全偏振。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** "Brewster's angle is the same as the critical angle." — No, critical angle is for total internal reflection; Brewster's angle is for polarisation by reflection.
- **中文:** "布儒斯特角等于临界角。" — 不对，临界角用于全内反射；布儒斯特角用于反射偏振。
- **English:** "All reflected light is polarised." — Only at Brewster's angle is it fully polarised; at other angles, it's partially polarised.
- **中文:** "所有反射光都是偏振的。" — 只有在布儒斯特角处才完全偏振；其他角度只是部分偏振。
- **English:** "Brewster's angle depends on the colour of light." — Yes, because refractive index varies with wavelength (dispersion).
- **中文:** "布儒斯特角取决于光的颜色。" — 是的，因为折射率随波长变化（色散）。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always state that reflected light is polarised **perpendicular to the plane of incidence**. Draw the ray diagram showing the reflected and refracted rays at 90° to each other.
- **中文:** 始终说明反射光**垂直于入射面**偏振。绘制显示反射光线和折射光线相互垂直的光路图。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Brewster's Law / 布儒斯特定律

$$ \tan \theta_B = n $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $\theta_B$ | Brewster's angle (angle of incidence for complete polarisation) | 布儒斯特角（完全偏振时的入射角） | degrees (°) |
| $n$ | Refractive index of the reflecting medium (relative to incident medium) | 反射介质的折射率（相对于入射介质） | dimensionless (无量纲) |

**Derivation / 推导:**
At Brewster's angle, the reflected and refracted rays are perpendicular: $\theta_B + \theta_r + 90^\circ = 180^\circ \Rightarrow \theta_r = 90^\circ - \theta_B$.

From Snell's law: $n_1 \sin \theta_B = n_2 \sin \theta_r$.

If $n_1 = 1$ (air) and $n_2 = n$: $\sin \theta_B = n \sin(90^\circ - \theta_B) = n \cos \theta_B$.

Therefore: $\tan \theta_B = n$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** The incident medium must be less optically dense than the reflecting medium (e.g., air to glass). The surface must be non-metallic (dielectric).
- **中文:** 入射介质的光密程度必须低于反射介质（如空气到玻璃）。表面必须是非金属的（电介质）。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Only applies to non-metallic surfaces. For metals, reflection behaviour is different due to free electrons.
- **中文:** 仅适用于非金属表面。对于金属，由于自由电子的存在，反射行为不同。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating Brewster's Angle / 计算布儒斯特角

### Question / 题目
**English:**
Light is incident from air onto a glass surface of refractive index $n = 1.50$. Calculate Brewster's angle for this interface. What is the angle of refraction at Brewster's angle?

**中文:**
光从空气入射到折射率 $n = 1.50$ 的玻璃表面。计算该界面的布儒斯特角。在布儒斯特角处，折射角是多少？

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Apply Brewster's law / 应用布儒斯特定律**

$$ \tan \theta_B = n = 1.50 $$

$$ \theta_B = \tan^{-1}(1.50) = 56.3^\circ $$

**Step 2: Find the angle of refraction / 求折射角**

At Brewster's angle, reflected and refracted rays are perpendicular:

$$ \theta_B + \theta_r = 90^\circ $$

$$ \theta_r = 90^\circ - 56.3^\circ = 33.7^\circ $$

**Alternative method using Snell's law / 使用斯涅尔定律的替代方法:**

$$ n_1 \sin \theta_B = n_2 \sin \theta_r $$

$$ 1 \times \sin 56.3^\circ = 1.50 \times \sin \theta_r $$

$$ \sin \theta_r = \frac{\sin 56.3^\circ}{1.50} = \frac{0.832}{1.50} = 0.555 $$

$$ \theta_r = \sin^{-1}(0.555) = 33.7^\circ $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $\theta_B = 56.3^\circ$, $\theta_r = 33.7^\circ$ | **答案：** $\theta_B = 56.3^\circ$，$\theta_r = 33.7^\circ$

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Remember that at Brewster's angle, $\theta_B + \theta_r = 90^\circ$. This is a quick check for your answer.
- **中文:** 记住在布儒斯特角处，$\theta_B + \theta_r = 90^\circ$。这是检查答案的快速方法。

## Example 2: Identifying Brewster's Angle from a Scenario / 从场景中识别布儒斯特角

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A photographer notices that when sunlight reflects off a calm lake at a certain angle, the glare is minimised when viewed through a polarising filter oriented horizontally. The refractive index of water is 1.33. Determine the angle of incidence at which the photographer is observing the reflection.

**中文:**
一位摄影师注意到，当阳光以特定角度从平静的湖面反射时，通过水平方向的偏振滤镜观察，眩光最小。水的折射率为1.33。确定摄影师观察反射时的入射角。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Understand the polarisation direction / 理解偏振方向**

The polarising filter is oriented horizontally to minimise glare. This means the reflected light is polarised **horizontally**, which is **perpendicular to the plane of incidence** (the plane is vertical). This confirms we are at Brewster's angle.

**Step 2: Apply Brewster's law / 应用布儒斯特定律**

$$ \tan \theta_B = n = 1.33 $$

$$ \theta_B = \tan^{-1}(1.33) = 53.1^\circ $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** The angle of incidence is $53.1^\circ$ (Brewster's angle for water). | **答案：** 入射角为 $53.1^\circ$（水的布儒斯特角）。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** In exam questions, "glare minimised by a polarising filter" is a strong hint that the reflection is at Brewster's angle.
- **中文:** 在考试题中，"通过偏振滤镜使眩光最小化"是反射处于布儒斯特角的强烈提示。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
This sub-topic connects to practical work in the following ways:

1. **Determining Brewster's angle experimentally:** Use a ray box, polarising filter, and a glass block. Rotate the glass block until the reflected light is completely extinguished by a polarising filter oriented perpendicular to the plane of incidence. Measure the angle of incidence — this is Brewster's angle.

2. **Measuring refractive index:** Once Brewster's angle is found, use $\tan \theta_B = n$ to calculate the refractive index of the material.

3. **Uncertainties:** The main uncertainty comes from judging when the reflected light is completely extinguished. Use a protractor with fine divisions (±0.5°) and repeat measurements.

4. **Graph plotting:** Plot degree of polarisation (qualitative) against angle of incidence to identify Brewster's angle.

**中文:**
本子知识点通过以下方式与实验考试联系：

1. **实验测定布儒斯特角：** 使用光具盘、偏振片和玻璃块。旋转玻璃块，直到通过垂直于入射面方向的偏振片观察时反射光完全消失。测量入射角——这就是布儒斯特角。

2. **测量折射率：** 找到布儒斯特角后，使用 $\tan \theta_B = n$ 计算材料的折射率。

3. **不确定度：** 主要不确定度来自判断反射光何时完全消失。使用精细分度的量角器（±0.5°）并重复测量。

4. **图表绘制：** 绘制偏振度（定性）与入射角的关系图，以识别布儒斯特角。
