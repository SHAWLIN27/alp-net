# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores the **Doppler Effect for Light**, specifically the phenomena of **redshift** and **blueshift**. Unlike sound, where the Doppler effect depends on the relative motion of source and observer through a medium, light's Doppler effect is governed by the principles of **special relativity** and the constancy of the speed of light. When a light source moves away from an observer, the observed wavelength increases (shifts towards the red end of the spectrum) — this is **redshift**. When it moves towards the observer, the wavelength decreases (shifts towards the blue end) — this is **blueshift**.

This concept is fundamental to modern astrophysics, allowing astronomers to determine the velocities of distant stars and galaxies, and providing key evidence for the **expanding universe**. It connects directly to the broader [[The Doppler Effect]] topic and builds on your understanding of [[Progressive Waves]].

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨**光的多普勒效应**，特别是**红移**和**蓝移**现象。与声音不同（声音的多普勒效应取决于声源和观察者相对于介质的运动），光的红移和蓝移受**狭义相对论**和光速不变原理的支配。当光源远离观察者时，观测到的波长增加（向光谱的红端移动）——这就是**红移**。当光源靠近观察者时，波长减小（向蓝端移动）——这就是**蓝移**。

这一概念是现代天体物理学的基础，使天文学家能够确定遥远恒星和星系的速度，并为**宇宙膨胀**提供了关键证据。它与更广泛的[[The Doppler Effect]]主题直接相关，并建立在您对[[Progressive Waves]]理解的基础上。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The Nature of Light's Doppler Effect / 光的多普勒效应的本质

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** Unlike sound, light does not require a medium to travel. The Doppler effect for light arises from the relativistic time dilation and length contraction effects described by **special relativity**. For a source moving at speed $v$ relative to an observer, the observed wavelength $\lambda_{\text{obs}}$ is related to the rest wavelength $\lambda_0$ by:

$$ \lambda_{\text{obs}} = \lambda_0 \sqrt{\frac{1 + \beta}{1 - \beta}} $$

where $\beta = v/c$. For non-relativistic speeds ($v \ll c$), this simplifies to the approximation used in A-Level:

$$ \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} \approx \frac{v}{c} $$

**中文:** 与声音不同，光不需要介质传播。光的多普勒效应源于**狭义相对论**中描述的时间膨胀和长度收缩效应。对于以速度 $v$ 相对于观察者运动的光源，观测波长 $\lambda_{\text{obs}}$ 与静止波长 $\lambda_0$ 的关系为：

$$ \lambda_{\text{obs}} = \lambda_0 \sqrt{\frac{1 + \beta}{1 - \beta}} $$

其中 $\beta = v/c$。对于非相对论速度（$v \ll c$），可简化为A-Level使用的近似公式：

$$ \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} \approx \frac{v}{c} $$

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** When a light source moves away, each successive wave crest is emitted from a slightly further distance, stretching the wavelength (redshift). When it moves towards, crests are emitted from closer distances, compressing the wavelength (blueshift). This is analogous to the sound Doppler effect, but the relativistic formula accounts for time dilation at high speeds.

**中文:** 当光源远离时，每个连续的波峰从稍远的距离发出，拉伸了波长（红移）。当光源靠近时，波峰从更近的距离发出，压缩了波长（蓝移）。这与声音的多普勒效应类似，但相对论公式考虑了高速下的时间膨胀。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **Misconception:** Redshift means the light actually becomes red. / **误区：** 红移意味着光真的变成红色。
  - **Truth:** Redshift is a shift in spectral lines towards longer wavelengths. A star moving away still emits the same spectrum, but all lines are shifted.
  - **事实：** 红移是光谱线向更长波长的移动。远离的恒星仍然发射相同的光谱，但所有谱线都发生了位移。
- **Misconception:** The formula $\frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda} = \frac{v}{c}$ is exact. / **误区：** 公式 $\frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda} = \frac{v}{c}$ 是精确的。
  - **Truth:** It's an approximation valid only when $v \ll c$.
  - **事实：** 这是一个近似公式，仅在 $v \ll c$ 时有效。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always state that redshift provides evidence for the **expanding universe**. Use the phrase "galaxies are moving away from each other" rather than "galaxies are moving away from Earth" to avoid implying Earth is the centre.
- **中文：** 务必说明红移为**宇宙膨胀**提供了证据。使用"星系彼此远离"而非"星系远离地球"，以避免暗示地球是中心。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## Equation 1: Doppler Shift for Light (Non-Relativistic Approximation)

$$ \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} = \frac{v}{c} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $\Delta \lambda$ | Change in wavelength ($\lambda_{\text{obs}} - \lambda_0$) | 波长变化量 ($\lambda_{\text{观测}} - \lambda_0$) | m (metres) |
| $\lambda_0$ | Rest wavelength (emitted wavelength) | 静止波长（发射波长） | m (metres) |
| $v$ | Relative speed of source (recessional velocity) | 光源的相对速度（退行速度） | m s$^{-1}$ |
| $c$ | Speed of light ($3.00 \times 10^8$ m s$^{-1}$) | 光速 ($3.00 \times 10^8$ m s$^{-1}$) | m s$^{-1}$ |

**Derivation / 推导:** This is an approximation derived from the full relativistic Doppler formula when $v \ll c$. The full formula is $\lambda_{\text{obs}} = \lambda_0 \sqrt{\frac{1 + v/c}{1 - v/c}}$. Using binomial expansion for small $v/c$ gives $\lambda_{\text{obs}} \approx \lambda_0 (1 + v/c)$, hence $\Delta \lambda / \lambda_0 \approx v/c$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Valid only when $v \ll c$ (non-relativistic speeds). For distant galaxies, $v$ is typically a few percent of $c$, so this approximation is acceptable.
- **中文：** 仅当 $v \ll c$（非相对论速度）时有效。对于遥远星系，$v$ 通常为光速的百分之几，因此该近似可以接受。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Fails for high-speed sources (e.g., quasars) where relativistic effects become significant. The full relativistic formula must be used.
- **中文：** 对于高速光源（如类星体）失效，此时相对论效应显著，必须使用完整的相对论公式。

## Equation 2: Redshift Parameter

$$ z = \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} = \frac{\lambda_{\text{obs}} - \lambda_0}{\lambda_0} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $z$ | Redshift parameter (dimensionless) | 红移参数（无量纲） | — |
| $\lambda_{\text{obs}}$ | Observed wavelength | 观测波长 | m |
| $\lambda_0$ | Rest wavelength | 静止波长 | m |

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** $z > 0$ indicates redshift (source moving away); $z < 0$ indicates blueshift (source moving towards).
- **中文：** $z > 0$ 表示红移（光源远离）；$z < 0$ 表示蓝移（光源靠近）。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating Recessional Velocity from Redshift / 从红移计算退行速度

### Question / 题目
**English:** A distant galaxy is observed to have a hydrogen spectral line at $\lambda_{\text{obs}} = 656.5$ nm. In the laboratory, the same line has a rest wavelength of $\lambda_0 = 656.3$ nm. Calculate the recessional velocity of the galaxy.

**中文:** 观测到一个遥远星系的氢光谱线在 $\lambda_{\text{obs}} = 656.5$ nm 处。在实验室中，同一条谱线的静止波长为 $\lambda_0 = 656.3$ nm。计算该星系的退行速度。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1:** Calculate the change in wavelength.
$$ \Delta \lambda = \lambda_{\text{obs}} - \lambda_0 = 656.5 - 656.3 = 0.2 \text{ nm} $$

**Step 2:** Calculate the redshift parameter.
$$ z = \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} = \frac{0.2}{656.3} = 3.05 \times 10^{-4} $$

**Step 3:** Use the Doppler shift formula.
$$ \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} = \frac{v}{c} \Rightarrow v = c \times \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} $$
$$ v = (3.00 \times 10^8) \times (3.05 \times 10^{-4}) = 9.15 \times 10^4 \text{ m s}^{-1} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $v = 9.15 \times 10^4$ m s$^{-1}$ (or 91.5 km s$^{-1}$) | **答案：** $v = 9.15 \times 10^4$ m s$^{-1}$（或 91.5 km s$^{-1}$）

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always check units! Wavelengths must be in the same units (both nm or both m). The ratio $\Delta \lambda / \lambda_0$ is dimensionless, so units cancel.
- **中文：** 务必检查单位！波长必须使用相同单位（均为 nm 或均为 m）。比值 $\Delta \lambda / \lambda_0$ 是无量纲的，因此单位会抵消。

## Example 2: Identifying Redshift or Blueshift / 识别红移或蓝移

### Question / 题目
**English:** A star is moving towards Earth at a speed of $2.0 \times 10^5$ m s$^{-1}$. A spectral line from the star has a rest wavelength of 500.0 nm. Calculate the observed wavelength and state whether this is a redshift or blueshift.

**中文:** 一颗恒星以 $2.0 \times 10^5$ m s$^{-1}$ 的速度向地球运动。该恒星的一条光谱线静止波长为 500.0 nm。计算观测波长，并说明这是红移还是蓝移。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1:** Since the star is moving towards Earth, the wavelength decreases (blueshift). $\Delta \lambda$ will be negative.

**Step 2:** Use the Doppler shift formula.
$$ \frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda_0} = \frac{v}{c} \Rightarrow \Delta \lambda = \lambda_0 \times \frac{v}{c} $$
$$ \Delta \lambda = 500.0 \times \frac{2.0 \times 10^5}{3.00 \times 10^8} = 500.0 \times 6.67 \times 10^{-4} = 0.333 \text{ nm} $$

**Step 3:** For a source moving towards, $\Delta \lambda$ is negative.
$$ \lambda_{\text{obs}} = \lambda_0 + \Delta \lambda = 500.0 - 0.333 = 499.667 \text{ nm} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $\lambda_{\text{obs}} = 499.7$ nm (3 s.f.). This is a **blueshift** because the observed wavelength is shorter than the rest wavelength. | **答案：** $\lambda_{\text{obs}} = 499.7$ nm（3位有效数字）。这是**蓝移**，因为观测波长比静止波长短。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Remember: **Towards = Blueshift** (shorter λ), **Away = Redshift** (longer λ). Use the mnemonic "TAB" — Towards Away Blueshift Redshift.
- **中文：** 记住：**靠近 = 蓝移**（波长变短），**远离 = 红移**（波长变长）。使用助记符"TAB"——Towards Away Blueshift Redshift。

| **Evidence / 证据** | Galactic redshift → galaxies receding → universe expanding (Hubble's Law). / 星系红移 → 星系退行 → 宇宙膨胀（哈勃定律）。 |
