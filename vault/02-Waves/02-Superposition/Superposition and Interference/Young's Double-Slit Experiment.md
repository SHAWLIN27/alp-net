# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Young's Double-Slit Experiment is the classic demonstration of wave interference, providing direct evidence for the wave nature of light. This experiment, first performed by Thomas Young in 1801, uses two coherent light sources created by passing monochromatic light through two narrow slits. The resulting interference pattern of alternating bright and dark fringes on a screen demonstrates [[Constructive and Destructive Interference]] in action. This sub-topic is central to understanding [[Superposition and Interference]] and forms the foundation for more advanced topics like [[Diffraction and the Diffraction Grating]]. The experiment allows us to calculate the wavelength of light using measurable quantities like slit separation, fringe spacing, and screen distance.

**中文:**
杨氏双缝实验是波动干涉的经典演示，为光的波动性提供了直接证据。该实验由托马斯·杨于1801年首次完成，利用通过两个狭缝的单色光产生两个相干光源。屏幕上明暗相间的干涉条纹展示了[[Constructive and Destructive Interference]]的实际应用。本子知识点是理解[[Superposition and Interference]]的核心，并为[[Diffraction and the Diffraction Grating]]等高级主题奠定基础。该实验使我们能够通过可测量的量（如狭缝间距、条纹间距和屏幕距离）来计算光的波长。

# 3. Core Definitions / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Coherent Sources** / 相干光源 | Two sources that emit waves of the same frequency with a constant phase difference | 发射相同频率且相位差恒定的波的两个光源 | Confusing coherence with monochromaticity; coherent sources must also have constant phase difference |
| **Fringe Spacing ($x$)** / 条纹间距 | The distance between the centers of two adjacent bright (or dark) fringes | 相邻两个明条纹（或暗条纹）中心之间的距离 | Measuring from bright to dark instead of bright to bright |
| **Slit Separation ($a$)** / 狭缝间距 | The distance between the centers of the two slits | 两个狭缝中心之间的距离 | Using slit width instead of slit separation |
| **Path Difference** / 光程差 | The difference in distance traveled by waves from two slits to a point on the screen | 从两个狭缝到屏幕上某点的波传播距离之差 | Forgetting that path difference determines whether interference is constructive or destructive |
| **Fringe** / 条纹 | A bright or dark band in the interference pattern | 干涉图案中的明或暗带 | Calling all fringes "bright" without considering destructive interference |
| **Monochromatic** / 单色光 | Light of a single wavelength (one color) | 单一波长（一种颜色）的光 | Assuming white light produces clear fringes (it produces colored fringes) |

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The Experimental Setup / 实验装置

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** Young's double-slit experiment uses a monochromatic light source (e.g., a laser or a lamp with a color filter) directed at a single slit. This single slit acts as a point source, ensuring that light spreads out via [[Diffraction and the Diffraction Grating|diffraction]]. The light then reaches two closely spaced parallel slits. These two slits act as [[Coherent Sources]] because they are illuminated by the same wavefront from the single slit. The light from the two slits overlaps on a screen, producing an interference pattern of alternating bright and dark fringes. The bright fringes occur where [[Constructive and Destructive Interference|constructive interference]] takes place, and dark fringes occur where destructive interference takes place.

**中文:** 杨氏双缝实验使用单色光源（如激光或带滤色镜的灯）照射单缝。这个单缝充当点光源，确保光通过[[Diffraction and the Diffraction Grating|衍射]]扩散。然后光到达两个紧密平行的狭缝。这两个狭缝充当[[Coherent Sources]]，因为它们被来自单缝的同一波前照亮。来自两个狭缝的光在屏幕上重叠，产生明暗相间的干涉条纹。明条纹出现在[[Constructive and Destructive Interference|相长干涉]]发生的地方，暗条纹出现在相消干涉发生的地方。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The experiment demonstrates that light behaves as a wave. The interference pattern cannot be explained by particle theory alone. The fringe spacing provides a direct measurement of the wavelength of light, which is typically on the order of $10^{-7}$ m for visible light.

**中文:** 该实验证明光具有波动性。干涉图案无法仅用粒子理论解释。条纹间距提供了光波长的直接测量，可见光的波长通常在$10^{-7}$ m量级。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Students often think the two slits themselves are the original sources. In reality, the single slit creates the coherent wavefront that illuminates both double slits.
- **中文:** 学生常认为两个狭缝本身就是原始光源。实际上，单缝产生相干波前，照亮两个双缝。
- **English:** Some believe the pattern is caused by light from each slit hitting the screen separately. The pattern only appears when light from both slits overlaps.
- **中文:** 有些人认为图案是由每个狭缝的光分别照射屏幕造成的。图案只有在两个狭缝的光重叠时才出现。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always mention that the single slit ensures coherence. Draw clear diagrams showing wavefronts from the single slit reaching both double slits simultaneously.
- **中文:** 务必提到单缝确保相干性。画出清晰的图表，显示来自单缝的波前同时到达两个双缝。

## 4.2 Conditions for Interference / 干涉条件

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** For a stable interference pattern to be observed, three conditions must be met:
1. **Coherent Sources:** The two sources must have a constant phase difference. In Young's experiment, this is achieved by using a single slit to illuminate both double slits.
2. **Same Frequency:** The waves from both sources must have the same frequency (monochromatic light).
3. **Overlapping Waves:** The waves must overlap in space for interference to occur.

**中文:** 要观察到稳定的干涉图案，必须满足三个条件：
1. **相干光源：** 两个光源必须具有恒定的相位差。在杨氏实验中，通过使用单缝照亮两个双缝来实现。
2. **相同频率：** 来自两个光源的波必须具有相同的频率（单色光）。
3. **波重叠：** 波必须在空间中重叠才能发生干涉。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** Without coherence, the phase difference between the two sources changes randomly over time, causing the interference pattern to shift rapidly and average out to a uniform intensity. This is why two separate light bulbs do not produce an interference pattern.

**中文:** 没有相干性，两个光源之间的相位差会随时间随机变化，导致干涉图案快速移动并平均为均匀强度。这就是为什么两个独立灯泡不会产生干涉图案。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Students think any two light sources can produce interference. They cannot unless they are coherent.
- **中文:** 学生认为任何两个光源都能产生干涉。除非它们是相干的，否则不能。
- **English:** Some believe the single slit is optional. It is essential for creating coherence.
- **中文:** 有些人认为单缝是可选的。它对产生相干性至关重要。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** When asked "Why is a single slit used?", the answer is always: "To create coherent sources by ensuring both double slits are illuminated by the same wavefront."
- **中文:** 当被问及"为什么使用单缝？"时，答案始终是："通过确保两个双缝被同一波前照亮来产生相干光源。"

## 4.3 Bright and Dark Fringe Formation / 明暗条纹的形成

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** The position of a point on the screen determines the [[Path Difference and Phase Difference|path difference]] between waves from the two slits.
- **Bright Fringe (Constructive Interference):** Path difference = $n\lambda$, where $n = 0, 1, 2, ...$ (integer). The central bright fringe (n=0) occurs where the path difference is zero.
- **Dark Fringe (Destructive Interference):** Path difference = $(n + \frac{1}{2})\lambda$, where $n = 0, 1, 2, ...$ (integer).

**中文:** 屏幕上某点的位置决定了来自两个狭缝的波之间的[[Path Difference and Phase Difference|光程差]]。
- **明条纹（相长干涉）：** 光程差 = $n\lambda$，其中 $n = 0, 1, 2, ...$（整数）。中央明条纹（n=0）出现在光程差为零的位置。
- **暗条纹（相消干涉）：** 光程差 = $(n + \frac{1}{2})\lambda$，其中 $n = 0, 1, 2, ...$（整数）。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The path difference determines whether the waves arrive in phase (constructive) or out of phase by half a wavelength (destructive). For small angles, the path difference is approximately $a \sin \theta$, where $\theta$ is the angle from the central axis.

**中文:** 光程差决定了波是同相到达（相长）还是相差半个波长（相消）。对于小角度，光程差近似为 $a \sin \theta$，其中 $\theta$ 是从中心轴测量的角度。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Students think the central fringe is always bright. This is true for Young's double-slit but not for all interference setups.
- **中文:** 学生认为中央条纹总是明亮的。这对于杨氏双缝是正确的，但并非对所有干涉装置都成立。
- **English:** Some confuse path difference with phase difference. Path difference is a distance; phase difference is an angle (in radians or degrees).
- **中文:** 有些人混淆光程差和相位差。光程差是距离；相位差是角度（弧度或度）。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Use the small-angle approximation: $\sin \theta \approx \tan \theta \approx \frac{y}{D}$, where $y$ is the distance from the central fringe.
- **中文:** 使用小角度近似：$\sin \theta \approx \tan \theta \approx \frac{y}{D}$，其中 $y$ 是距中央条纹的距离。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 The Double-Slit Equation / 双缝公式

$$ \lambda = \frac{ax}{D} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $\lambda$ | Wavelength of light | 光的波长 | m (meters) |
| $a$ | Slit separation (distance between centers of two slits) | 狭缝间距（两个狭缝中心之间的距离） | m (meters) |
| $x$ | Fringe spacing (distance between adjacent bright or dark fringes) | 条纹间距（相邻明条纹或暗条纹之间的距离） | m (meters) |
| $D$ | Distance from double slits to screen | 双缝到屏幕的距离 | m (meters) |

**Derivation / 推导:**
**English:** For small angles, $\sin \theta \approx \tan \theta \approx \frac{y}{D}$, where $y$ is the distance from the central maximum. For the first bright fringe (n=1), the path difference is $\lambda = a \sin \theta \approx a \frac{x}{D}$. Rearranging gives $\lambda = \frac{ax}{D}$.

**中文:** 对于小角度，$\sin \theta \approx \tan \theta \approx \frac{y}{D}$，其中 $y$ 是距中央最大值的距离。对于第一级明条纹（n=1），光程差为 $\lambda = a \sin \theta \approx a \frac{x}{D}$。整理得 $\lambda = \frac{ax}{D}$。

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** The equation is valid only when $D \gg a$ (screen is far from slits) and for small angles ($\theta < 10^\circ$). The slits must be narrow enough to cause diffraction but not so narrow that diffraction effects dominate.
- **中文:** 该公式仅在 $D \gg a$（屏幕远离狭缝）且小角度（$\theta < 10^\circ$）时有效。狭缝必须足够窄以产生衍射，但不能太窄以至于衍射效应占主导。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** The equation assumes ideal conditions: perfectly monochromatic light, infinitely narrow slits, and no other sources of interference. In practice, the pattern may be less sharp due to finite slit width and imperfect coherence.
- **中文:** 该公式假设理想条件：完全单色光、无限窄的狭缝、无其他干涉源。实际上，由于有限狭缝宽度和不完全相干性，图案可能不那么清晰。

## 5.2 Fringe Spacing Relationship / 条纹间距关系

$$ x = \frac{\lambda D}{a} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $x$ | Fringe spacing | 条纹间距 | m |
| $\lambda$ | Wavelength | 波长 | m |
| $D$ | Screen distance | 屏幕距离 | m |
| $a$ | Slit separation | 狭缝间距 | m |

**Derivation / 推导:**
**English:** Directly from $\lambda = \frac{ax}{D}$, rearranging gives $x = \frac{\lambda D}{a}$.

**中文:** 直接从 $\lambda = \frac{ax}{D}$ 整理得 $x = \frac{\lambda D}{a}$。

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Same as above: $D \gg a$ and small angles.
- **中文:** 同上：$D \gg a$ 且小角度。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Fringe spacing is constant for all fringes only under the small-angle approximation. For larger angles, the spacing increases slightly.
- **中文:** 仅在小角度近似下，所有条纹的间距才恒定。对于较大角度，间距会略微增加。

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 Intensity vs. Position on Screen / 强度与屏幕位置关系

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Position on screen (y) / 屏幕上的位置 (y)
- **Y-axis:** Intensity (I) / 强度 (I)

### Shape / 形状
**English:** The intensity pattern shows a series of equally spaced peaks (bright fringes) with approximately equal intensity. The intensity varies sinusoidally, following $I = I_0 \cos^2(\frac{\pi a y}{\lambda D})$, where $I_0$ is the maximum intensity.

**中文:** 强度图案显示一系列等间距的峰值（明条纹），强度大致相等。强度呈正弦变化，遵循 $I = I_0 \cos^2(\frac{\pi a y}{\lambda D})$，其中 $I_0$ 是最大强度。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
**English:** The gradient of the intensity curve at any point indicates how rapidly the intensity changes with position. At the center of bright fringes, the gradient is zero (maximum intensity). At the center of dark fringes, the gradient is also zero (minimum intensity).

**中文:** 强度曲线在任何点的斜率表示强度随位置变化的速率。在明条纹中心，斜率为零（最大强度）。在暗条纹中心，斜率也为零（最小强度）。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
**English:** The area under the intensity-position graph represents the total energy received per unit time per unit area at that region of the screen.

**中文:** 强度-位置图下的面积表示该屏幕区域每单位时间每单位面积接收的总能量。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
**English:** Be able to sketch the intensity pattern and label the positions of bright and dark fringes. Understand that the pattern is symmetric about the central maximum.

**中文:** 能够画出强度图案并标注明暗条纹的位置。理解图案关于中央最大值对称。

## 6.2 Fringe Spacing vs. Slit Separation / 条纹间距与狭缝间距关系

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Slit separation (a) / 狭缝间距 (a)
- **Y-axis:** Fringe spacing (x) / 条纹间距 (x)

### Shape / 形状
**English:** Inverse relationship: $x \propto \frac{1}{a}$. As slit separation increases, fringe spacing decreases. The graph is a hyperbola.

**中文:** 反比关系：$x \propto \frac{1}{a}$。随着狭缝间距增加，条纹间距减小。图形为双曲线。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
**English:** The gradient is negative and decreases in magnitude as $a$ increases. The product $ax$ is constant (equal to $\lambda D$).

**中文:** 斜率为负，且随着 $a$ 增加而减小。乘积 $ax$ 为常数（等于 $\lambda D$）。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
**English:** Not applicable for this relationship.

**中文:** 此关系不适用。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
**English:** If asked "How does fringe spacing change if slit separation is doubled?", answer: "Fringe spacing is halved."

**中文:** 如果被问"如果狭缝间距加倍，条纹间距如何变化？"，回答："条纹间距减半。"

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating Wavelength / 计算波长

### Question / 题目
**English:** In a Young's double-slit experiment, the slit separation is 0.50 mm. The distance from the slits to the screen is 2.0 m. The distance between the centers of the 1st and 5th bright fringes is measured as 8.0 mm. Calculate the wavelength of the light used.

**中文:** 在杨氏双缝实验中，狭缝间距为0.50 mm。从狭缝到屏幕的距离为2.0 m。第1和第5明条纹中心之间的距离测量为8.0 mm。计算所用光的波长。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify known quantities / 步骤1：确定已知量**
- Slit separation $a = 0.50 \text{ mm} = 0.50 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m} = 5.0 \times 10^{-4} \text{ m}$
- Screen distance $D = 2.0 \text{ m}$
- Distance between 1st and 5th bright fringes = 8.0 mm = $8.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m}$

**Step 2: Calculate fringe spacing / 步骤2：计算条纹间距**
- Number of fringe spacings between 1st and 5th bright fringes = 4
- Fringe spacing $x = \frac{8.0 \times 10^{-3}}{4} = 2.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m}$

**Step 3: Apply the double-slit equation / 步骤3：应用双缝公式**
$$ \lambda = \frac{ax}{D} = \frac{(5.0 \times 10^{-4})(2.0 \times 10^{-3})}{2.0} $$

**Step 4: Calculate / 步骤4：计算**
$$ \lambda = \frac{1.0 \times 10^{-6}}{2.0} = 5.0 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $\lambda = 5.0 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}$ (or 500 nm) | **答案：** $\lambda = 5.0 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}$（或500 nm）

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Always convert all distances to meters before using the formula. The fringe spacing $x$ is the distance between adjacent bright (or dark) fringes, not the distance between the first and last fringe.

**中文:** 在使用公式前，务必将所有距离转换为米。条纹间距 $x$ 是相邻明（或暗）条纹之间的距离，而不是第一个和最后一个条纹之间的距离。

## Example 2: Effect of Changing Parameters / 参数变化的影响

### Question / 题目
**English:** In a Young's double-slit experiment using red light of wavelength 650 nm, the fringe spacing is 3.0 mm. The red light is replaced with blue light of wavelength 450 nm. Calculate the new fringe spacing, assuming all other parameters remain the same.

**中文:** 在使用波长为650 nm的红光的杨氏双缝实验中，条纹间距为3.0 mm。将红光替换为波长为450 nm的蓝光。假设所有其他参数保持不变，计算新的条纹间距。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify known quantities / 步骤1：确定已知量**
- Initial wavelength $\lambda_1 = 650 \text{ nm} = 6.5 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}$
- New wavelength $\lambda_2 = 450 \text{ nm} = 4.5 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}$
- Initial fringe spacing $x_1 = 3.0 \text{ mm} = 3.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m}$

**Step 2: Use the relationship / 步骤2：使用关系式**
- Since $x \propto \lambda$ (from $x = \frac{\lambda D}{a}$, with $D$ and $a$ constant):
$$ \frac{x_2}{x_1} = \frac{\lambda_2}{\lambda_1} $$

**Step 3: Calculate new fringe spacing / 步骤3：计算新的条纹间距**
$$ x_2 = x_1 \times \frac{\lambda_2}{\lambda_1} = 3.0 \times 10^{-3} \times \frac{4.5 \times 10^{-7}}{6.5 \times 10^{-7}} $$

$$ x_2 = 3.0 \times 10^{-3} \times 0.692 = 2.08 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $x_2 = 2.1 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m}$ (or 2.1 mm) | **答案：** $x_2 = 2.1 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m}$（或2.1 mm）

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** When parameters change proportionally, use ratios to avoid recalculating constants. Blue light has a shorter wavelength than red light, so the fringe spacing decreases.

**中文:** 当参数成比例变化时，使用比例法避免重新计算常数。蓝光的波长比红光短，因此条纹间距减小。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
Young's double-slit experiment connects to practical skills in several ways:

1. **Measurements:** Students must measure small distances accurately using a ruler or vernier caliper. The fringe spacing $x$ is typically a few millimeters, requiring careful measurement. The slit separation $a$ is often given but may need to be measured using a traveling microscope.

2. **Uncertainties:** The uncertainty in $\lambda$ depends on the uncertainties in $a$, $x$, and $D$. The percentage uncertainty in $\lambda$ is the sum of percentage uncertainties in $a$, $x$, and $D$: $\frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda} = \frac{\Delta a}{a} + \frac{\Delta x}{x} + \frac{\Delta D}{D}$.

3. **Graph Plotting:** Plotting $x$ against $D$ gives a straight line through the origin with gradient $\frac{\lambda}{a}$, allowing $\lambda$ to be determined from the gradient.

4. **Experimental Design:** Key considerations include:
   - Using a laser for monochromatic, coherent light
   - Ensuring the screen is perpendicular to the light path
   - Measuring multiple fringe spacings to reduce percentage uncertainty
   - Using a dark room to improve visibility of fringes

**中文:**
杨氏双缝实验在多个方面与实验技能相关：

1. **测量：** 学生必须使用尺子或游标卡尺准确测量小距离。条纹间距 $x$ 通常为几毫米，需要仔细测量。狭缝间距 $a$ 通常给出，但可能需要使用读数显微镜测量。

2. **不确定度：** $\lambda$ 的不确定度取决于 $a$、$x$ 和 $D$ 的不确定度。$\lambda$ 的百分比不确定度是 $a$、$x$ 和 $D$ 的百分比不确定度之和：$\frac{\Delta \lambda}{\lambda} = \frac{\Delta a}{a} + \frac{\Delta x}{x} + \frac{\Delta D}{D}$。

3. **绘图：** 绘制 $x$ 对 $D$ 的图得到一条通过原点的直线，斜率为 $\frac{\lambda}{a}$，从而可以从斜率确定 $\lambda$。

4. **实验设计：** 关键考虑因素包括：
   - 使用激光作为单色相干光
   - 确保屏幕垂直于光路
   - 测量多个条纹间距以减少百分比不确定度
   - 使用暗室以提高条纹可见度

> 📋 **CIE Only:** In CAIE Paper 3 (Practical), students may be asked to set up the experiment and measure fringe spacing using a ruler and a lamp with a color filter.
> 
> 📋 **Edexcel Only:** In Edexcel IAL Unit 2, practical questions may focus on using a laser and measuring fringe spacing with a meter rule.

| **Key Formula / 核心公式** | $\lambda = \frac{ax}{D}$ or $x = \frac{\lambda D}{a}$ |
