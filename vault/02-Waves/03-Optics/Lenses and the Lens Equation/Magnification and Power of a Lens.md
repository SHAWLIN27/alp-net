# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores two key properties of lenses: **magnification** and **power**. Magnification describes how much larger (or smaller) an image appears compared to the object, while power quantifies a lens's ability to converge or diverge light. These concepts are essential for understanding optical instruments like microscopes, telescopes, and eyeglasses. Magnification is directly linked to the [[The Thin Lens Equation]] through object and image distances, while power is determined by the lens's focal length. Together, they provide a complete description of a lens's optical performance.

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨透镜的两个关键特性：**放大率** 和 **光焦度**。放大率描述了像相对于物体的大小变化，而光焦度则量化了透镜会聚或发散光线的能力。这些概念对于理解显微镜、望远镜和眼镜等光学仪器至关重要。放大率通过物距和像距与[[The Thin Lens Equation]]直接相关，而光焦度则由透镜的焦距决定。两者共同提供了透镜光学性能的完整描述。

# 3. Core Definitions / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Linear Magnification** / 线性放大率 | The ratio of the image height to the object height, or the ratio of image distance to object distance. | 像高与物高之比，或像距与物距之比。 | Confusing $m$ with angular magnification; forgetting sign conventions |
| **Power of a Lens** / 光焦度 | The reciprocal of the focal length measured in metres; quantifies the lens's ability to converge or diverge light. | 焦距（以米为单位）的倒数；量化透镜会聚或发散光线的能力。 | Using focal length in cm instead of m; forgetting units (dioptres) |
| **Dioptre (D)** / 屈光度 | The SI unit of lens power; 1 D = 1 m⁻¹. | 透镜光焦度的SI单位；1 D = 1 m⁻¹。 | Writing "D" as lowercase "d" |
| **Focal Length** / 焦距 | The distance from the optical centre to the principal focus. | 从光心到主焦点的距离。 | Forgetting sign: converging lenses have positive $f$, diverging have negative $f$ |
| **Real Image** / 实像 | An image formed where light rays actually converge; can be projected on a screen. | 光线实际会聚形成的像；可以投影在屏幕上。 | Thinking all real images are inverted (they are, but also magnified or diminished) |

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Linear Magnification / 线性放大率

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
Linear magnification $m$ tells us how many times larger (or smaller) the image is compared to the object. It can be calculated in two equivalent ways:

$$ m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = \frac{v}{u} $$

where $h_i$ is image height, $h_o$ is object height, $v$ is image distance, and $u$ is object distance. The sign of $m$ indicates orientation: positive $m$ means upright (virtual image), negative $m$ means inverted (real image). For a [[Converging and Diverging Lenses|converging lens]], when $u > f$, $m$ is negative (inverted real image); when $u < f$, $m > 1$ and positive (magnified virtual image).

**中文:**
线性放大率 $m$ 告诉我们像相对于物体放大了多少倍。它可以通过两种等效方式计算：

$$ m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = \frac{v}{u} $$

其中 $h_i$ 是像高，$h_o$ 是物高，$v$ 是像距，$u$ 是物距。$m$ 的符号表示方向：正 $m$ 表示正立（虚像），负 $m$ 表示倒立（实像）。对于[[Converging and Diverging Lenses|会聚透镜]]，当 $u > f$ 时，$m$ 为负（倒立实像）；当 $u < f$ 时，$m > 1$ 且为正（放大的虚像）。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
Magnification is a dimensionless ratio. $|m| > 1$ means the image is larger than the object; $|m| < 1$ means the image is smaller; $|m| = 1$ means same size. The sign tells us whether the image is upright or inverted relative to the object.

**中文:**
放大率是一个无量纲的比值。$|m| > 1$ 表示像比物体大；$|m| < 1$ 表示像比物体小；$|m| = 1$ 表示等大。符号告诉我们像是正立还是倒立。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking $m$ can only be calculated from heights, not distances
  - Forgetting that $m$ can be negative (inverted images)
  - Confusing linear magnification with angular magnification (used in telescopes)
- **中文:**
  - 认为 $m$ 只能通过高度计算，不能通过距离计算
  - 忘记 $m$ 可以是负数（倒立像）
  - 混淆线性放大率和角放大率（用于望远镜）

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always check units: $u$ and $v$ must be in the same units. Use sign conventions consistently. For past paper questions, if asked for "magnification" without specifying, they usually mean $|m|$.
- **中文:** 始终检查单位：$u$ 和 $v$ 必须使用相同单位。一致使用符号约定。在真题中，如果只问"放大率"而未特别说明，通常是指 $|m|$。

## 4.2 Power of a Lens / 光焦度

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The power $P$ of a lens is defined as:

$$ P = \frac{1}{f} $$

where $f$ is the focal length in **metres**. The unit of power is the **dioptre (D)**, where 1 D = 1 m⁻¹. A converging lens has positive power (since $f > 0$), while a diverging lens has negative power (since $f < 0$). The power tells us how strongly a lens bends light: a lens with $P = +5$ D converges light more strongly than one with $P = +2$ D.

**中文:**
透镜的光焦度 $P$ 定义为：

$$ P = \frac{1}{f} $$

其中 $f$ 是以**米**为单位的焦距。光焦度的单位是**屈光度 (D)**，其中 1 D = 1 m⁻¹。会聚透镜具有正光焦度（因为 $f > 0$），而发散透镜具有负光焦度（因为 $f < 0$）。光焦度告诉我们透镜弯曲光线的能力：$P = +5$ D 的透镜比 $P = +2$ D 的透镜会聚光线更强。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
A lens with higher power bends light more sharply, bringing rays to a focus in a shorter distance. For example, a +10 D lens has a focal length of 0.1 m (10 cm), while a +2 D lens has a focal length of 0.5 m (50 cm). In optometry, spectacle lenses are prescribed in dioptres.

**中文:**
光焦度越高的透镜弯曲光线越剧烈，使光线在更短的距离内会聚。例如，+10 D 的透镜焦距为 0.1 m（10 cm），而 +2 D 的透镜焦距为 0.5 m（50 cm）。在验光中，眼镜镜片以屈光度为单位开具处方。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见错误
- **English:**
  - Using focal length in cm instead of m (gives wrong power by factor of 100)
  - Forgetting that diverging lenses have negative power
  - Thinking power is always positive
- **中文:**
  - 使用厘米而非米作为焦距单位（导致光焦度相差100倍）
  - 忘记发散透镜具有负光焦度
  - 认为光焦度总是正的

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always convert $f$ to metres before calculating $P$. For combined lens systems (e.g., two lenses in contact), total power $P_{total} = P_1 + P_2$. This is a common exam question type.
- **中文:** 在计算 $P$ 之前，始终将 $f$ 转换为米。对于组合透镜系统（例如两个透镜接触），总光焦度 $P_{total} = P_1 + P_2$。这是常见的考试题型。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## Equation 1: Linear Magnification

$$ m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = \frac{v}{u} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $m$ | Linear magnification | 线性放大率 | dimensionless (无量纲) |
| $h_i$ | Image height | 像高 | m (or any length unit) |
| $h_o$ | Object height | 物高 | m (or any length unit) |
| $v$ | Image distance from lens | 像距 | m |
| $u$ | Object distance from lens | 物距 | m |

**Derivation / 推导:**
From similar triangles in ray diagrams: the ratio of image height to object height equals the ratio of image distance to object distance.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Valid for all thin lenses (converging and diverging). Sign conventions must be applied: $u$ is positive for real objects, $v$ is positive for real images and negative for virtual images.
- **中文:** 适用于所有薄透镜（会聚和发散）。必须应用符号约定：$u$ 对实物为正，$v$ 对实像为正，对虚像为负。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Only gives linear magnification, not angular magnification. Does not account for lens aberrations.
- **中文:** 仅给出线性放大率，而非角放大率。不考虑透镜像差。

## Equation 2: Power of a Lens

$$ P = \frac{1}{f} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $P$ | Power of lens | 光焦度 | D (dioptre / 屈光度) |
| $f$ | Focal length | 焦距 | m (metres / 米) |

**Derivation / 推导:**
Definition: power is the reciprocal of focal length. A lens with short focal length bends light more, hence has higher power.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** $f$ must be in metres. Sign: converging lens $f > 0$, $P > 0$; diverging lens $f < 0$, $P < 0$.
- **中文:** $f$ 必须以米为单位。符号：会聚透镜 $f > 0$, $P > 0$；发散透镜 $f < 0$, $P < 0$。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Assumes thin lens approximation. For thick lenses or lens systems, effective focal length is used.
- **中文:** 假设薄透镜近似。对于厚透镜或透镜系统，使用等效焦距。

## Equation 3: Combined Lens Power (in contact)

$$ P_{total} = P_1 + P_2 $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $P_{total}$ | Total power of combined lenses | 组合透镜总光焦度 | D |
| $P_1, P_2$ | Individual lens powers | 单个透镜光焦度 | D |

**Derivation / 推导:**
For thin lenses in contact, the combined focal length satisfies $\frac{1}{f_{total}} = \frac{1}{f_1} + \frac{1}{f_2}$, hence $P_{total} = P_1 + P_2$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Lenses must be thin and in contact (no air gap). Valid for both converging and diverging lenses.
- **中文:** 透镜必须是薄透镜且接触（无空气间隙）。适用于会聚和发散透镜。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Does not apply if lenses are separated by a distance.
- **中文:** 如果透镜之间有距离则不适用。

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 Magnification vs Object Distance (Converging Lens) / 放大率与物距关系（会聚透镜）

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Object distance $u$ (m) / 物距 $u$ (m)
- **Y-axis:** Magnification $m$ (dimensionless) / 放大率 $m$（无量纲）

### Shape / 形状
**English:** A hyperbolic curve. As $u$ increases from 0 to $f$, $m$ decreases from $+\infty$ to $+\infty$ (approaches infinity near $u=f$). At $u = f$, $m$ is undefined (image at infinity). For $u > f$, $m$ becomes negative (inverted image), starting from $-\infty$ at $u = f^+$ and approaching 0 as $u \to \infty$. At $u = 2f$, $m = -1$ (same size, inverted).

**中文:** 双曲线形状。当 $u$ 从 0 增加到 $f$ 时，$m$ 从 $+\infty$ 减小（在 $u=f$ 附近趋近无穷大）。在 $u = f$ 处，$m$ 未定义（像在无穷远）。当 $u > f$ 时，$m$ 变为负值（倒立像），从 $u = f^+$ 处的 $-\infty$ 开始，当 $u \to \infty$ 时趋近于 0。在 $u = 2f$ 处，$m = -1$（等大倒立）。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
**English:** The gradient $\frac{dm}{du}$ shows how rapidly magnification changes with object distance. Near $u = f$, the gradient is very steep (small changes in $u$ cause large changes in $m$).

**中文:** 梯度 $\frac{dm}{du}$ 表示放大率随物距变化的速率。在 $u = f$ 附近，梯度非常陡峭（$u$ 的微小变化导致 $m$ 的显著变化）。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
**English:** No direct physical meaning for area under this curve.

**中文:** 该曲线下的面积没有直接的物理意义。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **English:** Be able to read $m$ values from a graph for given $u$. Know that $m = -1$ at $u = 2f$ and $m = 0$ as $u \to \infty$.
- **中文:** 能够从图中读取给定 $u$ 对应的 $m$ 值。知道在 $u = 2f$ 处 $m = -1$，当 $u \to \infty$ 时 $m = 0$。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating Magnification / 计算放大率

### Question / 题目
**English:**
An object of height 2.0 cm is placed 15 cm from a converging lens of focal length 10 cm. Calculate:
(a) The image distance
(b) The linear magnification
(c) The image height

**中文:**
一个高度为 2.0 cm 的物体放置在焦距为 10 cm 的会聚透镜前 15 cm 处。计算：
(a) 像距
(b) 线性放大率
(c) 像高

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Use the thin lens equation**
$$ \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{u} + \frac{1}{v} $$
$$ \frac{1}{10} = \frac{1}{15} + \frac{1}{v} $$
$$ \frac{1}{v} = \frac{1}{10} - \frac{1}{15} = \frac{3 - 2}{30} = \frac{1}{30} $$
$$ v = 30 \text{ cm} $$

**Step 2: Calculate magnification**
$$ m = \frac{v}{u} = \frac{30}{15} = 2.0 $$

**Step 3: Calculate image height**
$$ m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} \implies h_i = m \times h_o = 2.0 \times 2.0 = 4.0 \text{ cm} $$

Since $v > 0$ and $m > 0$, the image is real and inverted (wait — $m$ is positive here, but $v > 0$ means real image, which is always inverted for a single lens). Actually, check: $m = v/u = 30/15 = +2$, but for a real image, $m$ should be negative. The sign convention: if we use $m = v/u$ without signs, we get $|m|$. The actual $m$ with sign: $m = -v/u = -2$ (since real image is inverted). So $h_i = |m| \times h_o = 4.0$ cm, and the image is inverted.

**Corrected:**
$$ m = -\frac{v}{u} = -\frac{30}{15} = -2.0 $$
$$ h_i = |m| \times h_o = 2.0 \times 2.0 = 4.0 \text{ cm} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) $v = 30$ cm (b) $m = -2.0$ (magnified, inverted) (c) $h_i = 4.0$ cm | **答案：** (a) $v = 30$ cm (b) $m = -2.0$（放大、倒立）(c) $h_i = 4.0$ cm

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always include the sign of $m$ when describing orientation. For real images from a single lens, $m$ is always negative.
- **中文:** 描述方向时始终包含 $m$ 的符号。对于单透镜的实像，$m$ 总是负的。

## Example 2: Lens Power Calculation / 光焦度计算

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A converging lens has a focal length of 25 cm. Calculate its power in dioptres. If this lens is placed in contact with a diverging lens of power -3.0 D, what is the total power and the combined focal length?

**中文:**
一个会聚透镜的焦距为 25 cm。计算其以屈光度为单位的光焦度。如果将该透镜与一个光焦度为 -3.0 D 的发散透镜接触放置，总光焦度和组合焦距是多少？

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Convert focal length to metres**
$$ f = 25 \text{ cm} = 0.25 \text{ m} $$

**Step 2: Calculate power of converging lens**
$$ P_1 = \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{0.25} = +4.0 \text{ D} $$

**Step 3: Calculate total power**
$$ P_{total} = P_1 + P_2 = +4.0 + (-3.0) = +1.0 \text{ D} $$

**Step 4: Calculate combined focal length**
$$ f_{total} = \frac{1}{P_{total}} = \frac{1}{1.0} = 1.0 \text{ m} = 100 \text{ cm} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $P_1 = +4.0$ D, $P_{total} = +1.0$ D, $f_{total} = 1.0$ m (100 cm) | **答案：** $P_1 = +4.0$ D, $P_{total} = +1.0$ D, $f_{total} = 1.0$ m (100 cm)

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always convert $f$ to metres before calculating $P$. For combined lenses, powers add algebraically (including signs).
- **中文:** 在计算 $P$ 之前，始终将 $f$ 转换为米。对于组合透镜，光焦度代数相加（包括符号）。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
This sub-topic connects to practical work in several ways:

1. **Measuring focal length:** Use the lens equation and magnification to determine $f$ experimentally. Place an object at known $u$, measure $v$ and image height, then calculate $f$ and $P$.

2. **Magnification measurement:** Use a screen to capture real images, measure $h_i$ with a ruler, and compare with $h_o$. Plot $m$ vs $u$ to verify the relationship.

3. **Uncertainties:** When measuring $u$ and $v$ with a metre rule (±1 mm), propagate uncertainties to find uncertainty in $m$ and $P$. For example, $\Delta m = m \left( \frac{\Delta u}{u} + \frac{\Delta v}{v} \right)$.

4. **Graph plotting:** Plot $1/v$ against $1/u$ to obtain a straight line with gradient -1 and intercept $1/f$. This is a common Paper 5/3 technique.

5. **Experimental design:** To find $P$ of an unknown lens, measure $f$ using the distant object method (object at infinity → image at focal plane) or the lens equation method.

**中文:**
本子知识点通过以下方式与实验工作联系：

1. **测量焦距：** 使用透镜方程和放大率通过实验确定 $f$。将物体放置在已知 $u$ 处，测量 $v$ 和像高，然后计算 $f$ 和 $P$。

2. **放大率测量：** 使用屏幕捕捉实像，用尺子测量 $h_i$，并与 $h_o$ 比较。绘制 $m$ 与 $u$ 的关系图以验证关系。

3. **不确定度：** 当使用米尺（±1 mm）测量 $u$ 和 $v$ 时，传播不确定度以找到 $m$ 和 $P$ 的不确定度。例如，$\Delta m = m \left( \frac{\Delta u}{u} + \frac{\Delta v}{v} \right)$。

4. **图表绘制：** 绘制 $1/v$ 与 $1/u$ 的关系图，得到斜率为 -1、截距为 $1/f$ 的直线。这是 Paper 5/3 的常用技巧。

5. **实验设计：** 要找到未知透镜的 $P$，使用远物法（物体在无穷远 → 像在焦平面）或透镜方程法测量 $f$。

# 12. Quick Revision Sheet / 速查表

| Category / 类别 | Key Points / 要点 |
|----------------|------------------|
| **Definition / 定义** | **Magnification:** $m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = \frac{v}{u}$ — ratio of image to object size. **Power:** $P = \frac{1}{f}$ — reciprocal of focal length in metres. |
| **Key Formula / 核心公式** | $m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = \frac{v}{u}$; $P = \frac{1}{f}$ (unit: D); $P_{total} = P_1 + P_2$ (lenses in contact) |
| **Sign Convention / 符号约定** | Converging lens: $f > 0$, $P > 0$; Diverging lens: $f < 0$, $P < 0$. Real image: $v > 0$, $m < 0$; Virtual image: $v < 0$, $m > 0$ |
| **Key Graph / 核心图表** | $m$ vs $u$: hyperbolic, vertical asymptote at $u = f$, $m = -1$ at $u = 2f$ |
| **Common Mistake / 常见错误** | Using $f$ in cm for power (must be m); forgetting sign of $m$ for real/virtual images; thinking diverging lenses have positive power |
| **Exam Tip / 考试提示** | Always convert cm → m for $P$; use $m = -v/u$ for sign-correct magnification; combined power adds algebraically |
| **Practical Link / 实验联系** | Measure $u$ and $v$ to find $f$ and $P$; plot $1/v$ vs $1/u$ to find $f$ from intercept; propagate uncertainties in $m$ and $P$ |
| **Real-World Application / 实际应用** | Eyeglass prescriptions in dioptres; microscope and telescope magnification; camera lens zoom |
