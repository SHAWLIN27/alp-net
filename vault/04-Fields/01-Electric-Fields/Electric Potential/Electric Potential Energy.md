# Electric Potential Energy / 电势能

---

# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Electric potential energy is the energy stored in a system of charged particles due to their positions in an electric field. This sub-topic explores how work is done to move charges against electric forces, converting this work into stored potential energy. Understanding electric potential energy is crucial for linking [[Electric Fields and Coulomb's Law]] to [[Electric Potential (V)]] and for analyzing the [[Motion of Charged Particles in Electric Fields]]. It forms the foundation for understanding [[Capacitance and Capacitors]] and parallels [[Gravitational Potential]] energy concepts.

**中文:**
电势能是带电粒子系统由于在电场中的位置而储存的能量。本子知识点探讨如何通过克服电场力对电荷做功，将功转化为储存的电势能。理解电势能对于将[[Electric Fields and Coulomb's Law]]与[[Electric Potential (V)]]联系起来，以及分析[[Motion of Charged Particles in Electric Fields]]至关重要。它构成了理解[[Capacitance and Capacitors]]的基础，并与[[Gravitational Potential]]能量概念相平行。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Work and Potential Energy / 功与电势能

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
When an external force moves a positive charge $q$ from point A to point B in an electric field, work must be done against the electric force. This work is stored as electric potential energy. The relationship is:

$$W_{\text{external}} = \Delta E_p = E_{pB} - E_{pA}$$

If the field does work on the charge (moving in direction of field for positive charge), potential energy decreases. This is analogous to [[Gravitational Potential]] energy where lifting a mass increases gravitational potential energy.

**中文:**
当外力在电场中将正电荷 $q$ 从A点移动到B点时，必须克服电场力做功。这个功被储存为电势能。关系为：

$$W_{\text{external}} = \Delta E_p = E_{pB} - E_{pA}$$

如果电场对电荷做功（正电荷沿电场方向移动），电势能减少。这类似于[[Gravitational Potential]]能量，其中提升质量会增加重力势能。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
Electric potential energy represents the capacity of a charge-field system to do work. A charge at high potential relative to another point has stored energy that can be converted to kinetic energy if released. This is the basis for understanding [[Motion of Charged Particles in Electric Fields]].

**中文:**
电势能代表电荷-场系统做功的能力。相对于另一点处于高电势的电荷具有储存的能量，如果释放可以转化为动能。这是理解[[Motion of Charged Particles in Electric Fields]]的基础。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking potential energy belongs to a single charge (it belongs to the system)
  - Confusing $E_p = qV$ with $E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$ (first is general, second is for point charges)
  - Forgetting that potential energy can be negative (attractive systems)
- **中文:**
  - 认为电势能属于单个电荷（它属于系统）
  - 混淆 $E_p = qV$ 与 $E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$（前者通用，后者用于点电荷）
  - 忘记电势能可以为负（吸引系统）

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always define your zero reference point. For point charges, zero is at infinity. For uniform fields, zero is often at the negative plate.
- **中文:** 始终定义你的零参考点。对于点电荷，零点在无穷远处。对于匀强电场，零点通常在负极板。

## 4.2 Potential Energy for Point Charges / 点电荷的电势能

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
For two point charges $Q$ (source) and $q$ (test charge) separated by distance $r$, the electric potential energy is:

$$E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$$

This is derived from integrating Coulomb's force over distance from infinity to $r$. The sign of $E_p$ depends on the product $Qq$:
- **Like charges** ($Qq > 0$): $E_p > 0$ (repulsive, energy stored)
- **Unlike charges** ($Qq < 0$): $E_p < 0$ (attractive, energy released)

**中文:**
对于相距 $r$ 的两个点电荷 $Q$（源电荷）和 $q$（试探电荷），电势能为：

$$E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$$

这是通过对库仑力从无穷远到 $r$ 的距离积分推导得出的。$E_p$ 的符号取决于乘积 $Qq$：
- **同种电荷**（$Qq > 0$）：$E_p > 0$（排斥，储存能量）
- **异种电荷**（$Qq < 0$）：$E_p < 0$（吸引，释放能量）

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The sign indicates whether work must be done to assemble the system (positive $E_p$) or whether energy is released when assembling (negative $E_p$). For like charges, bringing them together requires work against repulsion. For unlike charges, they naturally attract, releasing energy.

**中文:**
符号表示组装系统是否需要做功（正 $E_p$）还是释放能量（负 $E_p$）。对于同种电荷，将它们靠近需要克服排斥力做功。对于异种电荷，它们自然吸引，释放能量。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking $E_p$ is always positive
  - Forgetting the $4\pi\epsilon_0$ in the denominator
  - Confusing $E_p$ with electric potential $V = \frac{Q}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$
- **中文:**
  - 认为 $E_p$ 总是正的
  - 忘记分母中的 $4\pi\epsilon_0$
  - 混淆 $E_p$ 与电势 $V = \frac{Q}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** When calculating work done to bring a charge from infinity to distance $r$, use $W = E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$. Remember the sign!
- **中文:** 计算将电荷从无穷远移动到距离 $r$ 所做的功时，使用 $W = E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$。记住符号！

## 4.3 Potential Energy in Uniform Fields / 匀强电场中的电势能

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
In a uniform electric field $E$ between parallel plates separated by distance $d$, the potential energy of charge $q$ at position $x$ from the negative plate is:

$$E_p = qEx$$

Or equivalently, using potential difference $V$:

$$E_p = qV$$

where $V$ is the potential at that point relative to the negative plate (zero potential). The change in potential energy when moving through $\Delta V$ is:

$$\Delta E_p = q\Delta V$$

**中文:**
在相距 $d$ 的平行板之间的匀强电场 $E$ 中，电荷 $q$ 在距负极板 $x$ 处的电势能为：

$$E_p = qEx$$

或者等效地，使用电势差 $V$：

$$E_p = qV$$

其中 $V$ 是该点相对于负极板（零电势）的电势。通过 $\Delta V$ 移动时电势能的变化为：

$$\Delta E_p = q\Delta V$$

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
This is analogous to gravitational potential energy $mgh$ in a uniform gravitational field. The electric field $E$ is like $g$, charge $q$ is like mass $m$, and distance $x$ is like height $h$.

**中文:**
这类似于匀强重力场中的重力势能 $mgh$。电场 $E$ 类似于 $g$，电荷 $q$ 类似于质量 $m$，距离 $x$ 类似于高度 $h$。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Using $E_p = qV$ when $V$ is absolute potential, not potential difference
  - Forgetting that $E_p = qEx$ only works for uniform fields
- **中文:**
  - 当 $V$ 是绝对电势而非电势差时使用 $E_p = qV$
  - 忘记 $E_p = qEx$ 仅适用于匀强电场

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** For uniform fields, always identify your zero potential reference point first. The negative plate is commonly taken as zero.
- **中文:** 对于匀强电场，始终先确定零电势参考点。通常将负极板作为零点。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## Equation 1: Point Charge Potential Energy / 点电荷电势能

$$E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $E_p$ | Electric potential energy | 电势能 | J (Joules) |
| $Q$ | Source charge | 源电荷 | C (Coulombs) |
| $q$ | Test charge | 试探电荷 | C |
| $r$ | Separation distance | 间距 | m |
| $\epsilon_0$ | Permittivity of free space | 真空介电常数 | F m$^{-1}$ |

**Derivation / 推导:**
$$W = \int_{\infty}^{r} F \, dr = \int_{\infty}^{r} \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r^2} \, dr = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \left[-\frac{1}{r}\right]_{\infty}^{r} = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$$

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Point charges in vacuum. Zero potential energy at infinity.
- **中文:** 真空中的点电荷。零电势能在无穷远处。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Does not account for induced charges or polarization. Assumes charges are stationary point particles.
- **中文:** 不考虑感应电荷或极化。假设电荷是静止的点粒子。

## Equation 2: Potential Energy Change / 电势能变化

$$\Delta E_p = q\Delta V$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $\Delta E_p$ | Change in potential energy | 电势能变化 | J |
| $q$ | Charge moved | 移动的电荷 | C |
| $\Delta V$ | Potential difference | 电势差 | V |

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Any electric field (uniform or non-uniform). $\Delta V$ is the potential difference between two points.
- **中文:** 任何电场（匀强或非匀强）。$\Delta V$ 是两点间的电势差。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Only gives change, not absolute value. Need reference point for absolute $E_p$.
- **中文:** 仅给出变化量，非绝对值。需要参考点才能得到绝对 $E_p$。

## Equation 3: Uniform Field Potential Energy / 匀强电场电势能

$$E_p = qEx$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $E$ | Electric field strength | 电场强度 | N C$^{-1}$ or V m$^{-1}$ |
| $x$ | Distance from reference plate | 距参考极板的距离 | m |

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Uniform electric field only. $x$ measured from zero potential plate.
- **中文:** 仅适用于匀强电场。$x$ 从零电势极板开始测量。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Point Charge Potential Energy / 点电荷电势能

### Question / 题目
**English:**
Two point charges $Q = +3.0 \times 10^{-6}$ C and $q = -2.0 \times 10^{-6}$ C are separated by a distance of $0.15$ m in vacuum. ($\epsilon_0 = 8.85 \times 10^{-12}$ F m$^{-1}$)

(a) Calculate the electric potential energy of the system.
(b) If the charges are moved to a separation of $0.30$ m, what is the change in potential energy? Is work done by the field or against the field?

**中文:**
两个点电荷 $Q = +3.0 \times 10^{-6}$ C 和 $q = -2.0 \times 10^{-6}$ C 在真空中相距 $0.15$ m。（$\epsilon_0 = 8.85 \times 10^{-12}$ F m$^{-1}$）

(a) 计算系统的电势能。
(b) 如果电荷间距变为 $0.30$ m，电势能的变化是多少？是电场做功还是克服电场做功？

### Solution / 解答

**Part (a):**

$$E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$$

$$E_p = \frac{(3.0 \times 10^{-6})(-2.0 \times 10^{-6})}{4\pi(8.85 \times 10^{-12})(0.15)}$$

$$E_p = \frac{-6.0 \times 10^{-12}}{4\pi(8.85 \times 10^{-12})(0.15)}$$

$$E_p = \frac{-6.0 \times 10^{-12}}{1.668 \times 10^{-11}}$$

$$E_p = -0.360 \text{ J}$$

**Part (b):**

At $r = 0.30$ m:

$$E_{p2} = \frac{-6.0 \times 10^{-12}}{4\pi(8.85 \times 10^{-12})(0.30)}$$

$$E_{p2} = \frac{-6.0 \times 10^{-12}}{3.336 \times 10^{-11}}$$

$$E_{p2} = -0.180 \text{ J}$$

Change in potential energy:

$$\Delta E_p = E_{p2} - E_{p1} = (-0.180) - (-0.360) = +0.180 \text{ J}$$

Since $\Delta E_p > 0$, work is done **against** the field (external work required to separate unlike charges).

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:**
(a) $E_p = -0.36$ J
(b) $\Delta E_p = +0.18$ J, work done against the field

**答案：**
(a) $E_p = -0.36$ J
(b) $\Delta E_p = +0.18$ J，克服电场做功

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** For unlike charges, $E_p$ is negative. Separating them (increasing $r$) makes $E_p$ less negative (increases), meaning work must be done against the attractive force.
- **中文:** 对于异种电荷，$E_p$ 为负。分离它们（增加 $r$）使 $E_p$ 负值减小（增加），意味着必须克服吸引力做功。

## Example 2: Energy Conservation in Uniform Field / 匀强电场中的能量守恒

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A proton ($q = +1.6 \times 10^{-19}$ C, $m = 1.67 \times 10^{-27}$ kg) is released from rest at the positive plate of a parallel plate capacitor. The plates are separated by $0.050$ m and the potential difference between them is $2000$ V.

(a) Calculate the change in electric potential energy as the proton moves to the negative plate.
(b) Determine the speed of the proton just before it hits the negative plate.

**中文:**
一个质子（$q = +1.6 \times 10^{-19}$ C，$m = 1.67 \times 10^{-27}$ kg）从平行板电容器的正极板静止释放。极板间距为 $0.050$ m，电势差为 $2000$ V。

(a) 计算质子移动到负极板时电势能的变化。
(b) 确定质子即将撞击负极板前的速度。

### Solution / 解答

**Part (a):**

The proton moves from positive plate (high potential) to negative plate (low potential).

$$\Delta V = V_{\text{final}} - V_{\text{initial}} = 0 - 2000 = -2000 \text{ V}$$

$$\Delta E_p = q\Delta V = (1.6 \times 10^{-19})(-2000)$$

$$\Delta E_p = -3.2 \times 10^{-16} \text{ J}$$

The negative sign means potential energy decreases (converted to kinetic energy).

**Part (b):**

By conservation of energy:

$$\Delta E_k + \Delta E_p = 0$$

$$\Delta E_k = -\Delta E_p = 3.2 \times 10^{-16} \text{ J}$$

Since proton starts from rest ($v_i = 0$):

$$\frac{1}{2}mv^2 = 3.2 \times 10^{-16}$$

$$v = \sqrt{\frac{2 \times 3.2 \times 10^{-16}}{1.67 \times 10^{-27}}}$$

$$v = \sqrt{3.83 \times 10^{11}}$$

$$v = 6.19 \times 10^5 \text{ m s}^{-1}$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:**
(a) $\Delta E_p = -3.2 \times 10^{-16}$ J
(b) $v = 6.19 \times 10^5$ m s$^{-1}$

**答案：**
(a) $\Delta E_p = -3.2 \times 10^{-16}$ J
(b) $v = 6.19 \times 10^5$ m s$^{-1}$

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** In energy conservation problems, always check the sign of $\Delta E_p$. A decrease in potential energy ($\Delta E_p < 0$) means an increase in kinetic energy.
- **中文:** 在能量守恒问题中，始终检查 $\Delta E_p$ 的符号。电势能减少（$\Delta E_p < 0$）意味着动能增加。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
While electric potential energy is not directly measured in practical work, it connects to several practical skills:

1. **Measurements:** Measuring potential differences with voltmeters to calculate $\Delta E_p = q\Delta V$
2. **Uncertainties:** Propagating uncertainties when calculating $E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$ from measured $Q$, $q$, and $r$
3. **Graph Plotting:** Plotting $E_p$ vs $r$ for point charges (inverse relationship) and $E_p$ vs $x$ for uniform fields (linear relationship)
4. **Experimental Design:** Using charged particles in electric fields to demonstrate energy conservation (e.g., Millikan's oil drop experiment, deflection tubes)
5. **Data Analysis:** Determining electric field strength from gradient of $E_p$ vs $x$ graph

**中文:**
虽然电势能不直接在实验工作中测量，但它与多项实验技能相关：

1. **测量：** 使用电压表测量电势差以计算 $\Delta E_p = q\Delta V$
2. **不确定度：** 从测量的 $Q$、$q$ 和 $r$ 计算 $E_p = \frac{Qq}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r}$ 时传播不确定度
3. **图表绘制：** 绘制点电荷的 $E_p$-$r$ 图（反比关系）和匀强电场的 $E_p$-$x$ 图（线性关系）
4. **实验设计：** 使用电场中的带电粒子演示能量守恒（例如密立根油滴实验、偏转管）
5. **数据分析：** 从 $E_p$-$x$ 图的斜率确定电场强度
