# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic covers the three fundamental gas laws that describe how pressure, volume, and temperature of a fixed mass of gas are related: **Boyle's Law** (pressure-volume relationship at constant temperature), **Charles's Law** (volume-temperature relationship at constant pressure), and the **Pressure Law** (pressure-temperature relationship at constant volume). These laws form the empirical foundation of the [[Ideal Gases]] model and are essential prerequisites for understanding the [[Ideal Gas Equation (pV = nRT)]]. They also connect directly to [[Kinetic Theory of Gases]] by providing experimental evidence for molecular motion. Mastery of these laws is critical for AS-level exams, as they appear in both multiple-choice and structured questions, often requiring graphical analysis and calculation.

**中文:**
本子知识点涵盖三大基本气体定律，描述固定质量气体的压强、体积和温度之间的关系：**玻意耳定律**（恒温下的压强-体积关系）、**查理定律**（恒压下的体积-温度关系）和**压强定律**（恒容下的压强-温度关系）。这些定律构成了[[Ideal Gases]]模型的实验基础，是理解[[Ideal Gas Equation (pV = nRT)]]的重要前提。它们还通过提供分子运动的实验证据与[[Kinetic Theory of Gases]]直接关联。掌握这些定律对AS级别考试至关重要，常见于选择题和结构题中，通常需要图形分析和计算。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Boyle's Law — Pressure-Volume Relationship / 玻意耳定律 — 压强-体积关系

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
Boyle's Law states that for a fixed mass of gas at constant temperature, $p \propto \frac{1}{V}$, or equivalently $pV = \text{constant}$. This means if you double the pressure, the volume halves, provided temperature remains unchanged. The law is named after [[Robert Boyle]] who published it in 1662. It can be derived from the [[Kinetic Theory of Gases]] by considering that at constant temperature, the average kinetic energy of molecules is constant; increasing volume reduces the frequency of collisions with walls, thus reducing pressure.

**中文:**
玻意耳定律指出，对于固定质量的气体，在温度不变时，$p \propto \frac{1}{V}$，即 $pV = \text{常数}$。这意味着如果压强加倍，体积减半，前提是温度保持不变。该定律以[[Robert Boyle]]命名，他于1662年发表。可以从[[Kinetic Theory of Gases]]推导：在恒温下，分子的平均动能不变；增加体积会减少与器壁的碰撞频率，从而降低压强。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** At constant temperature, gas molecules have constant average kinetic energy. When volume decreases, molecules have less space to move, so they hit the walls more frequently, increasing pressure. The product pV represents the "internal energy" of the gas at constant temperature.

**中文:** 在恒温下，气体分子的平均动能不变。当体积减小时，分子运动空间变小，与器壁碰撞更频繁，压强增大。乘积pV代表恒温下气体的"内能"。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking pV = constant means p and V are directly proportional (they are inversely proportional).
- **CN:** 认为pV = 常数意味着p和V成正比（实际成反比）。
- **EN:** Forgetting that temperature must be constant — if temperature changes, Boyle's Law does not apply.
- **CN:** 忘记温度必须恒定——如果温度变化，玻意耳定律不适用。
- **EN:** Confusing with the [[Ideal Gas Equation (pV = nRT)]] — Boyle's Law is a special case where n and T are constant.
- **CN:** 与[[Ideal Gas Equation (pV = nRT)]]混淆——玻意耳定律是n和T恒定的特例。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** Always check units: pressure in Pa, volume in m³ (or consistent units). Convert cm³ to m³ (×10⁻⁶).
- **CN:** 始终检查单位：压强用Pa，体积用m³（或一致单位）。将cm³转换为m³（×10⁻⁶）。
- **EN:** For graphical questions, a p-V graph is a hyperbola; a p-1/V graph is a straight line through origin.
- **CN:** 对于图形题，p-V图是双曲线；p-1/V图是通过原点的直线。

## 4.2 Charles's Law — Volume-Temperature Relationship / 查理定律 — 体积-温度关系

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
Charles's Law states that for a fixed mass of gas at constant pressure, $V \propto T$, where T is the absolute temperature in Kelvin. Equivalently, $\frac{V}{T} = \text{constant}$. This means if you double the Kelvin temperature, the volume doubles, provided pressure remains constant. The law was formulated by [[Jacques Charles]] around 1787. It explains why hot air balloons rise — heating the air increases its volume, reducing density.

**中文:**
查理定律指出，对于固定质量的气体，在压强不变时，$V \propto T$，其中T是开尔文绝对温度。即$\frac{V}{T} = \text{常数}$。这意味着如果开尔文温度加倍，体积加倍，前提是压强保持不变。该定律由[[Jacques Charles]]于1787年左右提出。它解释了热气球上升的原因——加热空气增加体积，降低密度。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** At constant pressure, increasing temperature gives gas molecules more kinetic energy. To keep pressure constant, the volume must increase so molecules hit the walls less frequently, compensating for their higher speed.

**中文:** 在恒压下，升高温度使气体分子获得更多动能。为保持压强不变，体积必须增大，使分子与器壁碰撞频率降低，以补偿其更高的速度。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Using Celsius temperature directly in the proportion — must convert to Kelvin first.
- **CN:** 直接在比例中使用摄氏温度——必须先转换为开尔文。
- **EN:** Thinking the graph of V vs T (Celsius) passes through origin — it intercepts the T-axis at −273.15°C.
- **CN:** 认为V对T（摄氏）的图通过原点——它在T轴上截距为−273.15°C。
- **EN:** Forgetting that pressure must be constant — if pressure changes, Charles's Law does not apply.
- **CN:** 忘记压强必须恒定——如果压强变化，查理定律不适用。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** Always convert °C to K: $T(K) = T(°C) + 273.15$. For A-Level, using 273 is usually acceptable.
- **CN:** 始终将°C转换为K：$T(K) = T(°C) + 273.15$。A-Level考试中，使用273通常可接受。
- **EN:** The V-T graph (Kelvin) is a straight line through origin — this proves absolute zero exists.
- **CN:** V-T图（开尔文）是通过原点的直线——这证明了绝对零度的存在。

## 4.3 Pressure Law — Pressure-Temperature Relationship / 压强定律 — 压强-温度关系

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The Pressure Law states that for a fixed mass of gas at constant volume, $p \propto T$, where T is the absolute temperature in Kelvin. Equivalently, $\frac{p}{T} = \text{constant}$. This means if you double the Kelvin temperature, the pressure doubles, provided volume remains constant. It is also known as **Gay-Lussac's Law** after [[Joseph Louis Gay-Lussac]] who published it in 1802. This law explains why car tire pressures increase after long drives — friction heats the tires, increasing pressure.

**中文:**
压强定律指出，对于固定质量的气体，在体积不变时，$p \propto T$，其中T是开尔文绝对温度。即$\frac{p}{T} = \text{常数}$。这意味着如果开尔文温度加倍，压强加倍，前提是体积保持不变。它也被称为**盖-吕萨克定律**，以[[Joseph Louis Gay-Lussac]]命名，他于1802年发表。该定律解释了为什么长途驾驶后汽车轮胎压强增加——摩擦加热轮胎，增加压强。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** At constant volume, increasing temperature gives gas molecules more kinetic energy. They move faster and hit the walls more frequently and with greater force, increasing pressure.

**中文:** 在恒容下，升高温度使气体分子获得更多动能。它们运动更快，与器壁碰撞更频繁且力度更大，从而增加压强。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking the p-T graph (Celsius) passes through origin — it intercepts at −273.15°C.
- **CN:** 认为p-T图（摄氏）通过原点——它在−273.15°C处有截距。
- **EN:** Confusing the Pressure Law with Charles's Law — remember which quantity is constant.
- **CN:** 混淆压强定律和查理定律——记住哪个量是恒定的。
- **EN:** Forgetting that volume must be constant — if volume changes, the Pressure Law does not apply.
- **CN:** 忘记体积必须恒定——如果体积变化，压强定律不适用。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** The p-T graph (Kelvin) is a straight line through origin — extrapolating to p=0 gives absolute zero.
- **CN:** p-T图（开尔文）是通过原点的直线——外推到p=0得到绝对零度。
- **EN:** For safety valve problems, use the Pressure Law to find maximum safe temperature.
- **CN:** 对于安全阀问题，使用压强定律找到最高安全温度。

## 4.4 Absolute Zero and the Kelvin Scale / 绝对零度与开尔文温标

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
Absolute zero (0 K = −273.15°C) is the theoretical temperature at which gas molecules have minimum kinetic energy (zero, in the classical model). Experimentally, it is found by extrapolating the V-T or p-T graphs to zero volume or zero pressure. The Kelvin scale is essential because all gas laws require absolute temperature — using Celsius would give incorrect proportionalities. The conversion is $T(K) = \theta(°C) + 273.15$.

**中文:**
绝对零度（0 K = −273.15°C）是气体分子具有最小动能（经典模型中为零）的理论温度。实验上，通过将V-T或p-T图外推到体积或压强为零来获得。开尔文温标至关重要，因为所有气体定律都需要绝对温度——使用摄氏温度会得到错误的比例关系。转换公式为 $T(K) = \theta(°C) + 273.15$。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** At absolute zero, all molecular motion (translational kinetic energy) would cease. In reality, quantum mechanics shows zero-point energy remains, but the classical gas model predicts zero volume/pressure.

**中文:** 在绝对零度时，所有分子运动（平动动能）将停止。实际上，量子力学显示零点能仍然存在，但经典气体模型预测体积/压强为零。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking absolute zero is −273°C exactly — it's −273.15°C.
- **CN:** 认为绝对零度恰好是−273°C——实际是−273.15°C。
- **EN:** Believing gases actually reach zero volume at absolute zero — they liquefy or solidify first.
- **CN:** 认为气体在绝对零度时体积真的为零——它们会先液化或固化。
- **EN:** Using Celsius in gas law calculations — always convert to Kelvin.
- **CN:** 在气体定律计算中使用摄氏温度——始终转换为开尔文。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** For extrapolation questions, draw the best-fit line and extend it to the temperature axis.
- **CN:** 对于外推题，画出最佳拟合线并延伸到温度轴。
- **EN:** Remember: 0°C = 273 K (approximately) — this is a common reference point.
- **CN:** 记住：0°C = 273 K（近似）——这是一个常见参考点。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Boyle's Law / 玻意耳定律

$$ p_1 V_1 = p_2 V_2 \quad \text{(constant T, fixed mass)} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $p_1, p_2$ | Initial and final pressure | 初始和最终压强 | Pa |
| $V_1, V_2$ | Initial and final volume | 初始和最终体积 | m³ |

**Derivation / 推导:** From $pV = nRT$, if n and T are constant, $pV = \text{constant}$.
**Conditions / 适用条件:** Fixed mass of gas, constant temperature.
**Limitations / 局限性:** Only applies to ideal gases at low pressures; real gases deviate at high pressures.

## 5.2 Charles's Law / 查理定律

$$ \frac{V_1}{T_1} = \frac{V_2}{T_2} \quad \text{(constant p, fixed mass)} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $V_1, V_2$ | Initial and final volume | 初始和最终体积 | m³ |
| $T_1, T_2$ | Initial and final absolute temperature | 初始和最终绝对温度 | K |

**Derivation / 推导:** From $pV = nRT$, if n and p are constant, $\frac{V}{T} = \frac{nR}{p} = \text{constant}$.
**Conditions / 适用条件:** Fixed mass of gas, constant pressure.
**Limitations / 局限性:** Requires Kelvin temperature; gas must not liquefy.

## 5.3 Pressure Law / 压强定律

$$ \frac{p_1}{T_1} = \frac{p_2}{T_2} \quad \text{(constant V, fixed mass)} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $p_1, p_2$ | Initial and final pressure | 初始和最终压强 | Pa |
| $T_1, T_2$ | Initial and final absolute temperature | 初始和最终绝对温度 | K |

**Derivation / 推导:** From $pV = nRT$, if n and V are constant, $\frac{p}{T} = \frac{nR}{V} = \text{constant}$.
**Conditions / 适用条件:** Fixed mass of gas, constant volume.
**Limitations / 局限性:** Requires Kelvin temperature; gas must not liquefy.

## 5.4 Temperature Conversion / 温度转换

$$ T(K) = \theta(°C) + 273.15 $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $T$ | Absolute temperature | 绝对温度 | K |
| $\theta$ | Celsius temperature | 摄氏温度 | °C |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** Always use in gas law calculations.
**Limitations / 局限性:** For A-Level, using 273 instead of 273.15 is usually acceptable.

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 p-V Graph (Boyle's Law) / p-V图（玻意耳定律）

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **x-axis:** Volume (V) / 体积 (V) — m³
- **y-axis:** Pressure (p) / 压强 (p) — Pa

### Shape / 形状
- **EN:** A rectangular hyperbola — curve that approaches both axes asymptotically.
- **CN:** 矩形双曲线——曲线渐近地接近两个坐标轴。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
- **EN:** Not constant; gradient = $-\frac{p}{V}$ at any point. The product pV is constant.
- **CN:** 不恒定；任意点的斜率 = $-\frac{p}{V}$。乘积pV是常数。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
- **EN:** Area under the p-V curve represents work done by/on the gas (for expansion/compression).
- **CN:** p-V曲线下的面积代表气体对外做功或外界对气体做功（膨胀/压缩时）。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **EN:** A steeper curve means higher temperature (since pV = nRT, larger pV product = higher T).
- **CN:** 更陡的曲线意味着更高的温度（因为pV = nRT，更大的pV乘积 = 更高的T）。

## 6.2 V-T Graph (Charles's Law) / V-T图（查理定律）

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **x-axis:** Temperature (T) / 温度 (T) — K (or °C)
- **y-axis:** Volume (V) / 体积 (V) — m³

### Shape / 形状
- **EN (Kelvin):** Straight line through origin. **EN (Celsius):** Straight line intercepting T-axis at −273.15°C.
- **CN (开尔文):** 通过原点的直线。**CN (摄氏):** 直线在T轴上截距为−273.15°C。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
- **EN:** Gradient = $\frac{V}{T} = \frac{nR}{p}$ (constant for fixed p and n). Steeper gradient means lower pressure.
- **CN:** 斜率 = $\frac{V}{T} = \frac{nR}{p}$（对于固定p和n为常数）。更陡的斜率意味着更低的压强。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
- **EN:** Not physically meaningful for this graph.
- **CN:** 该图的面积没有物理意义。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **EN:** Extrapolating to V=0 gives absolute zero (−273.15°C). Different lines for different pressures.
- **CN:** 外推到V=0得到绝对零度（−273.15°C）。不同压强对应不同直线。

## 6.3 p-T Graph (Pressure Law) / p-T图（压强定律）

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **x-axis:** Temperature (T) / 温度 (T) — K (or °C)
- **y-axis:** Pressure (p) / 压强 (p) — Pa

### Shape / 形状
- **EN (Kelvin):** Straight line through origin. **EN (Celsius):** Straight line intercepting T-axis at −273.15°C.
- **CN (开尔文):** 通过原点的直线。**CN (摄氏):** 直线在T轴上截距为−273.15°C。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
- **EN:** Gradient = $\frac{p}{T} = \frac{nR}{V}$ (constant for fixed V and n). Steeper gradient means smaller volume.
- **CN:** 斜率 = $\frac{p}{T} = \frac{nR}{V}$（对于固定V和n为常数）。更陡的斜率意味着更小的体积。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
- **EN:** Not physically meaningful for this graph.
- **CN:** 该图的面积没有物理意义。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **EN:** Extrapolating to p=0 gives absolute zero (−273.15°C). Different lines for different volumes.
- **CN:** 外推到p=0得到绝对零度（−273.15°C）。不同体积对应不同直线。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Boyle's Law Calculation / 例题1：玻意耳定律计算

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A gas syringe contains 50.0 cm³ of air at atmospheric pressure (1.01 × 10⁵ Pa). The piston is pushed in until the volume is 20.0 cm³. The temperature remains constant. Calculate the new pressure of the gas.

**中文:**
一个气体注射器在标准大气压（1.01 × 10⁵ Pa）下含有50.0 cm³空气。活塞被推入直到体积变为20.0 cm³。温度保持不变。计算气体的新压强。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1:** Identify the known quantities.
$p_1 = 1.01 \times 10^5 \text{ Pa}$, $V_1 = 50.0 \text{ cm}^3$, $V_2 = 20.0 \text{ cm}^3$

**Step 2:** Apply Boyle's Law: $p_1 V_1 = p_2 V_2$

$$ p_2 = \frac{p_1 V_1}{V_2} = \frac{(1.01 \times 10^5)(50.0)}{20.0} $$

**Step 3:** Calculate.
$$ p_2 = \frac{5.05 \times 10^6}{20.0} = 2.525 \times 10^5 \text{ Pa} $$

**Step 4:** Express with appropriate significant figures.
$$ p_2 = 2.53 \times 10^5 \text{ Pa} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $2.53 \times 10^5$ Pa | **答案：** $2.53 \times 10^5$ Pa

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Units of volume cancel out, so you can use cm³ directly — no need to convert to m³.
- **CN:** 体积单位可以约掉，所以可以直接使用cm³——无需转换为m³。

## Example 2: Charles's Law with Temperature Conversion / 例题2：带温度转换的查理定律

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A balloon contains 2.50 m³ of helium at 20.0°C. The pressure remains constant. The balloon is heated to 60.0°C. Calculate the new volume of the balloon.

**中文:**
一个气球在20.0°C时含有2.50 m³的氦气。压强保持不变。气球被加热到60.0°C。计算气球的新体积。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1:** Convert temperatures to Kelvin.
$T_1 = 20.0 + 273 = 293 \text{ K}$
$T_2 = 60.0 + 273 = 333 \text{ K}$

**Step 2:** Apply Charles's Law: $\frac{V_1}{T_1} = \frac{V_2}{T_2}$

$$ V_2 = V_1 \times \frac{T_2}{T_1} = 2.50 \times \frac{333}{293} $$

**Step 3:** Calculate.
$$ V_2 = 2.50 \times 1.1365 = 2.841 \text{ m}^3 $$

**Step 4:** Express with appropriate significant figures.
$$ V_2 = 2.84 \text{ m}^3 $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** 2.84 m³ | **答案：** 2.84 m³

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Always convert Celsius to Kelvin before using gas laws. Using 273 is acceptable for A-Level.
- **CN:** 在使用气体定律前，始终将摄氏温度转换为开尔文。A-Level中使用273是可接受的。

## Example 3: Pressure Law — Finding Absolute Zero / 例题3：压强定律——求绝对零度

### Question / 题目
**English:**
In an experiment to verify the Pressure Law, the following data were obtained for a fixed mass of gas at constant volume:

| Temperature (°C) | 0 | 20 | 40 | 60 | 80 | 100 |
|-----------------|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pressure (kPa) | 101 | 108 | 116 | 123 | 130 | 138 |

Plot a graph of pressure against temperature in °C and use it to estimate the value of absolute zero.

**中文:**
在验证压强定律的实验中，固定质量气体在恒定体积下获得以下数据：

| 温度 (°C) | 0 | 20 | 40 | 60 | 80 | 100 |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 压强 (kPa) | 101 | 108 | 116 | 123 | 130 | 138 |

绘制压强对温度（°C）的图，并用它来估计绝对零度的值。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1:** Plot the graph with temperature on x-axis (°C) and pressure on y-axis (kPa).

**Step 2:** Draw a best-fit straight line through the points.

**Step 3:** Extrapolate the line backward until it crosses the temperature axis (where p = 0).

**Step 4:** Read the intercept. From the data, the line crosses at approximately −273°C.

**Step 5:** Calculate using two points to verify:
Using points (0°C, 101 kPa) and (100°C, 138 kPa):
Gradient = $\frac{138 - 101}{100 - 0} = \frac{37}{100} = 0.37 \text{ kPa/°C}$

Equation: $p = 0.37\theta + 101$

Set p = 0: $0 = 0.37\theta + 101$
$\theta = -\frac{101}{0.37} = -273°\text{C}$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** Absolute zero ≈ −273°C (or −273.15°C theoretically) | **答案：** 绝对零度 ≈ −273°C（理论上为−273.15°C）

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** The intercept gives absolute zero. More accurate data gives −273.15°C.
- **CN:** 截距给出绝对零度。更精确的数据给出−273.15°C。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
This sub-topic connects to practical work in several ways:

1. **Boyle's Law Experiment:** Use a gas syringe with weights to vary pressure and measure volume. Key skills: reading volume scale accurately, ensuring constant temperature (wait for thermal equilibrium), plotting p against 1/V to get a straight line.

2. **Charles's Law Experiment:** Use a capillary tube with a mercury plug in a water bath. Key skills: measuring length of air column (proportional to volume), controlling temperature with water bath, ensuring constant pressure (open tube).

3. **Pressure Law Experiment:** Use a sealed flask with a pressure gauge in a water bath. Key skills: reading pressure gauge, controlling temperature, ensuring constant volume (rigid container).

4. **Uncertainties:** Volume readings ±0.1 cm³, temperature readings ±0.5°C, pressure readings ±1 kPa. Error analysis includes systematic errors (heat loss, friction in syringe) and random errors (parallax in reading scales).

5. **Graph Plotting:** Plot p against 1/V for Boyle's Law (straight line through origin), V against T for Charles's Law, p against T for Pressure Law. Calculate gradients and intercepts.

**中文:**
本子知识点通过多种方式与实验工作联系：

1. **玻意耳定律实验：** 使用带砝码的气体注射器改变压强并测量体积。关键技能：准确读取体积刻度、确保恒温（等待热平衡）、绘制p对1/V图得到直线。

2. **查理定律实验：** 使用水浴中带汞塞的毛细管。关键技能：测量气柱长度（与体积成正比）、用水浴控制温度、确保恒压（开口管）。

3. **压强定律实验：** 使用水浴中带压强计的密封烧瓶。关键技能：读取压强计、控制温度、确保恒容（刚性容器）。

4. **不确定度：** 体积读数±0.1 cm³，温度读数±0.5°C，压强读数±1 kPa。误差分析包括系统误差（热损失、注射器摩擦）和随机误差（读取刻度时的视差）。

5. **图形绘制：** 玻意耳定律绘制p对1/V图（通过原点的直线），查理定律绘制V对T图，压强定律绘制p对T图。计算斜率和截距。
