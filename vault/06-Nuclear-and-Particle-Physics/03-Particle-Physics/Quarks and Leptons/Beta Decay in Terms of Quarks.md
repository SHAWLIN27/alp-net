# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
Beta decay is a fundamental nuclear process that transforms a neutron into a proton (or vice versa) within an unstable nucleus, accompanied by the emission of an electron (β⁻) and an antineutrino (or a positron and a neutrino for β⁺ decay). At the quark level, this process reveals the weak interaction's ability to change quark flavor — specifically, a down quark transforms into an up quark (β⁻ decay) or an up quark transforms into a down quark (β⁺ decay). This sub-topic bridges [[Nuclear Fission and Fusion]] with [[The Six Quarks (Up, Down, Strange, Charm, Top, Bottom)]] and is essential for understanding [[Conservation Laws (Baryon Number, Lepton Number, Strangeness)]]. For A-Level students, mastering beta decay in quark terms is crucial because it demonstrates how the weak nuclear force operates at the most fundamental level, explaining why certain nuclei are unstable and how elements transmute.

**中文:**
β衰变是一种基本的核过程，在不稳定原子核内将中子转变为质子（或反之），同时发射电子（β⁻）和反中微子（或正电子和中微子用于β⁺衰变）。在夸克层面，这一过程揭示了弱相互作用改变夸克味道的能力——具体来说，下夸克转变为上夸克（β⁻衰变）或上夸克转变为下夸克（β⁺衰变）。本子知识点将[[核裂变与聚变]]与[[六种夸克（上、下、奇、粲、顶、底）]]联系起来，对理解[[守恒定律（重子数、轻子数、奇异数）]]至关重要。对于A-Level学生，掌握夸克层面的β衰变至关重要，因为它展示了弱核力在最基本层面如何运作，解释了为什么某些原子核不稳定以及元素如何嬗变。

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Beta-Minus (β⁻) Decay at the Quark Level / 夸克层面的β⁻衰变

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
In β⁻ decay, a neutron (udd) transforms into a proton (uud). At the quark level, one of the neutron's down quarks changes into an up quark via the weak interaction. This process is mediated by a W⁻ boson, which immediately decays into an electron (e⁻) and an electron antineutrino (ν̄ₑ). The quark-level equation is:

$$ d \rightarrow u + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

The overall nuclear equation is:

$$ n \rightarrow p + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

Or in terms of quarks:

$$ udd \rightarrow uud + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

**中文:**
在β⁻衰变中，中子（udd）转变为质子（uud）。在夸克层面，中子中的一个下夸克通过弱相互作用变为上夸克。这一过程由W⁻玻色子介导，W⁻玻色子立即衰变为电子（e⁻）和电子反中微子（ν̄ₑ）。夸克级方程为：

$$ d \rightarrow u + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

整体核方程为：

$$ n \rightarrow p + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

或用夸克表示：

$$ udd \rightarrow uud + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The weak interaction changes the flavor of a quark (down → up), which changes the overall hadron from a neutron to a proton. The W⁻ boson carries away the negative charge, which is then transferred to the electron. The antineutrino ensures lepton number conservation.

**中文:** 弱相互作用改变夸克的味道（下→上），从而将整体强子从中子变为质子。W⁻玻色子带走负电荷，然后传递给电子。反中微子确保轻子数守恒。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- ❌ **English:** "The electron comes from inside the nucleus." — No, the electron is created during the decay from the energy released.
- ❌ **中文:** "电子来自原子核内部。" — 不对，电子是在衰变过程中从释放的能量中产生的。
- ❌ **English:** "The antineutrino is the same as a neutrino." — No, they are antiparticles with opposite lepton numbers.
- ❌ **中文:** "反中微子和中微子一样。" — 不对，它们是反粒子，轻子数相反。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ **English:** Always write the antineutrino with a bar: ν̄ₑ (not νₑ). The bar indicates antiparticle.
- ✅ **中文:** 始终用横线写反中微子：ν̄ₑ（不是νₑ）。横线表示反粒子。
- ✅ **English:** Check conservation laws: charge (0 → +1 -1 +0), baryon number (+1 → +1), lepton number (0 → +1 -1).
- ✅ **中文:** 检查守恒定律：电荷（0 → +1 -1 +0），重子数（+1 → +1），轻子数（0 → +1 -1）。

## 4.2 Beta-Plus (β⁺) Decay at the Quark Level / 夸克层面的β⁺衰变

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
In β⁺ decay, a proton (uud) transforms into a neutron (udd). At the quark level, one of the proton's up quarks changes into a down quark via the weak interaction. This process is mediated by a W⁺ boson, which decays into a positron (e⁺) and an electron neutrino (νₑ). The quark-level equation is:

$$ u \rightarrow d + e^+ + \nu_e $$

The overall nuclear equation is:

$$ p \rightarrow n + e^+ + \nu_e $$

Or in terms of quarks:

$$ uud \rightarrow udd + e^+ + \nu_e $$

**中文:**
在β⁺衰变中，质子（uud）转变为中子（udd）。在夸克层面，质子中的一个上夸克通过弱相互作用变为下夸克。这一过程由W⁺玻色子介导，W⁺玻色子衰变为正电子（e⁺）和电子中微子（νₑ）。夸克级方程为：

$$ u \rightarrow d + e^+ + \nu_e $$

整体核方程为：

$$ p \rightarrow n + e^+ + \nu_e $$

或用夸克表示：

$$ uud \rightarrow udd + e^+ + \nu_e $$

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The weak interaction changes up quark to down quark, converting a proton to a neutron. The W⁺ boson carries positive charge to the positron. This process only occurs in proton-rich nuclei where the extra energy from the strong force makes it energetically possible — a free proton cannot undergo β⁺ decay.

**中文:** 弱相互作用将上夸克变为下夸克，将质子转变为中子。W⁺玻色子将正电荷传递给正电子。这一过程只发生在富质子核中，强力的额外能量使其在能量上可能——自由质子不能发生β⁺衰变。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- ❌ **English:** "A free proton can undergo β⁺ decay." — No, a free proton is stable because the neutron is heavier (mass difference ~1.29 MeV/c²).
- ❌ **中文:** "自由质子可以发生β⁺衰变。" — 不对，自由质子稳定，因为中子更重（质量差约1.29 MeV/c²）。
- ❌ **English:** "β⁺ decay emits an electron." — No, it emits a positron (the antiparticle of the electron).
- ❌ **中文:** "β⁺衰变发射电子。" — 不对，它发射正电子（电子的反粒子）。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ **English:** Remember: β⁻ decay emits e⁻ + ν̄ₑ; β⁺ decay emits e⁺ + νₑ. The neutrino/antineutrino flips.
- ✅ **中文:** 记住：β⁻衰变发射e⁻ + ν̄ₑ；β⁺衰变发射e⁺ + νₑ。中微子/反中微子互换。
- ✅ **English:** For β⁺ decay, the proton must be bound in a nucleus — the extra binding energy makes it possible.
- ✅ **中文:** 对于β⁺衰变，质子必须束缚在原子核中——额外的结合能使其成为可能。

## 4.3 Why Free Neutrons Decay but Free Protons Do Not / 为什么自由中子衰变而自由质子不衰变

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
A free neutron is unstable with a half-life of about 15 minutes (880 seconds). This is because the neutron mass (939.565 MeV/c²) is greater than the proton mass (938.272 MeV/c²) plus the electron mass (0.511 MeV/c²). The mass difference of ~1.29 MeV/c² provides enough energy for the decay to occur. In contrast, a free proton is lighter than a neutron, so β⁺ decay (which would require the proton to become a heavier neutron) is energetically forbidden. However, inside a nucleus, the binding energy can make β⁺ decay possible if the daughter nucleus is more stable.

**中文:**
自由中子不稳定，半衰期约15分钟（880秒）。这是因为中子质量（939.565 MeV/c²）大于质子质量（938.272 MeV/c²）加上电子质量（0.511 MeV/c²）。约1.29 MeV/c²的质量差提供了足够的能量使衰变发生。相反，自由质子比中子轻，因此β⁺衰变（需要质子变为更重的中子）在能量上被禁止。然而，在原子核内部，如果子核更稳定，结合能可以使β⁺衰变成为可能。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** This explains why all free neutrons eventually decay, but protons are stable (proton decay has never been observed). In nuclei, the balance between the strong force and electrostatic repulsion determines whether β⁻ or β⁺ decay occurs.

**中文:** 这解释了为什么所有自由中子最终都会衰变，而质子稳定（从未观察到质子衰变）。在原子核中，强力和静电排斥之间的平衡决定了发生β⁻还是β⁺衰变。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- ✅ **English:** Quote the mass difference: mₙ - mₚ ≈ 1.29 MeV/c² > mₑ (0.511 MeV/c²).
- ✅ **中文:** 引用质量差：mₙ - mₚ ≈ 1.29 MeV/c² > mₑ (0.511 MeV/c²)。
- ✅ **English:** The decay energy is shared between the electron and antineutrino as kinetic energy.
- ✅ **中文:** 衰变能量在电子和反中微子之间以动能形式分配。

- **English:** Does not explain why the decay rate varies between different nuclei.

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 Beta Decay Energy Spectrum / β衰变能谱

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Kinetic energy of emitted electron/positron / 发射电子/正电子的动能 (MeV)
- **Y-axis:** Number of particles detected / 检测到的粒子数

### Shape / 形状
**English:** A continuous spectrum from zero up to a maximum energy (E_max). The shape is a broad curve that peaks at around ⅓ of E_max, then drops to zero at E_max. This continuous spectrum was historically puzzling because it suggested energy was not conserved — until the neutrino was postulated by Pauli in 1930.

**中文:** 从零到最大能量（E_max）的连续能谱。形状是一条宽曲线，在约⅓ E_max处达到峰值，然后在E_max处降至零。这种连续能谱在历史上令人困惑，因为它暗示能量不守恒——直到1930年泡利提出中微子假说。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
**English:** The gradient shows the rate of change of particle count with energy. The negative gradient after the peak indicates fewer particles at higher energies.

**中文:** 斜率表示粒子数随能量的变化率。峰值后的负梯度表示高能量粒子较少。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
**English:** The total area under the curve represents the total number of beta particles emitted.

**中文:** 曲线下的总面积表示发射的β粒子总数。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **English:** The continuous spectrum proves that beta decay involves three particles (not two) — the energy is shared between the electron and the (anti)neutrino.
- **中文:** 连续能谱证明β衰变涉及三个粒子（不是两个）——能量在电子和（反）中微子之间分配。
- **English:** E_max corresponds to the case where the (anti)neutrino carries zero kinetic energy.
- **中文:** E_max对应于（反）中微子携带零动能的情况。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Writing Quark-Level Equations / 例1：写出夸克级方程

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A carbon-14 nucleus undergoes beta-minus decay:
$$ ^{14}_{6}C \rightarrow ^{14}_{7}N + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

(a) Write the quark-level equation for this decay.
(b) Identify the exchange particle involved.
(c) Verify that charge, baryon number, and lepton number are conserved.

**中文:**
碳-14原子核发生β⁻衰变：
$$ ^{14}_{6}C \rightarrow ^{14}_{7}N + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

(a) 写出此衰变的夸克级方程。
(b) 确定涉及的交换粒子。
(c) 验证电荷、重子数和轻子数守恒。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Quark-level equation / 夸克级方程:**

The neutron in carbon-14 contains (udd). One down quark changes to an up quark:

$$ d \rightarrow u + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

Or in terms of the hadron:

$$ n \rightarrow p + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e $$

**(b) Exchange particle / 交换粒子:**

The exchange particle is the **W⁻ boson** (W⁻玻色子).

**(c) Conservation check / 守恒检查:**

| Quantity | Before (d) | After (u + e⁻ + ν̄ₑ) | Conserved? |
|----------|-----------|---------------------|------------|
| Charge | -⅓e | +⅔e - e + 0 = -⅓e | ✅ Yes |
| Baryon number | +⅓ | +⅓ + 0 + 0 = +⅓ | ✅ Yes |
| Lepton number | 0 | 0 + 1 + (-1) = 0 | ✅ Yes |

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) $d \rightarrow u + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e$ (b) W⁻ boson (c) All conserved | **答案：** (a) $d \rightarrow u + e^- + \bar{\nu}_e$ (b) W⁻玻色子 (c) 全部守恒

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** The antineutrino has lepton number -1, which cancels the electron's +1.
- **中文:** 反中微子的轻子数为-1，与电子的+1抵消。

## Example 2: Beta-Plus Decay Analysis / 例2：β⁺衰变分析

### Question / 题目
**English:**
Fluorine-18 undergoes beta-plus decay:
$$ ^{18}_{9}F \rightarrow ^{18}_{8}O + e^+ + \nu_e $$

(a) Write the quark-level equation for this decay.
(b) Explain why this decay cannot occur for a free proton.
(c) State the exchange particle and draw a Feynman diagram for this process.

**中文:**
氟-18发生β⁺衰变：
$$ ^{18}_{9}F \rightarrow ^{18}_{8}O + e^+ + \nu_e $$

(a) 写出此衰变的夸克级方程。
(b) 解释为什么自由质子不能发生此衰变。
(c) 说明交换粒子并画出此过程的费曼图。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Quark-level equation / 夸克级方程:**

One up quark in the proton changes to a down quark:

$$ u \rightarrow d + e^+ + \nu_e $$

**(b) Why free proton cannot decay / 为什么自由质子不能衰变:**

**English:** A free proton has mass 938.272 MeV/c², while a neutron has mass 939.565 MeV/c². The neutron is heavier by ~1.29 MeV/c². For β⁺ decay to occur, the proton would need to become a heavier particle, which violates energy conservation. The decay is only possible inside a nucleus where the binding energy of the daughter nucleus provides the extra energy.

**中文:** 自由质子质量为938.272 MeV/c²，而中子质量为939.565 MeV/c²。中子重约1.29 MeV/c²。要使β⁺衰变发生，质子需要变成更重的粒子，这违反能量守恒。只有在原子核内部，子核的结合能提供额外能量时，衰变才可能发生。

**(c) Exchange particle and Feynman diagram / 交换粒子和费曼图:**

**English:** The exchange particle is the **W⁺ boson**. The Feynman diagram shows an up quark emitting a W⁺ boson and becoming a down quark. The W⁺ boson then decays into a positron (e⁺) and an electron neutrino (νₑ).

**中文:** 交换粒子是**W⁺玻色子**。费曼图显示上夸克发射W⁺玻色子并变为下夸克。W⁺玻色子然后衰变为正电子（e⁺）和电子中微子（νₑ）。

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) $u \rightarrow d + e^+ + \nu_e$ (b) Neutron is heavier than proton (c) W⁺ boson | **答案：** (a) $u \rightarrow d + e^+ + \nu_e$ (b) 中子比质子重 (c) W⁺玻色子

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Remember: β⁻ decay → W⁻ boson → e⁻ + ν̄ₑ; β⁺ decay → W⁺ boson → e⁺ + νₑ.
- **中文:** 记住：β⁻衰变 → W⁻玻色子 → e⁻ + ν̄ₑ；β⁺衰变 → W⁺玻色子 → e⁺ + νₑ。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
Beta decay connects to practical skills in several ways:

1. **Detection of beta particles:** Use of Geiger-Müller tubes, cloud chambers, or scintillation detectors to observe beta radiation. Students should understand that beta particles are electrons/positrons and can be distinguished from alpha and gamma by their penetration and ionization properties.

2. **Half-life measurement:** Measuring the decay rate of a beta-emitting source (e.g., protactinium-234 from uranium) to determine half-life. This involves background radiation subtraction and uncertainty analysis.

3. **Energy spectrum measurement:** Using a magnetic spectrometer to measure the kinetic energy distribution of beta particles. The continuous spectrum provides experimental evidence for the neutrino.

4. **Uncertainties:** When measuring count rates, students must account for random decay (Poisson statistics) and calculate uncertainties using $\sqrt{N}$ for count data.

5. **Graph plotting:** Plotting ln(count rate) vs. time to determine decay constant λ from the gradient.

**中文:**
β衰变在多个方面与实验技能相关：

1. **β粒子的探测：** 使用盖革-米勒管、云室或闪烁探测器观察β辐射。学生应理解β粒子是电子/正电子，可通过穿透和电离特性与α和γ区分。

2. **半衰期测量：** 测量β放射源（如铀中的镤-234）的衰变率以确定半衰期。这涉及本底辐射扣除和不确定度分析。

3. **能谱测量：** 使用磁谱仪测量β粒子的动能分布。连续能谱为中微子提供实验证据。

4. **不确定度：** 测量计数率时，学生必须考虑随机衰变（泊松统计）并使用$\sqrt{N}$计算计数数据的不确定度。

5. **图表绘制：** 绘制ln(计数率)与时间的关系图，从斜率确定衰变常数λ。

> 📋 **Edexcel Only:** Edexcel IAL Paper 3 (Practical Skills) may include questions on beta particle detection and half-life determination using a GM tube.

> 📋 **CIE Only:** CIE Paper 5 (Planning, Analysis and Evaluation) may require students to plan an experiment to investigate beta decay or analyze given data.

| **Exam Tip / 考试提示** | Always check lepton number: νₑ = +1, ν̄ₑ = -1 |
