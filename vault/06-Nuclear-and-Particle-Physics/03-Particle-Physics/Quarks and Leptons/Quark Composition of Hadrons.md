# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores the **quark composition of hadrons** — the fundamental building blocks of protons, neutrons, and other particles that experience the strong nuclear force. Hadrons are divided into two families: **baryons** (made of three quarks) and **mesons** (made of a quark-antiquark pair). Understanding quark composition is essential for explaining particle properties like charge, baryon number, and strangeness, and for interpreting high-energy particle interactions. This knowledge directly connects to [[Beta Decay in Terms of Quarks]] and [[Conservation Laws (Baryon Number, Lepton Number, Strangeness)]], and builds on [[Fundamental Particles]].

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨**强子的夸克组成**——构成质子、中子及其他参与强相互作用的粒子的基本单元。强子分为两类：**重子**（由三个夸克组成）和**介子**（由一个夸克和一个反夸克组成）。理解夸克组成对于解释电荷、重子数和奇异数等粒子性质，以及解读高能粒子相互作用至关重要。这部分知识直接联系到[[Beta Decay in Terms of Quarks]]和[[Conservation Laws (Baryon Number, Lepton Number, Strangeness)]]，并建立在[[Fundamental Particles]]的基础上。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Quark Model of Hadrons / 强子的夸克模型

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The quark model proposes that all hadrons are composed of quarks. There are six flavours of quarks, but at A-Level, we focus on the three lightest: **up (u)**, **down (d)**, and **strange (s)**. Each quark has a fractional electric charge: $u = +\frac{2}{3}e$, $d = -\frac{1}{3}e$, $s = -\frac{1}{3}e$. Antiquarks have opposite charges: $\bar{u} = -\frac{2}{3}e$, $\bar{d} = +\frac{1}{3}e$, $\bar{s} = +\frac{1}{3}e$.

Hadrons are classified into:
- **Baryons** (qqq): Three quarks combine to give integer charge. Examples: proton (uud), neutron (udd).
- **Mesons** (q\bar{q}): A quark and an antiquark combine. Examples: pion ($\pi^+ = u\bar{d}$), kaon ($K^+ = u\bar{s}$).

**中文:**
夸克模型提出所有强子都由夸克组成。夸克有六种味，但在A-Level阶段，我们关注最轻的三种：**上夸克(u)**、**下夸克(d)** 和**奇异夸克(s)**。每个夸克带有分数电荷：$u = +\frac{2}{3}e$，$d = -\frac{1}{3}e$，$s = -\frac{1}{3}e$。反夸克带有相反的电荷：$\bar{u} = -\frac{2}{3}e$，$\bar{d} = +\frac{1}{3}e$，$\bar{s} = +\frac{1}{3}e$。

强子分为两类：
- **重子** (qqq)：三个夸克组合成整数电荷。例如：质子(uud)、中子(udd)。
- **介子** (q\bar{q})：一个夸克和一个反夸克组合。例如：π介子($\pi^+ = u\bar{d}$)、K介子($K^+ = u\bar{s}$)。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The quark model explains why hadrons have integer charges despite quarks having fractional charges — the combination of three quarks (or a quark-antiquark pair) always sums to an integer. It also explains the existence of particles like kaons, which contain strange quarks and exhibit strangeness as a quantum number.

**中文:**
夸克模型解释了为什么尽管夸克带有分数电荷，强子却具有整数电荷——三个夸克（或一个夸克-反夸克对）的组合总是加起来得到整数。它也解释了像K介子这样的粒子的存在，它们含有奇异夸克并表现出奇异数这一量子数。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking quarks are "inside" hadrons like marbles in a bag (they are quantum excitations, not classical particles).
  - Confusing baryon number with charge — baryon number is always +1 for baryons, 0 for mesons.
  - Forgetting that antiquarks have negative baryon number ($B = -\frac{1}{3}$).
- **中文:**
  - 认为夸克像袋子里的弹珠一样"在"强子内部（它们是量子激发，不是经典粒子）。
  - 混淆重子数和电荷——重子的重子数总是+1，介子为0。
  - 忘记反夸克具有负的重子数($B = -\frac{1}{3}$)。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always check charge conservation first when determining quark composition. Then check baryon number. Strangeness is only conserved in strong interactions.
- **中文:** 在确定夸克组成时，始终先检查电荷守恒，然后检查重子数。奇异数仅在强相互作用中守恒。

## 4.2 Baryon Number and Strangeness / 重子数和奇异数

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
**Baryon number (B)** is a conserved quantum number: $B = +1$ for baryons, $B = -1$ for antibaryons, $B = 0$ for mesons and leptons. Each quark has $B = +\frac{1}{3}$, each antiquark has $B = -\frac{1}{3}$.

**Strangeness (S)** is a quantum number associated with strange quarks: $S = -1$ for a strange quark, $S = +1$ for a strange antiquark. Non-strange quarks (u, d) have $S = 0$. Strangeness is conserved in strong interactions but NOT in weak interactions.

**中文:**
**重子数(B)** 是一个守恒的量子数：重子$B = +1$，反重子$B = -1$，介子和轻子$B = 0$。每个夸克有$B = +\frac{1}{3}$，每个反夸克有$B = -\frac{1}{3}$。

**奇异数(S)** 是与奇异夸克相关的量子数：奇异夸克$S = -1$，反奇异夸克$S = +1$。非奇异夸克(u, d)的$S = 0$。奇异数在强相互作用中守恒，但在弱相互作用中不守恒。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Thinking strangeness is always conserved (it's only conserved in strong interactions).
- **中文:** 认为奇异数总是守恒的（它只在强相互作用中守恒）。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** When a kaon decays via weak interaction (e.g., $K^+ \to \mu^+ + \nu_\mu$), strangeness changes — this is allowed.
- **中文:** 当K介子通过弱相互作用衰变时（例如$K^+ \to \mu^+ + \nu_\mu$），奇异数会改变——这是允许的。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Charge of a Hadron / 强子的电荷

$$ Q_{\text{hadron}} = \sum_{\text{quarks}} Q_{\text{quark}} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $Q_{\text{hadron}}$ | Total charge of hadron | 强子的总电荷 | $e$ (elementary charge) |
| $Q_{\text{quark}}$ | Charge of individual quark ($+\frac{2}{3}e$ or $-\frac{1}{3}e$) | 单个夸克的电荷 | $e$ |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** Always applies. | 始终适用。

## 5.2 Baryon Number of a Hadron / 强子的重子数

$$ B_{\text{hadron}} = \frac{1}{3}(n_q - n_{\bar{q}}) $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $B_{\text{hadron}}$ | Baryon number of hadron | 强子的重子数 | dimensionless |
| $n_q$ | Number of quarks | 夸克数量 | - |
| $n_{\bar{q}}$ | Number of antiquarks | 反夸克数量 | - |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** Always applies. | 始终适用。

## 5.3 Strangeness of a Hadron / 强子的奇异数

$$ S_{\text{hadron}} = -(n_s - n_{\bar{s}}) $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $S_{\text{hadron}}$ | Strangeness of hadron | 强子的奇异数 | dimensionless |
| $n_s$ | Number of strange quarks | 奇异夸克数量 | - |
| $n_{\bar{s}}$ | Number of strange antiquarks | 反奇异夸克数量 | - |

**Conditions / 适用条件:** Conserved in strong interactions only. | 仅在强相互作用中守恒。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Determining Quark Composition / 确定夸克组成

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A hadron has charge $+1e$, baryon number $+1$, and strangeness $-1$. Determine its quark composition.

**中文:**
一个强子具有电荷$+1e$、重子数$+1$和奇异数$-1$。确定其夸克组成。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Identify the type of hadron / 步骤1：确定强子类型**
- Baryon number $+1$ → it is a baryon (three quarks).
- 重子数$+1$ → 它是重子（三个夸克）。

**Step 2: Determine the number of strange quarks / 步骤2：确定奇异夸克数量**
- Strangeness $S = -1$ → one strange quark ($s$) is present.
- 奇异数$S = -1$ → 存在一个奇异夸克($s$)。

**Step 3: Determine the remaining quarks / 步骤3：确定剩余夸克**
- Total charge: $+1e$
- Strange quark charge: $-\frac{1}{3}e$
- Remaining charge from two quarks: $+1e - (-\frac{1}{3}e) = +\frac{4}{3}e$
- Two quarks must sum to $+\frac{4}{3}e$: possible combinations:
  - $u + u = +\frac{2}{3} + \frac{2}{3} = +\frac{4}{3}e$ ✓
  - $u + d = +\frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{3} = +\frac{1}{3}e$ ✗
  - $d + d = -\frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{3} = -\frac{2}{3}e$ ✗
- Therefore, the two remaining quarks are both up quarks ($u$).

**Step 4: Write the composition / 步骤4：写出组成**
- Quark composition: $uus$
- This is the $\Sigma^+$ baryon.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $uus$ (Sigma plus baryon) | **答案：** $uus$ (Σ⁺重子)

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always start with baryon number to determine if it's a baryon or meson, then use strangeness to find strange quarks, then use charge to find the remaining quarks.
- **中文:** 始终先从重子数开始确定是重子还是介子，然后用奇异数找到奇异夸克，最后用电荷找到剩余夸克。

## Example 2: Conservation Laws in Particle Interactions / 粒子相互作用中的守恒定律

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A kaon $K^-$ collides with a proton $p$ to produce a lambda baryon $\Lambda^0$ and a pion $\pi^+$. Write the equation and verify that charge, baryon number, and strangeness are conserved.

**中文:**
一个K⁻介子与一个质子碰撞产生一个Λ⁰重子和一个π⁺介子。写出方程并验证电荷、重子数和奇异数守恒。

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Write the interaction / 步骤1：写出相互作用**
$$ K^- + p \to \Lambda^0 + \pi^+ $$

**Step 2: Identify quark compositions / 步骤2：确定夸克组成**
- $K^- = \bar{u}s$ (charge: $-1$, B: $0$, S: $-1$)
- $p = uud$ (charge: $+1$, B: $+1$, S: $0$)
- $\Lambda^0 = uds$ (charge: $0$, B: $+1$, S: $-1$)
- $\pi^+ = u\bar{d}$ (charge: $+1$, B: $0$, S: $0$)

**Step 3: Check conservation / 步骤3：检查守恒**

| Quantity | Before | After | Conserved? |
|----------|--------|-------|------------|
| Charge | $-1 + (+1) = 0$ | $0 + (+1) = +1$ | ❌ **NOT conserved** |

Wait — charge is not conserved! Let's re-check.

Actually, $K^-$ has charge $-1$, $p$ has charge $+1$, so total before = $0$.
$\Lambda^0$ has charge $0$, $\pi^+$ has charge $+1$, so total after = $+1$.

This interaction is **not possible** as written. The correct interaction might be:
$$ K^- + p \to \Lambda^0 + \pi^0 $$
where $\pi^0$ has charge $0$.

Let's verify this corrected interaction:

| Quantity | Before | After | Conserved? |
|----------|--------|-------|------------|
| Charge | $-1 + (+1) = 0$ | $0 + 0 = 0$ | ✓ |
| Baryon number | $0 + (+1) = +1$ | $+1 + 0 = +1$ | ✓ |
| Strangeness | $-1 + 0 = -1$ | $-1 + 0 = -1$ | ✓ |

**Step 4: Conclusion / 步骤4：结论**
The corrected interaction $K^- + p \to \Lambda^0 + \pi^0$ conserves charge, baryon number, and strangeness.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $K^- + p \to \Lambda^0 + \pi^0$ — all quantities conserved. | **答案：** $K^- + p \to \Lambda^0 + \pi^0$ — 所有量守恒。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always check charge conservation first — it's the most common mistake to miss. Then check baryon number, then strangeness.
- **中文:** 始终先检查电荷守恒——这是最常见的错误。然后检查重子数，最后检查奇异数。

# 12. Quick Revision Sheet / 速查表

| Category / 类别 | Key Points / 要点 |
|----------------|------------------|
| **Definition / 定义** | Hadrons = particles made of quarks. Baryons (qqq), Mesons (q\bar{q}). |
| **Key Quarks / 关键夸克** | u (+2/3e), d (-1/3e), s (-1/3e). Antiquarks have opposite charge. |
| **Key Hadrons / 关键强子** | p = uud, n = udd, π⁺ = u\bar{d}, K⁺ = u\bar{s}, Λ⁰ = uds, Σ⁺ = uus |
| **Baryon Number / 重子数** | Baryon: +1, Meson: 0, Quark: +1/3, Antiquark: -1/3. Always conserved. |
| **Strangeness / 奇异数** | s quark: -1, \bar{s}: +1. Conserved in strong, NOT in weak. |
| **Key Formula / 核心公式** | $Q = \sum Q_q$, $B = \frac{1}{3}(n_q - n_{\bar{q}})$, $S = -(n_s - n_{\bar{s}})$ |
| **Exam Tip / 考试提示** | Always check charge first, then baryon number, then strangeness. Strangeness only conserved in strong interactions. |
| **Common Mistake / 常见错误** | Forgetting antiquarks have negative baryon number. Applying strangeness conservation to weak interactions. |
