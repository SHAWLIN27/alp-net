# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic covers the **experimental observations** that form the foundation of the photoelectric effect. Before any theoretical explanation, scientists conducted experiments that revealed several puzzling results that could not be explained by classical wave theory. These observations include: the existence of a **threshold frequency**, the instantaneous emission of electrons, the dependence of **photocurrent** on **intensity**, and the dependence of **maximum kinetic energy** on **frequency**. Understanding these experimental facts is crucial because they provide the evidence that led to [[Einstein's Photon Model]] and the development of quantum physics. This sub-topic connects directly to [[The Photoelectric Effect]] hub and serves as the empirical basis for all subsequent theoretical developments.

**中文:**
本子知识点涵盖构成光电效应基础的**实验观测结果**。在理论解释之前，科学家们进行的实验揭示了一些经典波动理论无法解释的令人困惑的结果。这些观测包括：**截止频率**的存在、电子的瞬时发射、**光电流**对**光强**的依赖性，以及**最大动能**对**频率**的依赖性。理解这些实验事实至关重要，因为它们提供了导致[[爱因斯坦光子模型]]和量子物理学发展的证据。本子知识点直接连接到[[光电效应]]中心主题，并作为所有后续理论发展的实验基础。

# 3. Core Definitions / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Photoelectric Effect** / 光电效应 | The emission of electrons from a metal surface when electromagnetic radiation of sufficient frequency is incident upon it | 当足够频率的电磁辐射照射金属表面时，电子从金属表面发射出来的现象 | Confusing with thermionic emission (heat) or photoemission from semiconductors |
| **Photoelectron** / 光电子 | An electron emitted from a metal surface as a result of the photoelectric effect | 由于光电效应而从金属表面发射出来的电子 | Thinking photoelectrons are different from normal electrons — they are the same |
| **Threshold Frequency** ($f_0$) / 截止频率 | The minimum frequency of incident electromagnetic radiation required to just cause emission of photoelectrons from a given metal surface | 刚好能够使光电子从给定金属表面发射出来的入射电磁辐射的最低频率 | Confusing with threshold wavelength — they are inversely related: $f_0 = c/\lambda_0$ |
| **Photocurrent** / 光电流 | The electric current resulting from the flow of photoelectrons in a photoelectric effect experiment | 在光电效应实验中由光电子流动产生的电流 | Thinking photocurrent is the same as the number of photoelectrons — it is proportional but not identical |
| **Stopping Potential** ($V_s$) / 遏止电势 | The minimum reverse potential difference that must be applied to reduce the photocurrent to zero | 为使光电流减小到零所需施加的最小反向电势差 | Confusing with threshold frequency — stopping potential relates to kinetic energy, not emission |
| **Intensity** / 光强 | The power per unit area incident on the metal surface, proportional to the number of photons per second per unit area | 入射到金属表面单位面积上的功率，与每秒每单位面积的光子数成正比 | Thinking intensity affects kinetic energy — it only affects the number of photoelectrons |

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The Experimental Setup / 实验装置

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The classic photoelectric effect experiment uses an **evacuated glass tube** containing two electrodes: a **photocathode** (the metal surface being studied) and an **anode** (collector). The photocathode is connected to the negative terminal of a variable power supply, and the anode to the positive terminal. A sensitive **microammeter** measures the photocurrent. Monochromatic light (single frequency) is directed onto the photocathode through a quartz window (which transmits UV light). The experiment can vary:
- The **frequency** of incident light (using different filters or light sources)
- The **intensity** of incident light (using neutral density filters or changing distance)
- The **potential difference** between cathode and anode (using the variable power supply)

**中文:**
经典的光电效应实验使用一个**真空玻璃管**，包含两个电极：**光电阴极**（被研究的金属表面）和**阳极**（收集极）。光电阴极连接到可变电源的负极端，阳极连接到正极端。一个灵敏的**微安表**测量光电流。单色光（单一频率）通过石英窗口（可透射紫外光）照射到光电阴极上。实验可以改变：
- 入射光的**频率**（使用不同的滤光片或光源）
- 入射光的**光强**（使用中性密度滤光片或改变距离）
- 阴极和阳极之间的**电势差**（使用可变电源）

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The experiment directly measures how electrons are ejected from a metal surface when light shines on it. The setup allows us to isolate and study the effects of light frequency, intensity, and applied voltage on the emission process.
**中文:** 该实验直接测量当光照射金属表面时电子如何被发射出来。该装置使我们能够分离并研究光的频率、光强和施加电压对发射过程的影响。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking the anode emits electrons — it's the **cathode** that emits
  - Believing the tube doesn't need to be evacuated — air molecules would scatter electrons
  - Confusing the direction of electron flow with conventional current direction
- **中文:**
  - 认为阳极发射电子——实际上是**阴极**发射
  - 认为管子不需要抽真空——空气分子会散射电子
  - 混淆电子流动方向与常规电流方向

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Draw the circuit diagram clearly showing cathode (negative), anode (positive), microammeter, and variable power supply. Label the photocathode and the incident light.
- **中文:** 清晰画出电路图，显示阴极（负极）、阳极（正极）、微安表和可变电源。标注光电阴极和入射光。

## 4.2 Effect of Intensity on Photocurrent / 光强对光电流的影响

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
When the frequency of incident light is **above the threshold frequency**, increasing the **intensity** of the light (more photons per second) causes a **proportional increase** in the **photocurrent**. This is because more photons mean more photoelectrons are emitted per second. However, the **maximum kinetic energy** of the photoelectrons remains **unchanged** — they are not ejected with greater speed. The stopping potential also remains constant.

**中文:**
当入射光的频率**高于截止频率**时，增加光的**光强**（每秒更多光子）会导致**光电流成比例增加**。这是因为更多的光子意味着每秒发射更多的光电子。然而，光电子的**最大动能**保持**不变**——它们不会以更大的速度被发射出来。遏止电势也保持不变。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** Intensity determines the **number** of photoelectrons, not their energy. Each photon ejects at most one electron, so more photons → more electrons → larger current.
**中文:** 光强决定光电子的**数量**，而不是它们的能量。每个光子最多发射一个电子，所以更多光子 → 更多电子 → 更大的电流。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Thinking higher intensity means electrons have more energy — this is a classical wave theory idea that is WRONG for the photoelectric effect.
- **中文:** 认为更高的光强意味着电子有更多能量——这是经典波动理论的想法，对于光电效应来说是**错误**的。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** State clearly: "Increasing intensity increases the photocurrent but does NOT affect the maximum kinetic energy or stopping potential."
- **中文:** 明确说明："增加光强会增加光电流，但不会影响最大动能或遏止电势。"

## 4.3 Effect of Frequency on Maximum Kinetic Energy / 频率对最大动能的影响

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
When the frequency of incident light is **above the threshold frequency**, increasing the **frequency** causes a **linear increase** in the **maximum kinetic energy** of photoelectrons. This is shown by the stopping potential increasing linearly with frequency. However, the **photocurrent** (number of electrons emitted per second) remains **constant** if the intensity is kept constant — because each photon still has the same probability of ejecting an electron.

**中文:**
当入射光的频率**高于截止频率**时，增加**频率**会导致光电子的**最大动能线性增加**。这表现为遏止电势随频率线性增加。然而，如果光强保持不变，**光电流**（每秒发射的电子数）保持**不变**——因为每个光子仍然有相同的概率发射一个电子。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** Frequency determines the **energy** of each photon ($E = hf$). Higher frequency photons carry more energy, which is transferred to the electrons as kinetic energy (after overcoming the work function).
**中文:** 频率决定每个光子的**能量**（$E = hf$）。更高频率的光子携带更多能量，这些能量在克服功函数后转化为电子的动能。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Thinking higher frequency means more electrons are emitted — frequency affects energy per electron, not the number of electrons.
- **中文:** 认为更高的频率意味着发射更多电子——频率影响每个电子的能量，而不是电子的数量。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Remember the key relationship: $KE_{max} = hf - \phi$. Frequency determines $KE_{max}$, intensity determines photocurrent.
- **中文:** 记住关键关系：$KE_{max} = hf - \phi$。频率决定$KE_{max}$，光强决定光电流。

## 4.4 Threshold Frequency / 截止频率

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
For each metal, there exists a **minimum frequency** of light below which **no photoelectrons are emitted**, regardless of how intense the light is. This is called the **threshold frequency** ($f_0$). If the frequency is below $f_0$, even the most intense light will not cause any emission. If the frequency is above $f_0$, even very dim light will cause immediate emission. The threshold frequency is a property of the metal — different metals have different threshold frequencies.

**中文:**
对于每种金属，存在一个**最低频率**，低于该频率时**没有光电子发射**，无论光有多强。这被称为**截止频率**（$f_0$）。如果频率低于$f_0$，即使最强的光也不会引起任何发射。如果频率高于$f_0$，即使非常微弱的光也会立即引起发射。截止频率是金属的特性——不同的金属有不同的截止频率。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The threshold frequency corresponds to the minimum energy a photon must have to overcome the **work function** ($\phi$) of the metal — the energy needed to remove an electron from the metal surface. At threshold: $hf_0 = \phi$.
**中文:** 截止频率对应于光子必须具有的最小能量，以克服金属的**功函数**（$\phi$）——从金属表面移除一个电子所需的能量。在截止频率处：$hf_0 = \phi$。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Thinking that very intense low-frequency light can eventually cause emission — this is WRONG. Below threshold frequency, NO emission occurs regardless of intensity.
- **中文:** 认为非常强的低频光最终能引起发射——这是**错误**的。低于截止频率，无论光强如何都不会发生发射。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** The threshold frequency is a key piece of evidence against classical wave theory. Memorize: $f_0 = \phi/h$.
- **中文:** 截止频率是反对经典波动理论的关键证据。记住：$f_0 = \phi/h$。

## 4.5 Instantaneous Emission / 瞬时发射

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
Photoelectrons are emitted **instantaneously** — within $10^{-9}$ seconds of the light striking the metal surface. There is **no measurable time delay** between the arrival of light and the emission of electrons, even for very low intensity light (as long as the frequency is above threshold). This is completely contrary to classical wave theory, which predicts a time delay for electrons to absorb enough energy from the wave.

**中文:**
光电子是**瞬时**发射的——在光照射到金属表面后的$10^{-9}$秒内。光的到达和电子的发射之间**没有可测量的时间延迟**，即使对于非常低强度的光也是如此（只要频率高于截止频率）。这与经典波动理论完全相反，经典波动理论预测电子需要时间延迟才能从波中吸收足够的能量。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The instantaneous emission shows that energy is transferred in discrete packets (photons), not continuously as waves. Each electron interacts with one photon at a time.
**中文:** 瞬时发射表明能量是以离散的包（光子）传递的，而不是像波那样连续传递。每个电子一次与一个光子相互作用。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Thinking there is a delay for very dim light — experiments show NO delay for any intensity above threshold.
- **中文:** 认为非常微弱的光有延迟——实验表明，对于任何高于截止频率的光强都没有延迟。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** This is a key argument against classical wave theory. State: "The instantaneous emission contradicts the wave theory prediction of a time delay for energy accumulation."
- **中文:** 这是反对经典波动理论的关键论点。说明："瞬时发射与波动理论预测的能量积累时间延迟相矛盾。"

## 4.6 Effect of Potential Difference on Photocurrent / 电势差对光电流的影响

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
When the anode is **positive** relative to the cathode (forward bias), photoelectrons are attracted to the anode, and the photocurrent reaches a **saturation value** where all emitted electrons are collected. As the anode becomes **negative** relative to the cathode (reverse bias), the photocurrent decreases because only electrons with sufficient kinetic energy can overcome the retarding potential. At the **stopping potential** ($V_s$), even the most energetic electrons cannot reach the anode, and the photocurrent becomes zero.

**中文:**
当阳极相对于阴极为**正**（正向偏压）时，光电子被吸引到阳极，光电流达到**饱和值**，此时所有发射的电子都被收集。当阳极相对于阴极为**负**（反向偏压）时，光电流减小，因为只有具有足够动能的电子才能克服减速电势。在**遏止电势**（$V_s$）处，即使能量最高的电子也无法到达阳极，光电流变为零。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The stopping potential directly measures the maximum kinetic energy of photoelectrons: $KE_{max} = eV_s$. By measuring $V_s$ for different frequencies, we can determine Planck's constant and the work function.
**中文:** 遏止电势直接测量光电子的最大动能：$KE_{max} = eV_s$。通过测量不同频率下的$V_s$，我们可以确定普朗克常数和功函数。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:** Confusing stopping potential with threshold frequency — stopping potential is a voltage, threshold frequency is a frequency.
- **中文:** 混淆遏止电势与截止频率——遏止电势是电压，截止频率是频率。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** The stopping potential is always positive when quoted as a magnitude. The applied voltage is negative (reverse bias) to stop electrons.
- **中文:** 遏止电势作为数值引用时总是正的。施加的电压是负的（反向偏压）来阻止电子。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Maximum Kinetic Energy from Stopping Potential / 从遏止电势求最大动能

$$ KE_{max} = eV_s $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $KE_{max}$ | Maximum kinetic energy of photoelectrons | 光电子的最大动能 | J (joules) |
| $e$ | Elementary charge ($1.60 \times 10^{-19}$ C) | 元电荷 | C (coulombs) |
| $V_s$ | Stopping potential | 遏止电势 | V (volts) |

**Derivation / 推导:** The work done by the electric field in stopping an electron is $W = eV_s$. This work equals the initial kinetic energy of the electron: $KE_{max} = eV_s$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Valid only when the photocurrent is reduced to exactly zero by the reverse bias.
- **中文:** 仅当反向偏压将光电流恰好减小到零时有效。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Assumes all kinetic energy is converted to electrical potential energy — no energy losses.
- **中文:** 假设所有动能都转化为电势能——没有能量损失。

## 5.2 Relationship between Stopping Potential and Frequency / 遏止电势与频率的关系

$$ V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\phi}{e} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $V_s$ | Stopping potential | 遏止电势 | V |
| $h$ | Planck's constant ($6.63 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s) | 普朗克常数 | J·s |
| $e$ | Elementary charge | 元电荷 | C |
| $f$ | Frequency of incident light | 入射光频率 | Hz |
| $\phi$ | Work function of the metal | 金属的功函数 | J |

**Derivation / 推导:** From Einstein's photoelectric equation: $hf = \phi + KE_{max}$. Substituting $KE_{max} = eV_s$ gives $hf = \phi + eV_s$, rearranged to $V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\phi}{e}$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Valid only when $f \geq f_0$ (above threshold frequency).
- **中文:** 仅当$f \geq f_0$（高于截止频率）时有效。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Assumes a clean metal surface with a single work function.
- **中文:** 假设具有单一功函数的清洁金属表面。

## 6.1 Photocurrent vs Potential Difference / 光电流与电势差的关系

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **x-axis:** Potential difference between anode and cathode (V) / 阳极和阴极之间的电势差 (V)
- **y-axis:** Photocurrent (I) / 光电流 (I)

### Shape / 形状
**English:** For a fixed frequency above threshold and fixed intensity, the graph shows:
- At large positive voltages: **saturation current** (all photoelectrons collected)
- As voltage decreases to zero: current remains at saturation
- At small negative voltages: current decreases as some electrons are repelled
- At the **stopping potential** ($V_s$): current reaches zero

**中文:** 对于高于截止频率的固定频率和固定光强，图形显示：
- 在大正电压处：**饱和电流**（所有光电子被收集）
- 当电压减小到零时：电流保持饱和
- 在小负电压处：电流减小，因为一些电子被排斥
- 在**遏止电势**（$V_s$）处：电流达到零

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
**English:** The gradient of the decreasing portion relates to the energy distribution of photoelectrons.
**中文:** 下降部分的斜率与光电子的能量分布有关。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
**English:** No meaningful area under this curve.
**中文:** 该曲线下没有有意义的面积。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
**English:** The stopping potential is the x-intercept (where I = 0). The saturation current is proportional to light intensity. Different curves for different intensities show different saturation currents but the SAME stopping potential.
**中文:** 遏止电势是x轴截距（I = 0处）。饱和电流与光强成正比。不同光强的不同曲线显示不同的饱和电流但**相同**的遏止电势。

## 6.2 Photocurrent vs Intensity / 光电流与光强的关系

### Axes / 坐标轴

- **y-axis:** Photocurrent (I_photo) / 光电流 (I_photo)

### Shape / 形状

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Interpreting Experimental Data / 解读实验数据

### Question / 题目
**English:**
In a photoelectric effect experiment, light of frequency $6.0 \times 10^{14}$ Hz is incident on a metal surface. The stopping potential is measured as 0.52 V. When the frequency is increased to $8.0 \times 10^{14}$ Hz, the stopping potential becomes 1.35 V.

(a) Explain what is meant by the term "stopping potential".
(b) Calculate the maximum kinetic energy of the photoelectrons for the first frequency.
(c) Determine Planck's constant from this data.
(d) Determine the work function of the metal.

**中文:**
在一个光电效应实验中，频率为$6.0 \times 10^{14}$ Hz的光照射到金属表面。测得遏止电势为0.52 V。当频率增加到$8.0 \times 10^{14}$ Hz时，遏止电势变为1.35 V。

(a) 解释"遏止电势"的含义。
(b) 计算第一个频率下光电子的最大动能。
(c) 从这些数据确定普朗克常数。
(d) 确定金属的功函数。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Explanation / 解释:**
**English:** The stopping potential is the minimum reverse potential difference that must be applied between the anode and cathode to reduce the photocurrent to zero. It is a measure of the maximum kinetic energy of the photoelectrons: $KE_{max} = eV_s$.

**中文:** 遏止电势是必须在阳极和阴极之间施加的最小反向电势差，以使光电流减小到零。它是光电子最大动能的量度：$KE_{max} = eV_s$。

**(b) Maximum kinetic energy / 最大动能:**
$$ KE_{max} = eV_s = (1.60 \times 10^{-19})(0.52) = 8.32 \times 10^{-20} \text{ J} $$

**Answer:** $8.32 \times 10^{-20}$ J | **答案：** $8.32 \times 10^{-20}$ J

**(c) Planck's constant / 普朗克常数:**
**English:** Using the equation $V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\phi}{e}$, the gradient of $V_s$ vs $f$ is $\frac{h}{e}$.

**中文:** 使用方程$V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\phi}{e}$，$V_s$对$f$的斜率是$\frac{h}{e}$。

$$ \text{Gradient} = \frac{\Delta V_s}{\Delta f} = \frac{1.35 - 0.52}{(8.0 - 6.0) \times 10^{14}} = \frac{0.83}{2.0 \times 10^{14}} = 4.15 \times 10^{-15} \text{ V·s} $$

$$ h = \text{Gradient} \times e = (4.15 \times 10^{-15})(1.60 \times 10^{-19}) = 6.64 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s} $$

**Answer:** $6.64 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s | **答案：** $6.64 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s

**(d) Work function / 功函数:**
**English:** Using $V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\phi}{e}$ with the first data point:

**中文:** 使用第一个数据点代入$V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\phi}{e}$：

$$ 0.52 = \frac{6.64 \times 10^{-34}}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}}(6.0 \times 10^{14}) - \frac{\phi}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}} $$

$$ 0.52 = 4.15 \times 10^{-15} \times 6.0 \times 10^{14} - \frac{\phi}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}} $$

$$ 0.52 = 2.49 - \frac{\phi}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}} $$

$$ \frac{\phi}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}} = 2.49 - 0.52 = 1.97 $$

$$ \phi = 1.97 \times 1.60 \times 10^{-19} = 3.15 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J} $$

**Answer:** $\phi = 3.15 \times 10^{-19}$ J | **答案：** $\phi = 3.15 \times 10^{-19}$ J

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) See explanation | (b) $8.32 \times 10^{-20}$ J | (c) $6.64 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s | (d) $3.15 \times 10^{-19}$ J
**答案：** (a) 见解释 | (b) $8.32 \times 10^{-20}$ J | (c) $6.64 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s | (d) $3.15 \times 10^{-19}$ J

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Always convert stopping potential to kinetic energy using $KE_{max} = eV_s$. The gradient of $V_s$ vs $f$ gives $h/e$, not $h$ directly.
**中文:** 始终使用$KE_{max} = eV_s$将遏止电势转换为动能。$V_s$对$f$的斜率给出$h/e$，而不是直接给出$h$。

## Example 2: Threshold Frequency Calculation / 截止频率计算

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A metal has a work function of $2.0 \times 10^{-19}$ J. Light of wavelength 500 nm is incident on the metal surface.

(a) Calculate the threshold frequency of the metal.
(b) Determine whether photoelectrons will be emitted.
(c) If photoelectrons are emitted, calculate their maximum kinetic energy.

**中文:**
一种金属的功函数为$2.0 \times 10^{-19}$ J。波长为500 nm的光照射到金属表面。

(a) 计算金属的截止频率。
(b) 确定是否会发射光电子。
(c) 如果发射光电子，计算它们的最大动能。

### Solution / 解答

**(a) Threshold frequency / 截止频率:**
$$ f_0 = \frac{\phi}{h} = \frac{2.0 \times 10^{-19}}{6.63 \times 10^{-34}} = 3.02 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz} $$

**Answer:** $3.02 \times 10^{14}$ Hz | **答案：** $3.02 \times 10^{14}$ Hz

**(b) Will photoelectrons be emitted? / 会发射光电子吗？**
**English:** First, find the frequency of the incident light:
$$ f = \frac{c}{\lambda} = \frac{3.00 \times 10^8}{500 \times 10^{-9}} = 6.00 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz} $$

Since $f = 6.00 \times 10^{14}$ Hz $> f_0 = 3.02 \times 10^{14}$ Hz, **yes**, photoelectrons will be emitted.

**中文:** 首先，求入射光的频率：
$$ f = \frac{c}{\lambda} = \frac{3.00 \times 10^8}{500 \times 10^{-9}} = 6.00 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz} $$

由于$f = 6.00 \times 10^{14}$ Hz $> f_0 = 3.02 \times 10^{14}$ Hz，**会**发射光电子。

**Answer:** Yes, photoelectrons will be emitted | **答案：** 会发射光电子

**(c) Maximum kinetic energy / 最大动能:**
$$ KE_{max} = hf - \phi = (6.63 \times 10^{-34})(6.00 \times 10^{14}) - 2.0 \times 10^{-19} $$
$$ KE_{max} = 3.98 \times 10^{-19} - 2.0 \times 10^{-19} = 1.98 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J} $$

**Answer:** $1.98 \times 10^{-19}$ J | **答案：** $1.98 \times 10^{-19}$ J

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) $3.02 \times 10^{14}$ Hz | (b) Yes | (c) $1.98 \times 10^{-19}$ J
**答案：** (a) $3.02 \times 10^{14}$ Hz | (b) 会 | (c) $1.98 \times 10^{-19}$ J

### Quick Tip / 提示
**English:** Always compare the incident frequency to the threshold frequency, not the wavelength. Convert wavelength to frequency first using $f = c/\lambda$.
**中文:** 始终将入射频率与截止频率比较，而不是波长。首先使用$f = c/\lambda$将波长转换为频率。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
The photoelectric effect experiment connects to practical skills in several ways:

1. **Measurements:** You need to measure stopping potential (using a voltmeter) and photocurrent (using a microammeter). Understanding the precision and uncertainty of these measurements is important.

2. **Graph Plotting:** Plotting $V_s$ against $f$ is a key skill. The graph should be a straight line, and you need to determine the gradient ($h/e$) and intercept ($-\phi/e$). Error bars should be considered.

3. **Experimental Design:** Key considerations include:
   - Using monochromatic light (filters or diffraction grating)
   - Ensuring the photocathode surface is clean (oxidation affects work function)
   - Using an evacuated tube to prevent electron collisions with air molecules
   - Using a quartz window (transmits UV light, unlike glass)

4. **Uncertainties:** The main sources of uncertainty are:
   - Determining the exact stopping potential (current never reaches exactly zero)
   - Frequency measurement (uncertainty in filter wavelength)
   - Contact potential differences between electrodes

5. **Data Analysis:** From the $V_s$ vs $f$ graph, you can determine:
   - Planck's constant ($h$) from the gradient
   - Work function ($\phi$) from the intercept
   - Threshold frequency ($f_0$) from the x-intercept

**中文:**
光电效应实验在以下几个方面与实验技能相关：

1. **测量：** 你需要测量遏止电势（使用电压表）和光电流（使用微安表）。理解这些测量的精度和不确定度很重要。

2. **绘图：** 绘制$V_s$对$f$的图是一项关键技能。图形应为一条直线，你需要确定斜率（$h/e$）和截距（$-\phi/e$）。应考虑误差棒。

3. **实验设计：** 关键考虑因素包括：
   - 使用单色光（滤光片或衍射光栅）
   - 确保光电阴极表面清洁（氧化会影响功函数）
   - 使用真空管以防止电子与空气分子碰撞
   - 使用石英窗口（可透射紫外光，玻璃不行）

4. **不确定度：** 不确定度的主要来源包括：
   - 确定准确的遏止电势（电流永远不会精确达到零）
   - 频率测量（滤光片波长的不确定度）
   - 电极之间的接触电势差

5. **数据分析：** 从$V_s$对$f$的图中，你可以确定：
   - 普朗克常数（$h$）来自斜率
   - 功函数（$\phi$）来自截距
   - 截止频率（$f_0$）来自x轴截距
