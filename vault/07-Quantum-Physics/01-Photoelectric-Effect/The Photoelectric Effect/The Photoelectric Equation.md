# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
The Photoelectric Equation, also known as Einstein's photoelectric equation, is the mathematical formulation that explains the photoelectric effect. It directly links the energy of incident photons to the kinetic energy of emitted electrons, incorporating the work function of the metal surface. This equation is the cornerstone of quantum physics as it provides the first direct evidence for the particle nature of light. It connects the [[Experimental Observations of the Photoelectric Effect]] with [[Einstein's Photon Model]], resolving the [[Failure of Classical Wave Theory]]. The equation is essential for understanding [[Stopping Potential and Maximum KE]] and forms the foundation for [[Wave-Particle Duality]].

**中文:**
光电效应方程，也称为爱因斯坦光电效应方程，是解释光电效应的数学表达式。它直接将入射光子的能量与发射电子的动能联系起来，并包含了金属表面的逸出功。这个方程是量子物理的基石，因为它首次直接证明了光的粒子性。它将[[Experimental Observations of the Photoelectric Effect]]与[[Einstein's Photon Model]]联系起来，解决了[[Failure of Classical Wave Theory]]。该方程对于理解[[Stopping Potential and Maximum KE]]至关重要，并为[[Wave-Particle Duality]]奠定了基础。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Energy Conservation in Photoelectric Emission / 光电发射中的能量守恒

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** The photoelectric equation is fundamentally an energy conservation statement. When a photon of energy $hf$ strikes a metal surface, its energy is transferred to an electron. Part of this energy ($\Phi$) is used to overcome the attractive forces binding the electron to the metal (the work function). The remaining energy appears as the kinetic energy of the emitted electron. For the most energetic electrons (those from the surface), this is $½mv²_{max}$. The equation can be written as:

$$hf = \Phi + \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$$

This equation explains all the key observations of the photoelectric effect: the existence of a [[Threshold Frequency and Work Function]], the independence of electron energy from intensity, and the instantaneous emission.

**中文:** 光电效应方程本质上是能量守恒的表述。当一个能量为 $hf$ 的光子撞击金属表面时，其能量被转移给一个电子。部分能量 ($\Phi$) 用于克服将电子束缚在金属上的吸引力（逸出功）。剩余的能量表现为发射电子的动能。对于能量最大的电子（来自表面的电子），这是 $½mv²_{max}$。该方程可写为：

$$hf = \Phi + \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$$

这个方程解释了光电效应的所有关键观察结果：[[Threshold Frequency and Work Function]]的存在、电子能量与强度无关以及瞬时发射。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The equation shows that the photoelectric effect is a one-to-one interaction between a photon and an electron. Each photon can only eject one electron, and the electron's kinetic energy depends only on the photon's frequency (energy), not on the number of photons (intensity). This is why increasing intensity increases the number of electrons (current) but not their maximum energy.

**中文:** 该方程表明光电效应是光子和电子之间的一对一相互作用。每个光子只能发射一个电子，电子的动能仅取决于光子的频率（能量），而不取决于光子数量（强度）。这就是为什么增加强度会增加电子数量（电流）但不会增加它们的最大能量。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking that all emitted electrons have the same kinetic energy (they have a range from 0 to maximum)
  - Believing that increasing intensity increases the maximum kinetic energy (it only increases the number of electrons)
  - Confusing the work function with the ionization energy of an atom
- **中文:**
  - 认为所有发射的电子都具有相同的动能（它们的范围从0到最大值）
  - 认为增加强度会增加最大动能（它只会增加电子数量）
  - 将逸出功与原子的电离能混淆

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Always write the equation in its full form: $hf = \Phi + \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$. Remember that $hf$ is the energy of ONE photon. When using the stopping potential form, use $eV_s = \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$.
- **中文:** 始终以完整形式写出方程：$hf = \Phi + \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$。记住 $hf$ 是一个光子的能量。使用遏止电压形式时，使用 $eV_s = \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$。

## 4.2 Stopping Potential Form / 遏止电压形式

### Explanation / 解释
**English:** The stopping potential $V_s$ is the voltage needed to stop the most energetic photoelectrons. Since the work done by the electric field equals the loss in kinetic energy:

$$eV_s = \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$$

Substituting into the photoelectric equation gives:

$$hf = \Phi + eV_s$$

Rearranging:

$$V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\Phi}{e}$$

This linear relationship between $V_s$ and $f$ is crucial for experimental verification. The gradient of the $V_s$ vs $f$ graph is $h/e$, allowing Planck's constant to be determined experimentally.

**中文:** 遏止电压 $V_s$ 是阻止能量最大的光电子所需的电压。由于电场做的功等于动能的损失：

$$eV_s = \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$$

代入光电效应方程得到：

$$hf = \Phi + eV_s$$

重新排列：

$$V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\Phi}{e}$$

$V_s$ 和 $f$ 之间的这种线性关系对于实验验证至关重要。$V_s$ 与 $f$ 关系图的斜率为 $h/e$，从而可以通过实验确定普朗克常数。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The stopping potential form directly links measurable electrical quantities (voltage) to quantum properties (photon frequency). It provides a practical method to determine Planck's constant and the work function of different metals. The y-intercept of the graph gives $-\Phi/e$, from which the work function can be calculated.

**中文:** 遏止电压形式直接将可测量的电学量（电压）与量子特性（光子频率）联系起来。它提供了一种确定不同金属的普朗克常数和逸出功的实用方法。图的y轴截距给出 $-\Phi/e$，由此可以计算逸出功。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - Thinking stopping potential is the voltage applied to accelerate electrons (it's to stop them)
  - Confusing the sign of the stopping potential (it's negative relative to the emitter)
  - Forgetting that $e$ is the elementary charge ($1.6 \times 10^{-19}$ C)
- **中文:**
  - 认为遏止电压是加速电子的电压（它是用来阻止电子的）
  - 混淆遏止电压的符号（相对于发射极是负的）
  - 忘记 $e$ 是元电荷 ($1.6 \times 10^{-19}$ C)

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** When given a graph of $V_s$ vs $f$, remember: gradient = $h/e$, y-intercept = $-\Phi/e$, x-intercept = $f_0$ (threshold frequency). Use these to find $h$, $\Phi$, and $f_0$.
- **中文:** 当给出 $V_s$ 与 $f$ 的关系图时，记住：斜率 = $h/e$，y轴截距 = $-\Phi/e$，x轴截距 = $f_0$（阈值频率）。用这些来求 $h$、$\Phi$ 和 $f_0$。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Photoelectric Equation (Energy Form) / 光电效应方程（能量形式）

$$hf = \Phi + \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $h$ | Planck's constant | 普朗克常数 | J·s |
| $f$ | Frequency of incident radiation | 入射辐射的频率 | Hz |
| $\Phi$ | Work function of the metal | 金属的逸出功 | J |
| $m$ | Mass of electron ($9.11 \times 10^{-31}$ kg) | 电子质量 | kg |
| $v_{max}$ | Maximum speed of emitted electrons | 发射电子的最大速度 | m·s⁻¹ |

**Derivation / 推导:** Based on energy conservation: photon energy = work function + maximum kinetic energy of emitted electron.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Only valid when $f \geq f_0$ (frequency ≥ threshold frequency). Assumes one photon interacts with one electron.
- **中文:** 仅当 $f \geq f_0$（频率 ≥ 阈值频率）时有效。假设一个光子与一个电子相互作用。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Does not account for the distribution of electron kinetic energies (only gives maximum). Does not explain why some electrons require more energy than the work function to escape.
- **中文:** 不考虑电子动能的分布（只给出最大值）。不解释为什么有些电子需要比逸出功更多的能量才能逃逸。

## 5.2 Photoelectric Equation (Stopping Potential Form) / 光电效应方程（遏止电压形式）

$$hf = \Phi + eV_s$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $e$ | Elementary charge ($1.60 \times 10^{-19}$ C) | 元电荷 | C |
| $V_s$ | Stopping potential | 遏止电压 | V |

**Derivation / 推导:** From $eV_s = \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$, substitute into $hf = \Phi + \frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Same as energy form. $V_s$ is the magnitude of the stopping potential (positive value).
- **中文:** 与能量形式相同。$V_s$ 是遏止电压的大小（正值）。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Assumes all kinetic energy is converted to electrical potential energy. Neglects any energy losses.
- **中文:** 假设所有动能都转化为电势能。忽略任何能量损失。

## 5.3 Linear Form for Graph Analysis / 用于图形分析的线性形式

$$V_s = \frac{h}{e}f - \frac{\Phi}{e}$$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $h/e$ | Gradient of $V_s$ vs $f$ graph | $V_s$ 与 $f$ 关系图的斜率 | V·s |
| $-\Phi/e$ | y-intercept of $V_s$ vs $f$ graph | $V_s$ 与 $f$ 关系图的y轴截距 | V |

**Derivation / 推导:** Rearranging $hf = \Phi + eV_s$ to make $V_s$ the subject.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Only valid for $f \geq f_0$. Below threshold frequency, no photoelectrons are emitted.
- **中文:** 仅对 $f \geq f_0$ 有效。低于阈值频率时，没有光电子发射。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Assumes a perfect linear relationship. Experimental data may show slight deviations due to contact potentials or surface contamination.
- **中文:** 假设完美的线性关系。由于接触电势或表面污染，实验数据可能显示轻微偏差。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating Maximum Kinetic Energy / 计算最大动能

### Question / 题目
**English:** A metal surface has a work function of $2.3 \text{ eV}$. Light of wavelength $450 \text{ nm}$ is incident on the surface. Calculate:
(a) The threshold frequency of the metal
(b) The maximum kinetic energy of the emitted photoelectrons in joules
(c) The stopping potential

(Use: $h = 6.63 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$, $c = 3.00 \times 10^8 \text{ m·s}^{-1}$, $e = 1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$, $1 \text{ eV} = 1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$)

**中文:** 某金属表面的逸出功为 $2.3 \text{ eV}$。波长为 $450 \text{ nm}$ 的光入射到该表面。计算：
(a) 该金属的阈值频率
(b) 发射光电子的最大动能（以焦耳为单位）
(c) 遏止电压

（使用：$h = 6.63 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$，$c = 3.00 \times 10^8 \text{ m·s}^{-1}$，$e = 1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$，$1 \text{ eV} = 1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Convert work function to joules / 将逸出功转换为焦耳**
$$\Phi = 2.3 \text{ eV} \times 1.60 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J/eV} = 3.68 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$$

**Step 2: Calculate threshold frequency / 计算阈值频率**
$$f_0 = \frac{\Phi}{h} = \frac{3.68 \times 10^{-19}}{6.63 \times 10^{-34}} = 5.55 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$$

**Step 3: Calculate photon frequency / 计算光子频率**
$$f = \frac{c}{\lambda} = \frac{3.00 \times 10^8}{450 \times 10^{-9}} = 6.67 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$$

**Step 4: Calculate photon energy / 计算光子能量**
$$E = hf = (6.63 \times 10^{-34})(6.67 \times 10^{14}) = 4.42 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$$

**Step 5: Apply photoelectric equation / 应用光电效应方程**
$$\frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max} = hf - \Phi = 4.42 \times 10^{-19} - 3.68 \times 10^{-19} = 7.40 \times 10^{-20} \text{ J}$$

**Step 6: Calculate stopping potential / 计算遏止电压**
$$V_s = \frac{\frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max}}{e} = \frac{7.40 \times 10^{-20}}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}} = 0.463 \text{ V}$$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (a) $f_0 = 5.55 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$ | **答案：** (a) $f_0 = 5.55 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$
(b) $\frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max} = 7.40 \times 10^{-20} \text{ J}$ | (b) $\frac{1}{2}mv^2_{max} = 7.40 \times 10^{-20} \text{ J}$
(c) $V_s = 0.463 \text{ V}$ | (c) $V_s = 0.463 \text{ V}$

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always check if the photon frequency is above the threshold frequency before applying the equation. If $f < f_0$, no photoelectrons are emitted.
- **中文:** 在应用方程之前，始终检查光子频率是否高于阈值频率。如果 $f < f_0$，则没有光电子发射。

## Example 2: Graph Analysis / 图形分析

### Question / 题目
**English:** In an experiment to determine Planck's constant, the following data was obtained for the stopping potential at different frequencies:

| Frequency ($\times 10^{14}$ Hz) | 5.0 | 6.0 | 7.0 | 8.0 | 9.0 |
|--------------------------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| Stopping Potential (V) | 0.20 | 0.62 | 1.04 | 1.46 | 1.88 |

(a) Plot a graph of stopping potential against frequency.
(b) Use the graph to determine:
   (i) Planck's constant
   (ii) The work function of the metal in eV
   (iii) The threshold frequency

**中文:** 在确定普朗克常数的实验中，获得了不同频率下的遏止电压数据：

| 频率 ($\times 10^{14}$ Hz) | 5.0 | 6.0 | 7.0 | 8.0 | 9.0 |
|---------------------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 遏止电压 (V) | 0.20 | 0.62 | 1.04 | 1.46 | 1.88 |

(a) 绘制遏止电压与频率的关系图。
(b) 使用该图确定：
   (i) 普朗克常数
   (ii) 金属的逸出功（以eV为单位）
   (iii) 阈值频率

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Plot the graph / 绘制图形**
- **English:** Plot $V_s$ on y-axis (scale: 0 to 2.0 V) and $f$ on x-axis (scale: 4.0 to 10.0 × 10¹⁴ Hz). Draw a best-fit straight line.
- **中文:** 在y轴上绘制 $V_s$（刻度：0到2.0 V），在x轴上绘制 $f$（刻度：4.0到10.0 × 10¹⁴ Hz）。绘制最佳拟合直线。

**Step 2: Calculate gradient / 计算斜率**
$$\text{gradient} = \frac{\Delta V_s}{\Delta f} = \frac{1.88 - 0.20}{(9.0 - 5.0) \times 10^{14}} = \frac{1.68}{4.0 \times 10^{14}} = 4.20 \times 10^{-15} \text{ V·s}$$

**Step 3: Calculate Planck's constant / 计算普朗克常数**
$$h = e \times \text{gradient} = (1.60 \times 10^{-19})(4.20 \times 10^{-15}) = 6.72 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$$

**Step 4: Find y-intercept / 求y轴截距**
- **English:** Extend the line to $f = 0$. From the graph, y-intercept ≈ -1.90 V
- **中文:** 将直线延伸到 $f = 0$。从图中，y轴截距 ≈ -1.90 V

**Step 5: Calculate work function / 计算逸出功**
$$\Phi = -e \times (\text{y-intercept}) = -(1.60 \times 10^{-19})(-1.90) = 3.04 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$$
$$\Phi \text{ in eV} = \frac{3.04 \times 10^{-19}}{1.60 \times 10^{-19}} = 1.90 \text{ eV}$$

**Step 6: Find threshold frequency / 求阈值频率**
- **English:** From the graph, x-intercept (where $V_s = 0$) ≈ $4.5 \times 10^{14}$ Hz
- **中文:** 从图中，x轴截距（$V_s = 0$ 处）≈ $4.5 \times 10^{14}$ Hz

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** (i) $h = 6.72 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$ | **答案：** (i) $h = 6.72 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$
(ii) $\Phi = 1.90 \text{ eV}$ | (ii) $\Phi = 1.90 \text{ eV}$
(iii) $f_0 = 4.5 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$ | (iii) $f_0 = 4.5 \times 10^{14} \text{ Hz}$

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** The gradient of $V_s$ vs $f$ graph is ALWAYS $h/e$, regardless of the metal. Different metals give different y-intercepts but the same gradient.
- **中文:** $V_s$ 与 $f$ 关系图的斜率始终是 $h/e$，与金属无关。不同的金属给出不同的y轴截距但相同的斜率。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
The photoelectric equation is directly tested through experimental determination of Planck's constant. Key practical skills include:

1. **Measurements:** Using a photocell circuit to measure stopping potential at different frequencies. Requires accurate measurement of voltage (using a voltmeter) and frequency (using monochromatic light sources or filters).

2. **Uncertainties:** 
   - Uncertainty in frequency determination from wavelength measurements
   - Uncertainty in stopping potential measurement (finding the exact voltage where current becomes zero)
   - Error propagation when calculating $h$ from gradient

3. **Graph Plotting:** 
   - Plotting $V_s$ against $f$ with appropriate scales
   - Drawing best-fit straight line
   - Determining gradient and intercept with uncertainties
   - Using error bars to show measurement uncertainties

4. **Experimental Design:**
   - Using different colored filters to vary frequency
   - Using different metal cathodes to compare work functions
   - Ensuring the photocell is in a vacuum to prevent collisions with air molecules
   - Using a sensitive microammeter to detect when current reaches zero

5. **Data Analysis:**
   - Converting between eV and J
   - Using $V_s = (h/e)f - \Phi/e$ to find $h$ and $\Phi$
   - Comparing experimental value of $h$ with accepted value ($6.63 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s)

**中文:**
光电效应方程通过实验测定普朗克常数直接进行测试。关键实验技能包括：

1. **测量：** 使用光电管电路测量不同频率下的遏止电压。需要精确测量电压（使用电压表）和频率（使用单色光源或滤光片）。

2. **不确定度：**
   - 从波长测量确定频率的不确定度
   - 遏止电压测量的不确定度（找到电流变为零的确切电压）
   - 从斜率计算 $h$ 时的误差传播

3. **图形绘制：**
   - 用适当的刻度绘制 $V_s$ 与 $f$ 的关系图
   - 绘制最佳拟合直线
   - 确定斜率和截距及其不确定度
   - 使用误差条显示测量不确定度

4. **实验设计：**
   - 使用不同颜色的滤光片改变频率
   - 使用不同的金属阴极比较逸出功
   - 确保光电管处于真空中以防止与空气分子碰撞
   - 使用灵敏的微安计检测电流何时达到零

5. **数据分析：**
   - 在eV和J之间转换
   - 使用 $V_s = (h/e)f - \Phi/e$ 求 $h$ 和 $\Phi$
   - 将 $h$ 的实验值与公认值 ($6.63 \times 10^{-34}$ J·s) 进行比较
