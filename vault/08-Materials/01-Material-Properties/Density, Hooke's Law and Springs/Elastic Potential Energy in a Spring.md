# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic explores the **energy stored** in a deformed spring. When a force is applied to stretch or compress a spring, work is done against the spring's restoring force, and this work is stored as **elastic potential energy (EPE)**. This concept bridges the gap between [[Hooke's Law and the Spring Constant]] and the principle of conservation of energy. Understanding EPE is crucial for analyzing oscillating systems (like mass-spring systems) and impact scenarios. It is a foundational concept for the later topic of [[Stress, Strain and Young Modulus]].

**中文:**
本子知识点探讨了变形弹簧中**储存的能量**。当施加力拉伸或压缩弹簧时，外力克服弹簧的回复力做功，这些功以**弹性势能 (EPE)** 的形式储存。这个概念连接了[[Hooke's Law and the Spring Constant]]与能量守恒原理。理解弹性势能对于分析振动系统（如弹簧-质量系统）和碰撞场景至关重要。它是后续学习[[Stress, Strain and Young Modulus]]的基础概念。

# 3. Core Definitions / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Elastic Potential Energy (EPE)** / 弹性势能 | The energy stored in a deformed elastic object (like a spring) due to work done in changing its shape. | 由于改变形状所做的功而储存在变形弹性物体（如弹簧）中的能量。 | Confusing EPE with work done *by* the spring. EPE is stored *in* the spring. |
| **Work Done on a Spring** / 对弹簧做的功 | The energy transferred to the spring by an external force to stretch or compress it. | 外力为拉伸或压缩弹簧而转移给弹簧的能量。 | Thinking work done is always equal to $Fx$. For a spring, the force is not constant, so work done = average force × distance = $\frac{1}{2}Fx$. |
| **Force-Extension Graph** / 力-伸长量图 | A graph plotting the applied force (or tension) against the extension (or compression) of the spring. | 绘制施加力（或张力）与弹簧伸长量（或压缩量）关系的图表。 | Forgetting that the area under this graph represents work done, which equals EPE only if the spring is elastic and no energy is lost to heat. |
| **Conservation of Energy** / 能量守恒 | The principle that energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transferred from one form to another. | 能量既不会凭空产生，也不会凭空消失，它只会从一种形式转化为另一种形式。 | Failing to account for all forms of energy (e.g., gravitational potential energy, kinetic energy, elastic potential energy) in a closed system. |

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 Derivation of Elastic Potential Energy / 弹性势能的推导

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The elastic potential energy (EPE) stored in a spring is derived from the work done to stretch it. Because the force required to stretch a spring obeying [[Hooke's Law and the Spring Constant]] ($F = kx$) increases linearly with extension, the work done is not simply $F \times x$. Instead, we must use the **average force**.

The average force during stretching from 0 to extension $x$ is $\frac{F_{initial} + F_{final}}{2} = \frac{0 + kx}{2} = \frac{1}{2}kx$.

Work done, $W = \text{average force} \times \text{distance} = (\frac{1}{2}kx) \times x = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$.

Since this work is stored as elastic potential energy, $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$. Substituting $F = kx$ gives the alternative form $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}Fx$.

**中文:**
储存在弹簧中的弹性势能 (EPE) 是从拉伸它所做的功推导出来的。因为拉伸一个遵守[[Hooke's Law and the Spring Constant]] ($F = kx$) 的弹簧所需的力随伸长量线性增加，所以所做的功不仅仅是 $F \times x$。相反，我们必须使用**平均力**。

从0拉伸到伸长量 $x$ 过程中的平均力是 $\frac{F_{初始} + F_{最终}}{2} = \frac{0 + kx}{2} = \frac{1}{2}kx$。

所做的功，$W = \text{平均力} \times \text{距离} = (\frac{1}{2}kx) \times x = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$。

由于这个功以弹性势能的形式储存，所以 $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$。代入 $F = kx$ 得到另一种形式 $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}Fx$。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The EPE represents the capacity of the stretched/compressed spring to do work when released. A stiffer spring ($k$ is larger) or a more stretched spring ($x$ is larger) stores more energy.
**中文:** 弹性势能代表了拉伸/压缩的弹簧在释放时做功的能力。更硬的弹簧（$k$ 更大）或拉伸得更长的弹簧（$x$ 更大）储存更多能量。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking EPE is $Fx$ instead of $\frac{1}{2}Fx$. The force is not constant.
- **CN:** 认为弹性势能是 $Fx$ 而不是 $\frac{1}{2}Fx$。力不是恒定的。
- **EN:** Forgetting that $x$ is the extension/compression from the natural length, not the total length.
- **CN:** 忘记 $x$ 是从自然长度的伸长/压缩量，而不是总长度。
- **EN:** Applying the formula to a spring that has exceeded its [[Elastic Limit and Plastic Deformation]]. The formula is only valid for elastic deformation.
- **CN:** 将公式应用于已超过其[[Elastic Limit and Plastic Deformation]]的弹簧。该公式仅对弹性变形有效。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** Always state the assumption that the spring obeys Hooke's Law when using $E = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$.
- **CN:** 在使用 $E = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$ 时，始终说明假设弹簧遵守胡克定律。
- **EN:** The area under a force-extension graph is the work done. For an elastic spring, this equals the EPE stored.
- **CN:** 力-伸长量图下的面积是所做的功。对于弹性弹簧，这等于储存的弹性势能。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## Equation 1: Elastic Potential Energy (Form 1)

$$ E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2} F x $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $E_{pe}$ | Elastic Potential Energy | 弹性势能 | J (Joules) |
| $F$ | Force applied to the spring (or tension in spring) | 施加在弹簧上的力（或弹簧中的张力） | N (Newtons) |
| $x$ | Extension or compression from natural length | 从自然长度的伸长或压缩量 | m (metres) |

## Equation 2: Elastic Potential Energy (Form 2)

$$ E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2} k x^2 $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $E_{pe}$ | Elastic Potential Energy | 弹性势能 | J (Joules) |
| $k$ | Spring constant (stiffness) | 弹簧常数（劲度系数） | N/m |
| $x$ | Extension or compression from natural length | 从自然长度的伸长或压缩量 | m (metres) |

**Derivation / 推导:**
From Hooke's Law, $F = kx$. The work done (and hence EPE) is the area under the $F-x$ graph, which is a triangle: $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height} = \frac{1}{2} \times x \times F = \frac{1}{2}Fx$. Substituting $F = kx$ gives $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}(kx)x = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **EN:** The spring must obey Hooke's Law (i.e., be within its [[Elastic Limit and Plastic Deformation]]). The deformation must be elastic.
- **CN:** 弹簧必须遵守胡克定律（即，在其[[Elastic Limit and Plastic Deformation]]范围内）。变形必须是弹性的。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **EN:** The formula does not apply if the spring is permanently deformed. It assumes no energy is lost to heat within the material.
- **CN:** 如果弹簧发生永久变形，该公式不适用。它假设材料内部没有能量以热量形式损失。

## 6.1 Force-Extension Graph and EPE / 力-伸长量图与弹性势能

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **X-axis:** Extension, $x$ / 伸长量, $x$ (m)
- **Y-axis:** Force, $F$ / 力, $F$ (N)

### Shape / 形状
- **EN:** A straight line through the origin (for a spring obeying Hooke's Law).
- **CN:** 一条通过原点的直线（对于遵守胡克定律的弹簧）。

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
- **EN:** The gradient of the line is the spring constant, $k$.
- **CN:** 直线的斜率是弹簧常数，$k$。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
- **EN:** The area under the line up to a given extension $x$ represents the work done to stretch the spring to that extension. This work is stored as **Elastic Potential Energy**.
- **CN:** 直线下到给定伸长量 $x$ 的面积代表将弹簧拉伸到该伸长量所做的功。这个功以**弹性势能**的形式储存。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **EN:** If asked to find the EPE from a graph, calculate the area of the triangle under the line. If the graph is not a straight line (beyond the limit of proportionality), the EPE is the total area under the curve, but the formula $\frac{1}{2}kx^2$ no longer applies.
- **CN:** 如果要求从图中找出弹性势能，计算线下三角形的面积。如果图形不是直线（超出比例极限），弹性势能是曲线下的总面积，但公式 $\frac{1}{2}kx^2$ 不再适用。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Calculating EPE / 计算弹性势能

### Question / 题目
**English:** A spring has a spring constant of $500 \text{ N/m}$. It is stretched by $0.04 \text{ m}$. Calculate the elastic potential energy stored in the spring.
**中文:** 一个弹簧的弹簧常数为 $500 \text{ N/m}$。它被拉伸了 $0.04 \text{ m}$。计算储存在弹簧中的弹性势能。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1:** Identify the known quantities.
$k = 500 \text{ N/m}$, $x = 0.04 \text{ m}$.

**Step 2:** Choose the appropriate formula.
$E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2} k x^2$

**Step 3:** Substitute and calculate.
$E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2} \times 500 \times (0.04)^2$
$E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2} \times 500 \times 0.0016$
$E_{pe} = 250 \times 0.0016$
$E_{pe} = 0.4 \text{ J}$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $0.4 \text{ J}$ | **答案：** $0.4 \text{ J}$

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Always square the extension ($x^2$) before multiplying by $k$ and $\frac{1}{2}$.
- **CN:** 在乘以 $k$ 和 $\frac{1}{2}$ 之前，务必先对伸长量进行平方 ($x^2$)。

## Example 2: Conservation of Energy with a Spring / 弹簧的能量守恒

### Question / 题目
**English:** A $0.2 \text{ kg}$ block is dropped from a height of $0.5 \text{ m}$ onto a vertical spring with $k = 100 \text{ N/m}$. The spring is initially at its natural length. Calculate the maximum compression of the spring. (Assume $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$ and no energy loss).
**中文:** 一个 $0.2 \text{ kg}$ 的物块从 $0.5 \text{ m}$ 的高度落到一个 $k = 100 \text{ N/m}$ 的竖直弹簧上。弹簧初始处于自然长度。计算弹簧的最大压缩量。（假设 $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$ 且无能量损失）。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1:** Define the system and principle.
The system is the block, spring, and Earth. By conservation of energy, the loss in gravitational potential energy (GPE) of the block equals the gain in elastic potential energy (EPE) of the spring.

**Step 2:** Write the energy equation.
Loss in GPE = Gain in EPE
$mg(h + x) = \frac{1}{2} k x^2$
Where $h = 0.5 \text{ m}$ is the drop height before hitting the spring, and $x$ is the maximum compression of the spring. The total fall distance is $h + x$.

**Step 3:** Substitute known values.
$0.2 \times 10 \times (0.5 + x) = \frac{1}{2} \times 100 \times x^2$
$2(0.5 + x) = 50 x^2$
$1 + 2x = 50 x^2$

**Step 4:** Rearrange into a quadratic equation.
$50x^2 - 2x - 1 = 0$

**Step 5:** Solve the quadratic using the formula $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.
$a = 50, b = -2, c = -1$
$x = \frac{2 \pm \sqrt{(-2)^2 - 4(50)(-1)}}{2(50)}$
$x = \frac{2 \pm \sqrt{4 + 200}}{100}$
$x = \frac{2 \pm \sqrt{204}}{100}$
$x = \frac{2 \pm 14.28}{100}$

**Step 6:** Choose the positive root (compression is positive).
$x = \frac{2 + 14.28}{100} = \frac{16.28}{100} = 0.1628 \text{ m}$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $0.163 \text{ m}$ (to 3 s.f.) | **答案：** $0.163 \text{ m}$ (3位有效数字)

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Don't forget the additional GPE lost as the block compresses the spring ($mgx$). This is a common mistake.
- **CN:** 不要忘记物块在压缩弹簧时损失的额外重力势能 ($mgx$)。这是一个常见错误。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
This sub-topic is directly linked to the core practical for investigating [[Hooke's Law and the Spring Constant]].
- **Measurements:** You will measure force (using masses and a newtonmeter) and extension (using a ruler).
- **Graph Plotting:** You will plot a force-extension graph. The area under this graph is the EPE.
- **Uncertainties:** The uncertainty in EPE comes from uncertainties in $F$ and $x$. Since $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}Fx$, the percentage uncertainty in EPE is the sum of the percentage uncertainties in $F$ and $x$.
- **Experimental Design:** You could design an experiment to verify conservation of energy by dropping a mass onto a spring and measuring the compression, comparing the calculated EPE to the calculated loss in GPE.

**中文:**
本子知识点与探究[[Hooke's Law and the Spring Constant]]的核心实验直接相关。
- **测量：** 你将测量力（使用砝码和弹簧测力计）和伸长量（使用尺子）。
- **图表绘制：** 你将绘制力-伸长量图。该图下的面积就是弹性势能。
- **不确定度：** 弹性势能的不确定度来自 $F$ 和 $x$ 的不确定度。由于 $E_{pe} = \frac{1}{2}Fx$，弹性势能的百分比不确定度是 $F$ 和 $x$ 的百分比不确定度之和。
- **实验设计：** 你可以设计一个实验来验证能量守恒，通过将一个质量块落到弹簧上并测量压缩量，将计算出的弹性势能与计算出的重力势能损失进行比较。
