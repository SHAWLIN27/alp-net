# 3. Core Definitions / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Luminosity (L)** / 光度 (L) | The total power (energy per second) radiated by a star. Often expressed in units of solar luminosity ($L_\odot$). | 恒星每秒辐射的总功率（能量）。通常以太阳光度 ($L_\odot$) 为单位表示。 | Confusing luminosity with apparent brightness. Luminosity is intrinsic; brightness depends on distance. |
| **Surface Temperature (T)** / 表面温度 (T) | The temperature of a star's photosphere, determined from its black body spectrum using Wien's displacement law. | 恒星光球层的温度，通过维恩位移定律从其黑体辐射谱确定。 | Thinking temperature increases from left to right. It is reversed: hot on the left. |
| **Spectral Class** / 光谱类型 | A classification system (OBAFGKM) based on the absorption lines in a star's spectrum, which correlates with surface temperature. | 基于恒星光谱中吸收线的分类系统 (OBAFGKM)，与表面温度相关。 | Forgetting the order: O (hottest) to M (coolest). |
| **Main Sequence** / 主序带 | The continuous band of stars on the H-R diagram where stars are fusing hydrogen into helium in their cores. The Sun is a main sequence star. | 赫罗图上的一条连续带状区域，恒星在此区域的核心将氢聚变为氦。太阳是一颗主序星。 | Thinking all stars on the main sequence have the same size. They vary greatly in mass and radius. |
| **Red Giant** / 红巨星 | A large, cool, luminous star in the upper-right region of the H-R diagram, after hydrogen in the core is exhausted. | 赫罗图右上区域的一颗大而冷、光度高的恒星，核心氢耗尽后形成。 | Confusing giants with supergiants. Giants are less luminous than supergiants. |
| **White Dwarf** / 白矮星 | A small, hot, low-luminosity star in the lower-left region of the H-R diagram, the final remnant of low-to-intermediate mass stars. | 赫罗图左下区域的一颗小而热、光度低的恒星，是低质量到中等质量恒星的最终残骸。 | Thinking white dwarfs are large because they are hot. They are extremely small (Earth-sized). |

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The Axes: Luminosity and Temperature / 坐标轴：光度和温度

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The H-R diagram is a scatter plot of stars. The **y-axis** is **Luminosity ($L$)** , usually measured in units of the Sun's luminosity ($L_\odot$). The **x-axis** is **Surface Temperature ($T$)** , measured in Kelvin (K), or equivalently **Spectral Class** (OBAFGKM). The axes are **logarithmic** because the range of stellar luminosities and temperatures is enormous (e.g., luminosity ranges from $10^{-4} L_\odot$ to $10^6 L_\odot$). A logarithmic scale compresses this range, making the diagram manageable. The temperature axis is **reversed**: the hottest stars (O-type, $T > 30,000$ K) are on the **left**, and the coolest stars (M-type, $T < 3,500$ K) are on the **right**. This convention was chosen historically because spectral classes were plotted before temperature was understood.

**中文:**
赫罗图是恒星的散点图。**y轴**是**光度 ($L$)** ，通常以太阳光度 ($L_\odot$) 为单位。**x轴**是**表面温度 ($T$)** ，以开尔文 (K) 为单位，或者等效地使用**光谱类型** (OBAFGKM)。坐标轴是**对数**的，因为恒星的光度和温度范围极其巨大（例如，光度范围从 $10^{-4} L_\odot$ 到 $10^6 L_\odot$）。对数尺度压缩了这个范围，使图表易于处理。温度轴是**反向**的：最热的恒星（O型，$T > 30,000$ K）在**左边**，最冷的恒星（M型，$T < 3,500$ K）在**右边**。这个惯例是历史上形成的，因为在理解温度之前，光谱类型就已经被绘制出来了。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The axes directly relate to two fundamental stellar properties: the total energy output (luminosity) and the surface temperature. The Stefan-Boltzmann law ($L = 4\pi R^2 \sigma T^4$) links these to a third property: the star's radius ($R$). Therefore, a star's position on the H-R diagram encodes its radius. Lines of constant radius (isoradius lines) are diagonal lines on the log-log plot.

**中文:**
坐标轴直接关联到两个基本的恒星属性：总能量输出（光度）和表面温度。斯特藩-玻尔兹曼定律 ($L = 4\pi R^2 \sigma T^4$) 将这些与第三个属性——恒星的半径 ($R$) 联系起来。因此，恒星在赫罗图上的位置编码了其半径。等半径线在对数-对数图上是对角线。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking the x-axis is "temperature increasing from left to right." It is the opposite.
- **CN:** 认为x轴是“温度从左到右增加”。事实正好相反。
- **EN:** Thinking the axes are linear. They are logarithmic.
- **CN:** 认为坐标轴是线性的。它们是对数的。
- **EN:** Confusing luminosity with apparent brightness. The H-R diagram uses absolute luminosity.
- **CN:** 混淆光度和视亮度。赫罗图使用绝对光度。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** When sketching the H-R diagram, always label the axes with units (e.g., $L/L_\odot$ and $T/K$). Show the direction of increasing temperature with an arrow pointing left.
- **CN:** 在画赫罗图草图时，始终标注坐标轴的单位（例如，$L/L_\odot$ 和 $T/K$）。用一个指向左边的箭头表示温度增加的方向。

## 4.2 The Main Regions / 主要区域

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The H-R diagram is divided into four main regions:
1.  **Main Sequence (MS):** A diagonal band running from the top-left (hot, luminous) to the bottom-right (cool, dim). About 90% of stars, including the [[Sun]], lie on the MS. These stars are fusing hydrogen in their cores.
2.  **Red Giants (RG):** Located in the top-right region (cool, luminous). These are stars that have exhausted core hydrogen and are fusing hydrogen in a shell around an inert helium core. They are much larger than MS stars of the same temperature.
3.  **Red Supergiants (RSG):** Above the red giants, even more luminous. These are very massive stars in a later stage of evolution.
4.  **White Dwarfs (WD):** Located in the bottom-left region (hot, dim). These are the final remnants of low-to-intermediate mass stars. They are extremely dense and small.

**中文:**
赫罗图分为四个主要区域：
1.  **主序带 (MS):** 一条从左上角（热、亮）延伸到右下角（冷、暗）的对角线带状区域。大约90%的恒星，包括[[太阳]]，都位于主序带上。这些恒星在其核心进行氢聚变。
2.  **红巨星 (RG):** 位于右上角区域（冷、亮）。这些是核心氢已耗尽，正在惰性氦核周围的壳层中进行氢聚变的恒星。它们比相同温度的主序星大得多。
3.  **红超巨星 (RSG):** 位于红巨星之上，光度更高。这些是处于演化后期阶段的超大质量恒星。
4.  **白矮星 (WD):** 位于左下角区域（热、暗）。这些是低质量到中等质量恒星的最终残骸。它们极其致密且体积小。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The position of a star on the H-R diagram is determined by its mass, age, and evolutionary stage. The Main Sequence is a mass sequence: more massive stars are hotter and more luminous. Giants and supergiants are evolved stars with large radii. White dwarfs are dead stars with very small radii.

**中文:**
恒星在赫罗图上的位置由其质量、年龄和演化阶段决定。主序带是一个质量序列：质量越大的恒星越热、光度越高。巨星和超巨星是半径很大的演化恒星。白矮星是半径非常小的死亡恒星。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking that stars move along the main sequence as they age. They stay on the MS until core hydrogen is depleted.
- **CN:** 认为恒星随着年龄增长会沿着主序带移动。它们在核心氢耗尽之前一直停留在主序带上。
- **EN:** Thinking all red stars are giants. Red dwarfs (M-type MS stars) are cool but small and dim.
- **CN:** 认为所有红色恒星都是巨星。红矮星（M型主序星）很冷，但体积小且暗淡。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** Be able to sketch the H-R diagram and label the four regions. Know the relative positions of the Sun, Sirius, Betelgeuse, and a typical white dwarf.
- **CN:** 能够画出赫罗图草图并标注四个区域。知道太阳、天狼星、参宿四和典型白矮星的相对位置。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Stefan-Boltzmann Law / 斯特藩-玻尔兹曼定律

$$ L = 4\pi R^2 \sigma T^4 $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $L$ | Luminosity of the star | 恒星的光度 | W (or $L_\odot$) |
| $R$ | Radius of the star | 恒星的半径 | m (or $R_\odot$) |
| $\sigma$ | Stefan-Boltzmann constant ($5.67 \times 10^{-8} \text{ W m}^{-2} \text{ K}^{-4}$) | 斯特藩-玻尔兹曼常数 | $\text{W m}^{-2} \text{ K}^{-4}$ |
| $T$ | Surface temperature of the star | 恒星的表面温度 | K |

**Derivation / 推导:** This is a fundamental law of black body radiation. The power radiated per unit surface area is $\sigma T^4$. Multiplying by the total surface area of a sphere ($4\pi R^2$) gives the total power (luminosity).

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **EN:** Assumes the star is a perfect black body. This is a good approximation for most stars.
- **CN:** 假设恒星是理想黑体。这对大多数恒星来说是一个很好的近似。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **EN:** Does not account for non-thermal emission (e.g., from magnetic fields or accretion disks).
- **CN:** 不考虑非热辐射（例如，来自磁场或吸积盘的辐射）。

## 5.2 Luminosity Ratio / 光度比

$$ \frac{L}{L_\odot} = \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 \left(\frac{T}{T_\odot}\right)^4 $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $L_\odot$ | Solar luminosity ($3.83 \times 10^{26} \text{ W}$) | 太阳光度 | W |
| $R_\odot$ | Solar radius ($6.96 \times 10^8 \text{ m}$) | 太阳半径 | m |
| $T_\odot$ | Solar surface temperature ($5,778 \text{ K}$) | 太阳表面温度 | K |

**Derivation / 推导:** Dividing the Stefan-Boltzmann law for a star by that for the Sun.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **EN:** Useful for comparing a star's properties to the Sun's.
- **CN:** 用于将恒星的属性与太阳进行比较。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **EN:** Requires knowing the star's radius, which is not directly measurable for most stars.
- **CN:** 需要知道恒星的半径，而大多数恒星的半径无法直接测量。

# 6. Graphs and Relationships / 图表与关系

## 6.1 The H-R Diagram / 赫罗图

### Axes / 坐标轴
- **Y-axis:** Luminosity ($L/L_\odot$) — logarithmic scale.
- **X-axis:** Surface Temperature (K) or Spectral Class — logarithmic scale, reversed (hot on left).

### Shape / 形状
- **Main Sequence:** A diagonal band from top-left to bottom-right.
- **Red Giants/Supergiants:** A cluster in the top-right.
- **White Dwarfs:** A cluster in the bottom-left.

### Gradient Meaning / 斜率含义
- **EN:** The slope of the main sequence is not constant, but it generally follows a power law: $L \propto T^\alpha$, where $\alpha$ varies with mass. For Sun-like stars, $L \propto M^{3.5}$.
- **CN:** 主序带的斜率不是恒定的，但通常遵循幂律：$L \propto T^\alpha$，其中 $\alpha$ 随质量变化。对于类太阳恒星，$L \propto M^{3.5}$。

### Area Meaning / 面积含义
- **EN:** The area of the diagram is not directly meaningful, but the density of stars in a region indicates the relative abundance of that type of star.
- **CN:** 图表的面积没有直接意义，但某个区域的恒星密度表明了该类型恒星的相对丰度。

### Exam Interpretation / 考试解读
- **EN:** Given a star's luminosity and temperature, you should be able to place it on the H-R diagram and identify its region. Given two of the three properties ($L, T, R$), you can use the Stefan-Boltzmann law to find the third.
- **CN:** 给定恒星的光度和温度，你应该能够将其放置在赫罗图上并识别其区域。给定三个属性 ($L, T, R$) 中的两个，你可以使用斯特藩-玻尔兹曼定律求出第三个。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Identifying a Star's Region / 示例1：识别恒星区域

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A star has a surface temperature of 4,000 K and a luminosity of $10^4 L_\odot$. In which region of the H-R diagram is this star located? What type of star is it?

**中文:**
一颗恒星的表面温度为 4,000 K，光度为 $10^4 L_\odot$。这颗恒星位于赫罗图的哪个区域？它是什么类型的恒星？

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1:** Recall the H-R diagram axes. Temperature 4,000 K is cool (right side). Luminosity $10^4 L_\odot$ is very high (top).

**Step 2:** The top-right region of the H-R diagram is occupied by Red Giants and Red Supergiants. A luminosity of $10^4 L_\odot$ is too high for a typical Red Giant (which are around $10^2 - 10^3 L_\odot$), so this is likely a **Red Supergiant**.

**Step 3:** Confirm using the Stefan-Boltzmann law to estimate radius:
$$ \frac{L}{L_\odot} = \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 \left(\frac{T}{T_\odot}\right)^4 $$
$$ 10^4 = \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 \left(\frac{4000}{5778}\right)^4 $$
$$ \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 = \frac{10^4}{(0.692)^4} = \frac{10^4}{0.229} \approx 4.37 \times 10^4 $$
$$ \frac{R}{R_\odot} \approx \sqrt{4.37 \times 10^4} \approx 209 $$
The radius is about 209 solar radii, which is characteristic of a supergiant.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** Red Supergiant | **答案：** 红超巨星

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Use the temperature and luminosity to locate the star on the H-R diagram. Cool + Luminous = Giant/Supergiant. Hot + Dim = White Dwarf.
- **CN:** 使用温度和光度在赫罗图上定位恒星。冷 + 亮 = 巨星/超巨星。热 + 暗 = 白矮星。

## Example 2: Using the H-R Diagram to Find Radius / 示例2：使用赫罗图求半径

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A star on the main sequence has a surface temperature of 10,000 K and a luminosity of $100 L_\odot$. Calculate its radius in solar radii.

**中文:**
一颗主序星的表面温度为 10,000 K，光度为 $100 L_\odot$。计算其以太阳半径表示的半径。

### Solution / 解答
**Step 1:** Use the luminosity ratio form of the Stefan-Boltzmann law:
$$ \frac{L}{L_\odot} = \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 \left(\frac{T}{T_\odot}\right)^4 $$

**Step 2:** Substitute values:
$$ 100 = \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 \left(\frac{10000}{5778}\right)^4 $$

**Step 3:** Calculate the temperature ratio:
$$ \frac{10000}{5778} \approx 1.73 $$
$$ (1.73)^4 \approx 8.96 $$

**Step 4:** Solve for $R/R_\odot$:
$$ \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 = \frac{100}{8.96} \approx 11.16 $$
$$ \frac{R}{R_\odot} \approx \sqrt{11.16} \approx 3.34 $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $R \approx 3.34 R_\odot$ | **答案：** $R \approx 3.34 R_\odot$

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Always check if your answer makes sense. A hotter, more luminous main sequence star should have a larger radius than the Sun.
- **CN:** 始终检查你的答案是否合理。一颗更热、更亮的主序星应该比太阳有更大的半径。

While you cannot directly plot stars in a school lab, the H-R diagram is a powerful tool for data analysis. In practical papers, you may be asked to:
- Plot data (luminosity vs. temperature) on a logarithmic graph.
- Interpret a given H-R diagram to determine the properties of stars.
- Estimate uncertainties in luminosity and temperature from observational data.
- Use the H-R diagram to test hypotheses about stellar evolution.

**中文:**
虽然你无法在学校实验室直接绘制恒星，但赫罗图是一个强大的数据分析工具。在实验考试中，你可能会被要求：
- 在对数图上绘制数据（光度 vs. 温度）。
- 解读给定的赫罗图以确定恒星的属性。
- 从观测数据中估计光度和温度的不确定度。
- 使用赫罗图来检验关于恒星演化的假设。

# 12. Quick Revision Sheet / 速查表

| Category / 类别 | Key Points / 要点 |
|----------------|------------------|
| **Definition / 定义** | A plot of Luminosity (y-axis) vs. Surface Temperature (x-axis). / 光度（y轴）与表面温度（x轴）的关系图。 |
| **Key Formula / 核心公式** | $L = 4\pi R^2 \sigma T^4$; $\frac{L}{L_\odot} = \left(\frac{R}{R_\odot}\right)^2 \left(\frac{T}{T_\odot}\right)^4$ |
| **Key Graph / 核心图表** | H-R Diagram: Main Sequence (diagonal), Red Giants (top-right), Red Supergiants (top-right, above giants), White Dwarfs (bottom-left). / 赫罗图：主序带（对角线）、红巨星（右上）、红超巨星（右上，巨星之上）、白矮星（左下）。 |
| **Axes Rules / 坐标轴规则** | Y-axis: Logarithmic. X-axis: Logarithmic and Reversed (hot left, cool right). / y轴：对数。x轴：对数且反向（左热右冷）。 |
| **Exam Tip / 考试提示** | Always sketch the H-R diagram with correct axes and regions. Use the Stefan-Boltzmann law to relate $L$, $T$, and $R$. / 始终画出带有正确坐标轴和区域的赫罗图。使用斯特藩-玻尔兹曼定律关联 $L$、$T$ 和 $R$。 |
