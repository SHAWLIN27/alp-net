# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
This sub-topic introduces the **spectral classification** of stars, specifically the **OBAFGKM sequence**. This sequence is a fundamental tool in astrophysics that categorizes stars based on their surface temperature, which directly determines the absorption lines observed in their spectra. The sequence is a core component of the [[The Hertzsprung-Russell Diagram]], as spectral class is the primary label on the horizontal axis (x-axis) of the H-R diagram. Understanding OBAFGKM is essential for interpreting stellar properties like temperature, color, and luminosity, and it forms the bridge between [[Black Body Radiation]] and the [[Main Sequence Stars]] on the H-R diagram. The mnemonic "Oh Be A Fine Girl/Guy, Kiss Me" is a classic way to remember the order from hottest (O) to coolest (M).

**中文:**
本子知识点介绍恒星的**光谱分类**，特别是 **OBAFGKM 序列**。该序列是天体物理学中的一个基本工具，根据恒星的表面温度对恒星进行分类，而表面温度直接决定了在其光谱中观测到的吸收线。该序列是 [[赫罗图]] 的核心组成部分，因为光谱类型是赫罗图水平轴（x轴）上的主要标签。理解 OBAFGKM 对于解释恒星特性（如温度、颜色和光度）至关重要，并且它构成了 [[黑体辐射]] 与赫罗图上 [[主序星]] 之间的桥梁。助记符 "Oh Be A Fine Girl/Guy, Kiss Me" 是记住从最热（O）到最冷（M）顺序的经典方法。

# 3. Core Definitions / 核心定义

| Term (EN/CN) | Definition (EN) | Definition (CN) | Common Mistakes / 常见错误 |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **Spectral Class** / 光谱类型 | A classification of stars based on the pattern of absorption lines in their spectrum, which is primarily determined by surface temperature. | 根据恒星光谱中吸收线的模式对恒星进行的分类，这主要由表面温度决定。 | Confusing spectral class with luminosity class (e.g., main sequence vs. giant). |
| **OBAFGKM Sequence** / OBAFGKM 序列 | The sequence of spectral classes ordered from hottest (O) to coolest (M). | 从最热 (O) 到最冷 (M) 排列的光谱类型序列。 | Forgetting the order; mixing up which end is hot vs. cold. |

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The OBAFGKM Sequence / OBAFGKM 序列

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The spectral classification of stars is based on the strength and pattern of [[Absorption Lines]] in their spectra. These lines are caused by atoms and ions in the star's cool outer atmosphere absorbing specific wavelengths of light from the hot interior. The strength of these lines depends critically on the star's **surface temperature**. The OBAFGKM sequence orders stars from hottest (O) to coolest (M). Each class is further subdivided into 10 subclasses (e.g., B0, B1, ..., B9, A0, A1...). Our Sun is a G2 star.

- **O-type (30,000 - 50,000 K):** Hottest, blue-white. Show lines of ionized helium (He II) and highly ionized atoms. Hydrogen lines are weak.
- **B-type (10,000 - 30,000 K):** Blue-white. Show lines of neutral helium (He I) and hydrogen lines are stronger.
- **A-type (7,500 - 10,000 K):** White. Show the strongest hydrogen ([[Balmer Lines]]) lines. Also show lines of ionized metals like Ca II.
- **F-type (6,000 - 7,500 K):** Yellow-white. Hydrogen lines are weaker. Lines of ionized metals (e.g., Ca II, Fe II) become stronger.
- **G-type (5,000 - 6,000 K):** Yellow. Show strong lines of ionized calcium (Ca II) and many neutral metals. Hydrogen lines are weaker. (Our Sun is G2).
- **K-type (3,500 - 5,000 K):** Orange. Show strong lines of neutral metals (e.g., Fe I, Ca I). Molecular bands (e.g., TiO) begin to appear.
- **M-type (< 3,500 K):** Coolest, red. Show strong molecular bands (e.g., TiO, VO). Lines of neutral metals are very strong.

**中文:**
恒星的光谱分类基于其光谱中[[吸收线]]的强度和模式。这些线是由恒星较冷的外层大气中的原子和离子吸收来自炽热内部的特定波长光引起的。这些线的强度关键取决于恒星的**表面温度**。OBAFGKM 序列将恒星从最热 (O) 排列到最冷 (M)。每个类别进一步细分为 10 个子类（例如，B0, B1, ..., B9, A0, A1...）。我们的太阳是一颗 G2 型星。

- **O 型 (30,000 - 50,000 K):** 最热，蓝白色。显示电离氦 (He II) 和高度电离原子的谱线。氢线较弱。
- **B 型 (10,000 - 30,000 K):** 蓝白色。显示中性氦 (He I) 的谱线，氢线更强。
- **A 型 (7,500 - 10,000 K):** 白色。显示最强的氢 ([[巴尔末线]]) 谱线。也显示电离金属（如 Ca II）的谱线。
- **F 型 (6,000 - 7,500 K):** 黄白色。氢线较弱。电离金属（例如 Ca II, Fe II）的谱线变得更强。
- **G 型 (5,000 - 6,000 K):** 黄色。显示强烈的电离钙 (Ca II) 谱线和许多中性金属谱线。氢线较弱。（我们的太阳是 G2 型）。
- **K 型 (3,500 - 5,000 K):** 橙色。显示强中性金属（例如 Fe I, Ca I）谱线。分子带（例如 TiO）开始出现。
- **M 型 (< 3,500 K):** 最冷，红色。显示强分子带（例如 TiO, VO）。中性金属谱线非常强。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:**
The spectral class is a direct indicator of a star's surface temperature. The pattern of absorption lines changes with temperature because the ionization and excitation states of atoms depend on temperature. For example, hydrogen Balmer lines are strongest at around 10,000 K (A-type stars) because at this temperature, a large fraction of hydrogen atoms are in the first excited state (n=2), which is required for Balmer absorption. In hotter stars (O, B), hydrogen is mostly ionized; in cooler stars (F, G, K, M), most hydrogen is in the ground state (n=1).

**中文:**
光谱类型是恒星表面温度的直接指标。吸收线的模式随温度变化，因为原子的电离和激发状态取决于温度。例如，氢巴尔末线在约 10,000 K（A 型星）时最强，因为在此温度下，很大一部分氢原子处于第一激发态 (n=2)，这是巴尔末吸收所必需的。在较热的恒星（O, B）中，氢大部分被电离；在较冷的恒星（F, G, K, M）中，大多数氢处于基态 (n=1)。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **EN:** Thinking that the OBAFGKM sequence is based on the *amount* of an element present. It is based on the *temperature*, which determines which absorption lines are visible.
- **中文:** 认为 OBAFGKM 序列是基于某种元素的*含量*。它是基于*温度*，温度决定了哪些吸收线可见。
- **EN:** Confusing spectral class with luminosity class (I-V). A star can be a G2 main sequence star (like the Sun) or a G2 giant star.
- **中文:** 混淆光谱类型和光度类型 (I-V)。一颗恒星可以是 G2 主序星（如太阳）或 G2 巨星。
- **EN:** Forgetting that O is the hottest and M is the coolest.
- **中文:** 忘记 O 是最热的，M 是最冷的。

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **EN:** Memorize the OBAFGKM sequence and a mnemonic. Be able to state the temperature range and color for each class.
- **中文:** 记住 OBAFGKM 序列和一个助记符。能够说出每个类别的温度范围和颜色。
- **EN:** Know which spectral class shows the strongest hydrogen Balmer lines (A-type).
- **中文:** 知道哪个光谱类型显示最强的氢巴尔末线（A 型）。
- **EN:** Be able to use an H-R diagram to determine the spectral class of a star given its temperature or color.
- **中文:** 能够使用赫罗图，根据给定的温度或颜色确定恒星的光谱类型。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

There is no single equation for spectral class. The key relationship is between spectral class and temperature, which is derived from [[Black Body Radiation]] and [[Wien's Displacement Law]].

$$ \lambda_{\text{max}} = \frac{2.898 \times 10^{-3}}{T} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $\lambda_{\text{max}}$ | Peak wavelength of emission | 发射峰值波长 | m |
| $T$ | Surface temperature | 表面温度 | K |

**Derivation / 推导:** This is [[Wien's Displacement Law]], which relates the peak wavelength of a black body's emission to its temperature. Hotter stars (O, B) have a shorter peak wavelength (blue/UV), while cooler stars (K, M) have a longer peak wavelength (red/IR).

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **EN:** Stars are approximated as black bodies.
- **中文:** 恒星被近似为黑体。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **EN:** Real stellar spectra have absorption lines superimposed on the black body continuum. The peak wavelength is still a good indicator of temperature.
- **中文:** 真实的恒星光谱在黑体连续谱上叠加了吸收线。峰值波长仍然是温度的良好指标。

## 7.1 The H-R Diagram with Spectral Classes / 带有光谱类型的赫罗图

### Description / 描述
- **EN:** The [[The Hertzsprung-Russell Diagram]] is a scatter plot of stars. The x-axis is spectral class (or temperature), increasing to the left (hotter stars on the left). The y-axis is luminosity (or absolute magnitude), increasing upwards. The main sequence runs diagonally from top-left (hot, luminous) to bottom-right (cool, dim). # 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Identifying Spectral Class from Temperature / 从温度识别光谱类型

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A star has a surface temperature of 8,000 K. What is its spectral class? What color does it appear? What type of absorption lines would be strongest in its spectrum?

**中文:**
一颗恒星的表面温度为 8,000 K。它的光谱类型是什么？它呈现什么颜色？在其光谱中，哪种类型的吸收线最强？

### Solution / 解答
1.  **Identify the temperature range:** 8,000 K falls within the range for A-type stars (7,500 - 10,000 K).
2.  **Determine the color:** A-type stars appear white.
3.  **Identify the strongest absorption lines:** A-type stars show the strongest hydrogen Balmer lines.

**解答步骤:**
1.  **确定温度范围:** 8,000 K 落在 A 型星 (7,500 - 10,000 K) 的范围内。
2.  **确定颜色:** A 型星呈现白色。
3.  **确定最强的吸收线:** A 型星显示最强的氢巴尔末线。

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** Spectral class: A. Color: White. Strongest lines: Hydrogen Balmer lines. | **答案：** 光谱类型：A。颜色：白色。最强谱线：氢巴尔末线。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** Memorize the temperature ranges for each spectral class. A common exam question is to give a temperature and ask for the spectral class.
- **中文:** 记住每个光谱类型的温度范围。一个常见的考试题目是给出温度并询问光谱类型。

## Example 2: Using the H-R Diagram / 使用赫罗图

### Question / 题目
**English:**
On an H-R diagram, a star is located on the main sequence with a spectral class of G2. What is its approximate luminosity compared to the Sun? What is its approximate surface temperature?

**中文:**
在赫罗图上，一颗恒星位于主序带上，光谱类型为 G2。与太阳相比，它的近似光度是多少？它的近似表面温度是多少？

### Solution / 解答
1.  **Identify the star:** The Sun is a G2 main sequence star.
2.  **Determine luminosity:** The Sun's luminosity is defined as 1 L☉.
3.  **Determine temperature:** G-type stars have a temperature range of 5,000 - 6,000 K. G2 is near the hotter end, so approximately 5,800 K.

**解答步骤:**
1.  **确定恒星:** 太阳是一颗 G2 主序星。
2.  **确定光度:** 太阳的光度定义为 1 L☉。
3.  **确定温度:** G 型星的温度范围为 5,000 - 6,000 K。G2 接近较热的一端，因此约为 5,800 K。

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** Luminosity: 1 L☉. Temperature: ~5,800 K. | **答案：** 光度：1 L☉。温度：~5,800 K。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **EN:** The Sun is a standard reference point. Know its spectral class (G2), temperature (~5,800 K), and luminosity (1 L☉).
- **中文:** 太阳是一个标准参考点。知道它的光谱类型 (G2)、温度 (~5,800 K) 和光度 (1 L☉)。
