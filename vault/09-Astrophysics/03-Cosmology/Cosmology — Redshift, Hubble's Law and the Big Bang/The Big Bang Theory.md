# The Big Bang Theory / 大爆炸理论

---

# 1. Overview / 概述

**English:**
The Big Bang Theory is the prevailing cosmological model describing the origin and evolution of the universe. It proposes that the universe began approximately 13.8 billion years ago from an extremely hot, dense singularity and has been expanding and cooling ever since. This sub-topic explores the key evidence supporting the Big Bang model, including [[Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation (CMB)]], [[Redshift and the Expanding Universe]], and the abundance of light elements. Understanding the Big Bang is essential for grasping how the universe evolved from its earliest moments to its current state, and it connects directly to [[Hubble's Law (v = H₀d)]] and the [[The Fate of the Universe]].

**中文:**
大爆炸理论是描述宇宙起源和演化的主流宇宙学模型。它提出宇宙大约在138亿年前从一个极其炽热、致密的奇点开始，并一直在膨胀和冷却。本子知识点探讨支持大爆炸模型的关键证据，包括[[宇宙微波背景辐射]]、[[红移与膨胀宇宙]]以及轻元素的丰度。理解大爆炸对于掌握宇宙如何从最初时刻演化到当前状态至关重要，并且它直接与[[哈勃定律 (v = H₀d)]]和[[宇宙的命运]]相关联。

# 4. Key Concepts Explained / 关键概念详解

## 4.1 The Timeline of the Big Bang / 大爆炸的时间线

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
The Big Bang is not an explosion in space but the expansion of space itself. The timeline can be divided into key epochs:

1. **Planck Epoch (0 to 10⁻⁴³ s):** The universe is a singularity. All four fundamental forces (gravity, electromagnetism, strong nuclear, weak nuclear) are unified. Physics as we know it breaks down.
2. **Grand Unification Epoch (10⁻⁴³ to 10⁻³⁶ s):** Gravity separates from the other forces.
3. **Inflationary Epoch (10⁻³⁶ to 10⁻³² s):** The universe undergoes exponential expansion, growing from subatomic to macroscopic size. This explains the uniformity of the CMB.
4. **Electroweak Epoch (10⁻³² to 10⁻¹² s):** The strong nuclear force separates. Quarks and leptons form.
5. **Quark Epoch (10⁻¹² to 10⁻⁶ s):** The universe is a quark-gluon plasma.
6. **Hadron Epoch (10⁻⁶ to 1 s):** Quarks combine to form protons and neutrons.
7. **Lepton Epoch (1 to 10 s):** Leptons (electrons, neutrinos) dominate.
8. **Primordial Nucleosynthesis (10 s to 20 min):** Protons and neutrons fuse to form light nuclei (H, He, Li).
9. **Recombination (≈ 380,000 years):** Electrons combine with nuclei to form neutral atoms. The universe becomes transparent. The CMB is released.
10. **Dark Ages (380,000 to 150 million years):** No stars yet; only neutral gas.
11. **First Stars and Galaxies (150 million years onward):** Gravity pulls matter together, forming stars and galaxies.

**中文:**
大爆炸不是空间中的爆炸，而是空间本身的膨胀。时间线可以分为关键时代：

1. **普朗克时代（0到10⁻⁴³秒）：** 宇宙是奇点。所有四种基本力（引力、电磁力、强核力、弱核力）统一。我们已知的物理定律失效。
2. **大统一时代（10⁻⁴³到10⁻³⁶秒）：** 引力与其他力分离。
3. **暴胀时代（10⁻³⁶到10⁻³²秒）：** 宇宙经历指数膨胀，从亚原子大小增长到宏观尺寸。这解释了CMB的均匀性。
4. **电弱时代（10⁻³²到10⁻¹²秒）：** 强核力分离。夸克和轻子形成。
5. **夸克时代（10⁻¹²到10⁻⁶秒）：** 宇宙是夸克-胶子等离子体。
6. **强子时代（10⁻⁶到1秒）：** 夸克结合形成质子和中子。
7. **轻子时代（1到10秒）：** 轻子（电子、中微子）主导。
8. **原始核合成（10秒到20分钟）：** 质子和中子融合形成轻原子核（H、He、Li）。
9. **复合（约38万年）：** 电子与原子核结合形成中性原子。宇宙变得透明。CMB被释放。
10. **黑暗时代（38万到1.5亿年）：** 没有恒星；只有中性气体。
11. **第一批恒星和星系（1.5亿年后）：** 引力将物质聚集在一起，形成恒星和星系。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** The timeline shows that the universe evolved from a simple, hot, dense state to the complex, cool, structured state we observe today. Each epoch represents a phase transition or change in the dominant physical processes.

**中文:** 时间线显示宇宙从一个简单、炽热、致密的状态演化到我们今天观察到的复杂、寒冷、有结构的状态。每个时代代表一个相变或主导物理过程的变化。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - The Big Bang was an explosion in space. (It was the expansion of space itself.)
  - The singularity was a point in space. (It was the entire universe.)
  - The Big Bang happened at a specific location. (It happened everywhere.)
- **中文:**
  - 大爆炸是空间中的爆炸。（它是空间本身的膨胀。）
  - 奇点是空间中的一个点。（它是整个宇宙。）
  - 大爆炸发生在特定位置。（它发生在任何地方。）

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** Memorize the key epochs and their approximate times. Be able to explain why inflation is needed (to solve the horizon and flatness problems). Know that the CMB was released at recombination.
- **中文:** 记住关键时代及其近似时间。能够解释为什么需要暴胀（解决视界和平坦性问题）。知道CMB是在复合时释放的。

## 4.2 Evidence for the Big Bang / 大爆炸的证据

### Explanation / 解释
**English:**
Three main pieces of evidence support the Big Bang theory:

1. **Expansion of the Universe ([[Hubble's Law (v = H₀d)]]):** Galaxies are moving away from us, with recessional velocity proportional to distance. This implies the universe was once smaller and denser. Extrapolating backward gives an age of ~13.8 billion years.

2. **Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) ([[Cosmic Microwave Background Radiation (CMB)]]):** A nearly uniform microwave signal at 2.7 K, observed in all directions. This is the "afterglow" of the Big Bang, released when the universe became transparent at recombination (~380,000 years). Its blackbody spectrum and uniformity strongly support the Big Bang model.

3. **Abundance of Light Elements (Primordial Nucleosynthesis):** The observed ratios of hydrogen (~75%), helium (~24%), and trace amounts of lithium and deuterium match predictions from Big Bang nucleosynthesis. Stars cannot produce these ratios; they must have been produced in the early universe.

**中文:**
三个主要证据支持大爆炸理论：

1. **宇宙的膨胀（[[哈勃定律 (v = H₀d)]]）：** 星系正在远离我们，退行速度与距离成正比。这意味着宇宙曾经更小、更致密。向后外推给出约138亿年的年龄。

2. **宇宙微波背景（CMB）（[[宇宙微波背景辐射]]）：** 在所有方向观测到的约2.7 K的几乎均匀的微波信号。这是大爆炸的"余辉"，在复合时（约38万年）宇宙变得透明时释放。其黑体光谱和均匀性强烈支持大爆炸模型。

3. **轻元素的丰度（原始核合成）：** 观测到的氢（约75%）、氦（约24%）以及微量锂和氘的比例与大爆炸核合成的预测相符。恒星无法产生这些比例；它们必须在早期宇宙中产生。

### Physical Meaning / 物理意义
**English:** These three pieces of evidence form a "triple test" for the Big Bang model. Each independently points to a hot, dense early universe. Together, they provide overwhelming support.

**中文:** 这三个证据构成了大爆炸模型的"三重检验"。每个都独立指向一个炽热、致密的早期宇宙。它们共同提供了压倒性的支持。

### Common Misconceptions / 常见误区
- **English:**
  - The CMB is from the Big Bang itself. (It's from recombination, ~380,000 years later.)
  - The CMB is uniform because it's from a single source. (It's uniform because the universe was in thermal equilibrium.)
  - Light elements are produced in stars. (Most helium and all deuterium are primordial.)
- **中文:**
  - CMB来自大爆炸本身。（它来自复合，约38万年后。）
  - CMB均匀是因为它来自单一来源。（它均匀是因为宇宙处于热平衡。）
  - 轻元素在恒星中产生。（大多数氦和所有氘是原始的。）

### Exam Tips / 考试提示
- **English:** For each piece of evidence, be able to state: (1) what it is, (2) how it's observed, (3) what it tells us about the early universe. Use the phrase "consistent with the Big Bang model" in your answers.
- **中文:** 对于每个证据，能够说明：（1）它是什么，（2）如何观测，（3）它告诉我们关于早期宇宙的什么。在答案中使用"与大爆炸模型一致"这个短语。

# 5. Essential Equations / 核心公式

## 5.1 Age of the Universe from Hubble's Constant / 从哈勃常数计算宇宙年龄

$$ t_0 = \frac{1}{H_0} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $t_0$ | Age of the universe | 宇宙年龄 | s or years |
| $H_0$ | Hubble's constant | 哈勃常数 | s⁻¹ or km s⁻¹ Mpc⁻¹ |

**Derivation / 推导:**
From Hubble's law: $v = H_0 d$. If we assume constant expansion rate, the time since the Big Bang is $t = d/v = d/(H_0 d) = 1/H_0$.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Assumes constant expansion rate (simplified model). In reality, expansion has slowed due to gravity and accelerated due to dark energy.
- **中文:** 假设膨胀速率恒定（简化模型）。实际上，膨胀因引力而减速，因暗能量而加速。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** Gives an approximate age. Modern measurements use more complex models incorporating dark energy and matter density.
- **中文:** 给出近似年龄。现代测量使用更复杂的模型，包含暗能量和物质密度。

**Example Calculation / 示例计算:**
If $H_0 = 70 \text{ km s}^{-1} \text{ Mpc}^{-1}$:
- Convert to s⁻¹: $1 \text{ Mpc} = 3.086 \times 10^{19} \text{ km}$, so $H_0 = 70 / (3.086 \times 10^{19}) \approx 2.27 \times 10^{-18} \text{ s}^{-1}$
- $t_0 = 1 / (2.27 \times 10^{-18}) \approx 4.41 \times 10^{17} \text{ s}$
- Convert to years: $4.41 \times 10^{17} / (3.156 \times 10^7) \approx 1.4 \times 10^{10} \text{ years} = 14 \text{ billion years}$

## 5.2 Redshift and Scale Factor / 红移与尺度因子

$$ 1 + z = \frac{\lambda_{\text{observed}}}{\lambda_{\text{emitted}}} = \frac{a(t_0)}{a(t)} $$

| Symbol (符号) | Meaning (EN) | Meaning (CN) | Unit (单位) |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|------------|
| $z$ | Redshift | 红移 | dimensionless |
| $\lambda$ | Wavelength | 波长 | m |
| $a(t)$ | Scale factor at time $t$ | 时间$t$的尺度因子 | dimensionless |

**Derivation / 推导:**
The scale factor $a(t)$ describes how the universe expands. At emission, $a(t)$ is smaller; at observation, $a(t_0) = 1$. The wavelength stretches with the expansion.

**Conditions / 适用条件:**
- **English:** Valid for all redshifts in an expanding universe.
- **中文:** 适用于膨胀宇宙中的所有红移。

**Limitations / 局限性:**
- **English:** For very high redshifts ($z > 1$), relativistic effects become important.
- **中文:** 对于非常高的红移（$z > 1$），相对论效应变得重要。

# 8. Worked Examples / 典型例题

## Example 1: Age of the Universe / 宇宙的年龄

### Question / 题目
**English:**
The Hubble constant is measured to be $H_0 = 68 \text{ km s}^{-1} \text{ Mpc}^{-1}$. Calculate the approximate age of the universe in years. (1 Mpc = $3.086 \times 10^{19}$ km, 1 year = $3.156 \times 10^7$ s)

**中文:**
哈勃常数测量为 $H_0 = 68 \text{ km s}^{-1} \text{ Mpc}^{-1}$。计算宇宙的近似年龄（以年为单位）。（1 Mpc = $3.086 \times 10^{19}$ km，1年 = $3.156 \times 10^7$ s）

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Convert $H_0$ to s⁻¹ / 将 $H_0$ 转换为 s⁻¹**
$$ H_0 = \frac{68 \text{ km s}^{-1}}{1 \text{ Mpc}} = \frac{68}{3.086 \times 10^{19}} \text{ s}^{-1} = 2.20 \times 10^{-18} \text{ s}^{-1} $$

**Step 2: Calculate age in seconds / 计算以秒为单位的年龄**
$$ t_0 = \frac{1}{H_0} = \frac{1}{2.20 \times 10^{-18}} = 4.55 \times 10^{17} \text{ s} $$

**Step 3: Convert to years / 转换为年**
$$ t_0 = \frac{4.55 \times 10^{17}}{3.156 \times 10^7} = 1.44 \times 10^{10} \text{ years} $$

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** $1.44 \times 10^{10}$ years (approximately 14.4 billion years) | **答案：** $1.44 \times 10^{10}$ 年（约144亿年）

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** Always convert units carefully. Remember that 1 Mpc = $3.086 \times 10^{19}$ km.
- **中文:** 始终仔细转换单位。记住1 Mpc = $3.086 \times 10^{19}$ km。

## Example 2: Redshift and Scale Factor / 红移与尺度因子

### Question / 题目
**English:**
A galaxy is observed with a redshift $z = 2.0$. What was the scale factor of the universe when the light was emitted, compared to today?

**中文:**
观测到一个星系的红移 $z = 2.0$。与今天相比，光发射时宇宙的尺度因子是多少？

### Solution / 解答

**Step 1: Use the redshift-scale factor relation / 使用红移-尺度因子关系**
$$ 1 + z = \frac{a(t_0)}{a(t)} $$

**Step 2: Substitute $z = 2.0$ / 代入 $z = 2.0$**
$$ 1 + 2.0 = \frac{a(t_0)}{a(t)} = 3.0 $$

**Step 3: Solve for $a(t)$ / 解出 $a(t)$**
$$ a(t) = \frac{a(t_0)}{3.0} $$

Since $a(t_0) = 1$ (today's scale factor), $a(t) = 1/3$.

### Final Answer / 最终答案
**Answer:** The universe was 1/3 of its current size when the light was emitted. | **答案：** 光发射时宇宙的大小是当前大小的1/3。

### Quick Tip / 提示
- **English:** A redshift of $z = 2$ means the universe was 3 times smaller. The scale factor is $1/(1+z)$.
- **中文:** 红移 $z = 2$ 意味着宇宙小了3倍。尺度因子是 $1/(1+z)$。

# 10. Practical Skills Connections / 实验技能链接

**English:**
While the Big Bang theory itself is not directly testable in a school laboratory, several practical skills are relevant:

1. **Data Analysis:** Analyzing Hubble's law data (velocity vs. distance) to determine $H_0$ and the age of the universe. This involves graph plotting, calculating gradients, and estimating uncertainties.
2. **Spectroscopy:** Understanding how redshift is measured from spectral lines. This connects to [[The Doppler Effect]] and [[Stellar Distances]].
3. **Blackbody Radiation:** Analyzing the CMB spectrum to determine its temperature. This involves fitting a blackbody curve to data.
4. **Uncertainty Analysis:** Estimating the uncertainty in $H_0$ and how it propagates to the age calculation.

**中文:**
虽然大爆炸理论本身不能在学校的实验室中直接测试，但几个实验技能是相关的：

1. **数据分析：** 分析哈勃定律数据（速度与距离）以确定 $H_0$ 和宇宙年龄。这涉及绘制图表、计算斜率和估计不确定度。
2. **光谱学：** 理解如何从光谱线测量红移。这与[[多普勒效应]]和[[恒星距离]]相关。
3. **黑体辐射：** 分析CMB光谱以确定其温度。这涉及将黑体曲线拟合到数据。
4. **不确定度分析：** 估计 $H_0$ 的不确定度以及它如何传播到年龄计算。
